# Prince Thallan II Meets Allythoia

*The sound of rain outside as the heavens empty.*

**Thallan II:** I don't care what it takes, what expenditure of Will or toil of the Damned or our own Blood, make it happen. Every day she drifts closer to death, and we will not weather this foul storm with Her Holiness dead. In Her name, blessed remain the Blessed.

**Guard:** Let me summon the Damned, Holin–my prince. They will draw you a hot bath while your ministers work. She will be alright; she's fought so hard.

**Thallan II:** I envy you. The troubles on my shoulders…

**A Voice from the Shadows:** Can be relieved.

*The guard lays his hand on the pommel of his sword, but cannot summon the will to draw.*

**Guard:** What… my hand–it will not move!

**Thallan II:** Wait, my knight. Hers is an aura laden with powerful magic. Speak, stranger, but introduce yourself before the acting Divine Ruler of Navarre, Prince Thallan Læl'Nathor II.

**The Woman:** Blessed with Wisdom and Patience both, my prince. I am Allythoia, and I appreciate both virtues. I understand your Queen–your mother–is dead.

**Thallan II:** Rumors of her demise are greatly exaggerated. Our Holy Blood cannot die; ours is the avatar of Life itself.

**Allythoia:** Yet her soul has passed; lost to the Sea of Pneuma, to Siren as is popularly said.

**Guard:** You address His Holiness Thallan II; mind your tongue before I take it.

*Allythoia smirks. Thallan growls.*

**Thallan II:** Silence, knight. What this stranger does not know is the magnitude of the marvel she stands within. We have bound the perversions of this world, the horrors of Tarwa, the wild and pagan gods of lesser races. In Holy Order have they been turned, and with the energies thus enslaved and the intellect of the greatest True Elves ever at my command, even the God of Death cannot hold her. I have vessels, perfect simulacra in prime youth and pristine health, I have–

**Allythoia:** You have inert bodies wanting for soul.

*Thallan II's lips thin, but he does not deny her.*

**Allythoia:** You have all but one piece of the puzzle; the engine to your success, in fact, you have bragged about already. This very Tower… You were mistaken in actuality. I do know the magnitude of this marvel within which I stand. I do know what your greatest minds have been able to put together. Where we diverge… is that I do know the means to draw your mother's soul back from beyond the Sea.

**Guard:** Blasphemy! You are a necromancer, befouler of our ways!

**Thallan II:** SILENCE! The taint of Death pneuma does not stain her. Allythoia… I cannot fathom how you think you should know as much about this Tower, about my family's secrets, about magic the way you claim. But know this–you will be tested, and if you are found wanting…

**Allythoia:** I would never have come, my prince, were I not the solution the world requires.
