# Hooded Figure Meeting Goblins

**Hooded Figure:** "Greetings, Wise Ones, and know peace. I come with boons."

**Jag:** He knows our tongue, Aag. Hold, guards; let him speak.

**Aag:** (squinting) "Who are you to trespass our sacred grounds?"

**Hooded Figure:** "I am but a messenger, a guide for those who seek power. We've seen what approaches, Man with his Axe and Fire and Malice."

**Jag:** "What do you know of our struggles, stranger? Your kind has never shown us concern–nor your faces."

**Aag:** "You could not have entered our warrens unnoticed. What are you?"

**Hooded Figure:** "A friend. A friend that knows the balance has shifted against you. But don't worry, this friend brings a gift–salvation."

**Aag:** (perks up) "Salvation? What kind of trick is this?"

**Hooded Figure:** "No trick–but the total upset of your status quo. The ingredients, the rituals–it is an old art, forgotten by the mortal races. The secrets and the powers will be yours–a power to make your enemies tremble."

**Aag:** "And what power is this?"

**Jag:** "This is too lofty, Aag Gax. The shadows may be deep, but they are insubstantial things."

**Hooded Figure:** "The power to summon your God. The Stormbringer, the Shaker of Earth. With His might, you can drive the humans from your sacred lands, reclaim your hunting grounds. Rebuild what you have lost. His power can be yours. Dagozog, I believe, is His name."

**Jag:** "The storms are not… storms do not ravage with purpose. They bring the rain, and yes they may burn, but nature destroys with new creation in mind–not vengeance."

**Aag:** "It's time the rains fell on deserving heads, Jag. It's time we took back what we lost! Stow your piety and so-called wisdom. We'll only lose more and more of us if we do nothing.

**Jag:** "This way is madness, Aag; we don't know this man! This… whatever he is. Take up arms and we will all fall! Can you not see suicide disguised as resistance?"

**Aag:** (invoking pneuma) "Silence! Leave! If you have no courage, our tribe doesn't need you. You."

**Aag:** (addressing the hooded figure) "Speak now and give me this power. What do you want for this?"

**Hooded Figure:** "Your success is all I want, but it is no simple task, High Priest. But with breathstone channeling the very pneuma that surrounds you, you can awaken Dagozog's wrath. The lives… the lives it will require are already lost to the men marching on your people. Take them back, let their sacrifice be for the gain of goblinkind. I will teach you the rites, the secrets that have been hidden from your kind for too long."

**Aag:** "Sacrifice? My own people? Jag was right–you are mad."

**Hooded Figure:** (smirking) "The power of a God is not cheap; you will bear it with all justice, all wisdom. Make no mistake–these goblins will die, but this way, My way, their souls will know completion. They will give glorious fulfillment to the common purpose of your people."

**Aag:** "Very well…Ah, what is our new friend's name?

**Hooded Figure:** "Kyrichos."

**Aag:** "Very well, Kyrichos. Teach us these secrets. I will do whatever it takes to protect our land."
