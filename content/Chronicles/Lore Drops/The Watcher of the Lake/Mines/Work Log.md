# Work Log

*An official production log recovered from the mines beneath the [[Watcher of the Lake]]. The entries are administrative in tone — until they aren't. The author is never named, but the entries make clear she held a position of authority over the Tower's artisan corps, and that she knew exactly what [[Thalaniel Shamashur|Shamashur]] was.*

---

**Date: 7th Moon, 12th Sun**

**Today's Progress:** At last the order of 100 sets of enchanted plate is completed. The artisans have outdone themselves both in effort and result. Our focus ought to shift toward the trebuchets soon. Reinforcing their frames will be necessary to withstand the ravages of their breathstone ammunition.

**Challenges:** Shamashur has been incessant with his demands on our production time. Whatever the nature of his odd obsessions, it is becoming increasingly difficult to balance his requests with our primary responsibilities.

---

**Date: 7th Moon, 17th Sun**

**Today's Progress:** I cannot rid myself of this insufferable doctor. The frustrations he levies do not compare to the chill he brings to any room, but this opinion doesn't extend to His Holiness. Word comes from the palace to entertain the doctor's requests, so I have spent the majority of the day addressing Shamashur's device specs. So much for our production schedule; I have reserved a number of hands but too few.

**Challenges:** Dr. Shamashur's projects continue to divert our attention away from critical tasks. This balancing act I must perform is bound to end in disaster.

---

**Date: 7th Moon, 22nd Sun**

**Today's Progress:** Designs for the new enchantments on our blades and shields have advanced satisfactorily. Our artisans have made admirable sacrifice of time to catch up, however I must afford them a week in reserve to rest. These are no Damned to be worked into the dirt.

**Challenges:** Shamashur has crossed the line once more. The time and service of one of our own artisans, unnamed in task, with her to be removed from her housing indefinitely? I would take our artisans off work for two weeks' rest to spite him, if I did not need the trebuchets off my list.

---

**Date: 7th Moon, 28th Sun**

**Today's Progress:** *(no entry)*

**Challenges:** I have been in nothing but meetings today, an unending series of maneuverings, bargains, and compromise strangled by one word from the palace. His Holiness will have [[Alyndra]] reassigned, uprooted both from her work and family. Whatever the drive of this Doctor, I will not have a True Elf treated in such a fashion without knowing the cause. His Holiness is mad if he thinks this behavior acceptable.

---

**Date: 8th Moon, 5th Sun**

**Today's Progress:** The work orders have been left with my apprentice. The overseers know their tasks and my artisans are professionals. My own attentions have been elsewhere.

**Challenges:** I ought to have renamed this Shamashur far earlier. I have spent a good deal of time in the other levels of the Tower, getting closer to the research in the lab. Their new quarters, just below the royal wing, make this difficult, particularly after some of the words I've shared with the doctor. Through my proxies I discover he has been working on the lowborn for some time, since the days of Amastacia herself. The nature of these experiments we have not uncovered, but of those lowborn elves he has taken, none have left. Now [[Alyndra]]… When I have more, these logs will hear it. If she is harmed, the entire See will know of Shamashur's crimes.
