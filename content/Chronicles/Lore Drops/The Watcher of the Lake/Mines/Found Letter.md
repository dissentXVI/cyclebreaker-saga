# Found Letter

*A letter discovered among the debris of the mines beneath the [[Watcher of the Lake]]. It was never sent. The paper is brittle with age, the handwriting careful — the handwriting of someone who chose each word as though it might be the last they ever wrote.*

---

My Dearest Family,

I have forgotten the sun but I have not forgotten your faces. The depths of these mines with all their brimstone could never steal the scent of your hair, my love, nor the colorless darkness the sight of my beautiful sons. Know my arms sting to hug you again, my ears are hollow for want of your laughter. I know no warmth without your love.

I cannot help but feel a deep regret for the years that have slipped away from us, stolen by the desperation of our situation. These past years have been all pain, all toil, all a blur of seamless night and day beneath the callous splendor of the Tower. How many milestones have I missed in this prison our people have built?

I begin to fear that our reunion is destined not for this material plane but for the [[Sea of Pneuma]] beyond it. Will we truly only be together again when our souls are claimed? I will tread those waters, my darlings, until I find you again. You three are in my heart and mind always; every stone I uncover is lifted with the strength you give me. I pray you are safe; I pray you know you are loved. I pray — skies above, earth below, know that I pray.

With all my love,

*Aviel*
