# Lab Report

**Researcher:** Dr. [[Thalaniel Shamashur]]

---

**Objective**

File once more under the umbrella of solving the mystery of Death. Where the melody of life fades — the strings snap, the orchestra tires, song ends — I come in, the maestro of the encore. We revive the request given by our late Empress to exterminate Death through the application of various pneumas. From the earth below to the heavens above, I will ensure the melody of Life shall lift forever.

---

**Methodology**

1. **Selection of Subjects:** Subjects were selected from the lowborn populace, of sufficient elven heritage to prove useful, motivated by the possibility to participate in the next step of Navarresean greatness. When we are ready to move to Trueborn trials, I have my ideal subject selected.

2. **Application of Dark Magic:** By gracious allowance of His Holiness [[Thallan Lael'Nathor II]], no quarter is to be given to the question of reversing Death. To that end, artifacts and constructs of heathen make have been brought into the confines of this study.

3. **Breathstone Infusion:** Breathstone has been used to stabilize the flow of energies, constructed in a circuit of my own design. This mitigates the danger of the unholy pneuma to mine own person and other operators outside the field of the experiment.

4. **Observation and Documentation:** Vital signs, physical changes, and mental states of the subjects were closely monitored throughout the experiment. Any signs of success or adverse effects were meticulously recorded by assistants sourced from around the empire. Due to the sensitive nature of this program, secrecy was paramount and the assistants were relieved of their own lives after.

---

**Results**

The initial stages of experimentation showed promise, as some subjects exhibited delayed aging and heightened vitality. However, as the experiments progressed, it became evident that the pursuit of immortality through this method was flawed.

1. **Physical and Mental Deterioration:** Subjects who initially showed promising results eventually succumbed to severe physical and mental deterioration. Their extended lifespans were accompanied by a rapid decline in health and sanity leading to incredible suffering deemed less than optimal.

2. **Disastrous Unleashing of Pneuma:** The spells used to manipulate life and death pneuma caused unintended backfire, leading to widespread destruction in the laboratory. The uncontrollable release of pneuma resulted in numerous casualties, further hindering the progress of our work.

3. **Ethical Implications:** The treatment of the test subjects raised ethical concerns among some of the early research teams, which we were able to get around by careful selections of successor teams.

---

**Conclusion**

The pursuit of curing death and achieving immortality through unholy pneuma in conjunction with the holy is proving to be more difficult than imagined. I will require more subjects and time, to say nothing of guards for my level. The stirrings of reactionaries are already sounding, and while I have the solution for one, the distractions from my masterpiece are most annoying.
