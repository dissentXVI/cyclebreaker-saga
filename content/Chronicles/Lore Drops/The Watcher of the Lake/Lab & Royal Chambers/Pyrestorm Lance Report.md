# Pyrestorm Lance Report

**Lab Report: Pyrestorm Lance Testing and Development**
**By Dr. [[Thalaniel Shamashur]]**
*Department of Arcane Innovation, Navarresean Empire*

---

**Project Overview**

The Pyrestorm Lance has been an annoying but necessary distraction from my work, but be not fooled — it is also less than promising. This report will outline the testing and development phases of the Lance, particularly in its test against the armored abomination fashioned by the barbarians of the West. This weapon shows promise against the so-called 'dreadnought' construct, so long as they deign to stand still. Fire can be terribly boring as an element, and the base level design we took from the Dwarves imposes limits I am unfamiliar with. I have handed this matter to a trusted second. Find her notes below.

---

**Testing Phases and Results**

**Phase 1: Calibration Testing**

*Objective:* Test the fire breathstone's stability under continuous energy draw.

*Results:*
- At lower power levels, the beam projected steady heat and minor damage over 15 meters.
- At full power, the breathstone emitted unstable pneuma bursts, causing overheating in the focusing array and potential disastrous decommission if allowed to continue.
- No structural damage to the test target (a dwarven steel plate).

*Notes:* The inferior steel of the dwarves has proven surprisingly resistant to the initial calibration tests. It's perplexing that their crude forges can produce materials with such resilience — likely more accidental than deliberate.

*Conclusion:* Adjustments must be made to stabilize pneuma output and focus the laser to a finer point for greater penetration.

---

**Phase 2: Field Test Against Dwarven Dreadnought**

*Objective:* Assess the Lance's effectiveness in combat conditions against a decommissioned dwarven dreadnought.

*Results:*
- The Lance successfully scorched external plating, leaving minor surface deformation.
- Repeated strikes created heat stress but failed to breach the primary plating.
- Dreadnought remained fully operational after the test, deflecting additional energy bursts with its runic shield wards.

*Notes:* The dwarves' amalgamation of technology and magic is a disgrace to proper arcane discipline. The failure to breach the dreadnought's hull is less a fault of our weapon and more an indication of the dwarves' use of layered redundancy — an admittedly clever solution to their inferior materials.

*Conclusion:* Additional power amplification is necessary to bypass the dwarves' layered defenses.

---

**Phase 3: Breathstone Stability Under Stress Conditions**

*Objective:* Determine the fire breathstone's capacity to sustain prolonged and amplified pneuma extraction.

*Results:*
- After 6 minutes, the breathstone exhibited structural fracturing.
- Pneuma output became erratic, resulting in beam instability.
- Testing was halted when the breathstone core shattered, releasing uncontrolled energy and destroying the testing chamber and the mythril container used to house it.

*Notes:* The volatile nature of the fire breathstone is both its greatest strength and its most frustrating limitation. Yet, with proper containment protocols, the breathstone's potential far exceeds anything those stubby-fingered artificers could comprehend.

*Conclusion:* A reinforced containment array is required to stabilize the pneuma flow and prevent catastrophic failure during extended use.

---

**Recommendations and Next Steps**

- **Focusing Array Enhancements:** Develop a more refined lens system to concentrate the beam and improve penetration of layered defenses.
- **Amplification Runes:** Incorporate advanced Navarresean rune inscriptions to amplify the pneuma flow without compromising breathstone integrity.
- **Testing Against Dwarven Wards:** Conduct further studies on the dwarves' shield wards to uncover weaknesses.
- **Improved Cooling Systems:** Integrate a secondary cooling matrix to manage heat buildup during prolonged use.

---

**Dr. Shamashur's Personal Reflections**

The fetishization and mythologizing of weapons like the dreadnought are exactly the foibles we as a people must be prepared to excise. The primal elements of this world have bent to our Will; with the fundamental building blocks of Aspiria at our beck and call, it is only a matter of time before the rest of the world follows suit. We are the architects of a new reality; they are the clumsy mechanics of overthick hunks of metal.

Where the dwarves wield hammers and gears, we will command the elemental forces of pneuma itself. Their mountains will be leveled; their forges will crumble; and all that shall remain of their legacy is ash.

For the glory of Navarresean vision.
