# Empress Amastacia's Journal

*The contents of this journal have been slashed with blots of ink and, in some spaces, quite literally cut with the tip of a quill. Here and there legibility persists — small snippets of a woman's diary, detailing the story of a queen both powerful and ruthless.*

---

*...acceptable morris player, but his efforts are causing too much division in how we present ourselves to the Greater Court. War depletes us, ruling depletes us; the very act of continuing our greatness depletes us. Without doubt the war with Tarwa has cost too many of us over the years, and the spread of my own House, our own resources, is so thin as to invite one of our remaining 'allies' to take their own turn at the reins. Since Thallan will not listen to reas—*

---

*Rare are the snippets that survive, but those that do paint the picture of a determined woman playing the role of queen of a dying people in a dying body. Of interest is the apparent companionship she found in one of her maids — a tiefling girl she would play at chess while dressing for the day. The writing is done by hand; one can tell by the shakiness of the quillstrokes as time — and her disease — progresses.*

---

*At the end of the diary a number of pages have been shredded. The last page has a dark splotch of ink blossomed in the center, bled through to the cover behind it and the page before it.*
