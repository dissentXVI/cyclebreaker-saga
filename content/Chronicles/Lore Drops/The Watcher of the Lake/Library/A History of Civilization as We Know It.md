*A heavy tome — well-preserved for its age, its cover embossed with the six-pointed sigil of the Holy See. The title page reads: "A History of Civilization as We Know It: A True Elf's Accounting of Navarre. Penned by Heiroloctus Kor." The author's voice throughout is that of a true believer, dripping with the casual supremacy of a scholar who has never questioned the foundations beneath his feet.*
