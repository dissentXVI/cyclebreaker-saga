# Journal Entry of Alyndra, Mage of the Cosmic Pillar

Let the elf that finds this journal be one of open mind and greater Will than my own, for if it is found my efforts have failed. I serve as one of the Blessed in the Cosmic Pillar, but over these past few years I have seen the foundations of my status eroding. As we aim our eyes to the heavens I cannot help but notice upon whose efforts our leaders stand the most. [[Amastacia Lael'Nathor|Amastacia]] has always been a woman of intense focus but never blind to the plights of her people. Now whispers have reached my ears, tales of cruel experiments conducted by her pet.

[[Thalaniel Shamashur|Thalaniel Shamashur]], this so-called biomancer, this sadist with a thirst for knowledge unquenchable. May his name be cursed and the Holy Pneuma forsake his Will. What breakthroughs he once explored in the name of Navarre be undone — now I understand he experiments upon True Elves of Navarre. You read this correctly; unholy work has been visited upon our own heads. What use are our slaves then? Shall the Blessed take their turn in the mines next?

Several of our people have gone missing in the tower, and evidence points to Shamashur's labs as their fate. I myself have presented these accusations to Her Holiness. By the skies above and the earth below there was no mark of surprise in Amastacia's manner; there was even less concern. To what end I cannot say, but this work must serve the royal family in some fashion. I've sent my Damned to gather information as they work most invisibly in the Tower.

If I cannot return our sights to Life and Law, I fear any victory over Tarwa will be hollow.

---

*The journal ends there. [[Alyndra]] survived the fall of the [[Navarresean See|Navarresean Empire]] — barely. She has been suspended in a Life pneuma stasis within the Tower ever since, kept alive by Shamashur's machinery long after its original purpose became irrelevant. She does not know how long she has been there. She does not know the empire is gone.*
