# Caelum Aes'Thiir, An Elf of Mad Theory

Navarre sits in a time of unprecedented strife, locked into a mission by an elf claiming to be more god than mortal. How, one asks, could an elf of middling talents so quickly surpass the invention of as great a mind as [[Eron Asj'Norriah|Asj'Norriah]], and why does this ascension require this sacrifice of blood?

What's worse is the broad swath of my countrymen that would abandon reason to continue this war of aggression. Show me the dragon that would strike first and I will be the first to volunteer for the front. Show me the elf that would strike first, and I will show you madness.
