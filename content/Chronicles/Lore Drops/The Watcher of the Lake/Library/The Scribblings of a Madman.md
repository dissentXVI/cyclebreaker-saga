*A brief and tattered journal. At first glance it seems to fall under the same curse of scrambled gibberish as the corrupted tomes elsewhere in this library — but it quickly becomes apparent that the incoherence belongs to the writer, not the chaos of this place. The hand varies wildly in size and pressure. Whole lines are underlined multiple times. Some words have been written over themselves until they've become unreadable through repetition.*

*No author is identified. The journal was shelved here rather than discarded, which suggests someone in the library considered its contents worth preserving — or worth containing. The references to [[Amastacia Lael'Nathor|Amastacia]] and the heir suggest this was written toward the end of the empire's life, possibly within the tower itself. What the author witnessed — or thought they witnessed — is unclear. That they were never the same afterward is not.*
