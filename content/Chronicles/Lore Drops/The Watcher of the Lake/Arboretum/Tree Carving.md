*These words are etched into the bark of a tree in the Arboretum of the [[The Cosmic Pillar|Watcher of the Lake]]. The carving is deep and deliberate, the letters worn but legible — the work of someone who meant to be remembered.*

---

"The Empress served a people that spat on her service across a life marred by the corruptive forces of this world. I name in her stead the crawling vine of the north's princess's rest. May these waxy thorns choke the undeserving while my mother finds restoration above it all."

---

*No name is carved alongside the inscription. The sentiment, however, speaks plainly enough — this was the hand of [[Thallan Lael'Nathor II]], and the mother he speaks of was [[Amastacia Lael'Nathor|Empress Amastacia]].*
