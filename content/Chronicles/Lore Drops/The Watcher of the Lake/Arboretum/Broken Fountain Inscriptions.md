*Two inscriptions are engraved on the basin of a broken fountain in the Arboretum of the [[The Cosmic Pillar|Watcher of the Lake]]. The stonework is cracked, and water no longer flows, but the text remains legible.*

---

**Emperor Phaedra Yin'Balar**

*Dedicated to the Caelynn's Bloat Flower — Aubergine in bloom, venomous in nature, enduring in purpose.*

By imperial proclamation and by the grace of the Radiant Hand, this flower is honored among the treasures of the Arboretum. Its petals swell thick and aubergine with venom — a reminder that beauty and lethality are not at odds, but are the truest expression of sovereign strength. The blossom grows thick and aubergine with venom, but the Radiant Hand does not fear. So too does the Empire endure what lesser powers cannot stomach. Let those who tend this garden remember: to cultivate power is to accept its full nature.

---

**Emperor Thallan Lael'Nathor**

*Dedicated to the Springbreath Flower — Yellow-petaled, its pollen a green mist of healing.*

Among all the wonders cultivated within this living archive, I confess the springbreath flower has occupied my thoughts most often — not for its rarity, but for its generosity. In a letter penned to Duchess Ilyvatyrr, I wrote what I still believe to be true:

*"We might all stand to learn more from the lands that bear us. The bounties of Aspiria heal naturally, at least as well as any physicker with a decade's experience. I dream of the day when Navarre might serve as a meadow of springbreath, with her peoples the animals living freed from aches and dangers."*

That dream has not yet come. But it is tended here, in yellow and green, patient as the soil.
