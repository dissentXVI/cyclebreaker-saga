*Three pedestals stand among the plantings of the Arboretum. Each bears a plaque — some dedicated by emperors, one by a captain speaking in the name of one who could not.*

---

**Empress Eron Asj'Norriah**

*Dedicated to the Nostos Flower — a blossom that reads and reflects the emotions of those who stand near it.*

Among those who came before and after me, it was common practice to dedicate a flower of use: one that heals, one that poisons, one that fortifies. I have chosen instead this flower, which does none of these things. The nostos does not cure or kill. It simply sees. What it saw in me, and why that mattered, I leave to no record. Some dedications do not require an explanation.

*— Empress Eron Asj'Norriah*

---

*Note: Whatever significance the nostos held for her is lost with her writings. None of her personal journals survived to the present day.*

---

**Plaque of Captain Arcaena Zumrah, made in the stead of Emperor Caelum Aes'Thiir**

*Dedicated to the Titan's Reach — blue-petaled, fed to soldiers to grant them greater height, strength, and unyielding endurance.*

The Emperor Caelum Aes'Thiir departed this world before he could make his dedication. By his word to me in his final days, I, Captain Arcaena Zumrah, place this plaque in his honor and in his name. He wished to honor the flower that built the legions that built the Empire. An old rhyme, recovered from the barracks of the Second Age, captures what he would have said himself:

*By ones and threes, fives for sure /*
*Titan's blood shall e'er endure /*
*But hand in hand or twice more /*
*Titan's foes dashed to the floor*

He knew the Empire was its soldiers. He never forgot it.

*— Captain Arcaena Zumrah, in service to the memory of Emperor Caelum Aes'Thiir*

---

**Emperor Vael'Dorath**

*Dedicated to the Blue Caprise — a flower whose properties enhance and amplify the potency of poisons.*

It has been said that the victories of the First Celestial Campaign were won by blade and will alone. This is flattery, and I have no patience for it. The Blue Caprise filled the veins of our enemies' hesitation and turned it fatal. The wyrmslayer does not merely strike — the wyrmslayer ensures the strike is final.

Let those who tend this garden understand: beauty in service of destruction is not corruption. It is clarity.

*Wyrmslayer Vael'Dorath, His reach unimpugnable!*
