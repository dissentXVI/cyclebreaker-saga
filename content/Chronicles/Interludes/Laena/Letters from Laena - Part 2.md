# Letters from Laena — Part 2

*A letter home from Laconia. Laena describes her companions — Frog, Ariesa, Gothmog — and the adventures they've shared so far, including goblin diplomacy and a portable horse.*

---

Dearest Oseye and Monifa,

Please accept my deepest apologies for neglecting my correspondence to you both. Things have been….interesting to say the least. I am currently in Laconia and exploring two avenues for a cure for you Moni.

The cataclysm/ruins - we have found a horribly arrogant, disgraced university scholar who, despite his unpleasantness, seems confident he can get us into the ancient Navaresean heart of the temple. If there’s powerful magic anywhere, it’s there.

The local healers Helen and Bella have some unique equipment with which they make medication. They have agreed to travel with me to Tarwa’kem whenever I return.

Neither of these are foolproof, but I am trying, I swear it to you both.

I have allied with a group of fellow travelers/adventurers. We are all working towards slightly different, but hopefully aligned goals in the long run. We’re perhaps unlikely allies, but we work together well.

*Frog* - She is a middle aged woman who dresses and looks like a bog witch, but can track you down from miles away. She is at one with nature and fierce both in word and in deed. However, she has a soft spot for young children (although sometimes they fear her appearance). Frog has been the toughest pomegranate to crack, to be sure. I think she must be suffering from incidents in her past, but she has declined to give us much detail. We’re all trying to support her, but Gothmog and I have been quite focused on a makeover. I can’t wait to dress her up in Tarwakem. She’ll hate it, but maybe she’ll have a sliver of happiness when she sees how beautiful she really is!

*Ariesa* - She is a Tiefling sorcerer and the ‘most powerful one in the world’ to hear her tell it. But I think she’s out to prove herself more than anything else. You both remember the incident with Tsua’s grandfather and the trade agreement that fell through with that Kula. That was Areisa’s Kula! She is so caring, brave, smart, and generous (maybe overly so), that I wish others would get to know her before passing judgment. It has been difficult traveling with someone from a race that is so mistrusted, but it has opened my eyes to the many injustices they face.

Gothmog - Upon meeting Gothmog, a huge green,muscular orc with a sword, I immediately identified him as a barbarian brute, but I could not have been more wrong. Gothmog is intense, determined, loving to his partner Theo, and overall well rounded. He is a university scholar and is also quite passionate about fine food and fashions. He’ll be pleased with the outstanding food and wine in our fair city indeed. He worships the bloodwolf Grond, who seems very keen on oaths, and it has me worried what he would do if what our party needed came into conflict with said oaths.

There are many stories I could tell you, and many more I hope to share over pastries together,  like how we rescued two children in a cavernous underground system full of evil goblins and poisonous plants, or how we discovered a fallen Vociran flying ship, or how we have a ring that lets us speak with a long deceased dwarven king; But for now let me impress you with the most unique, magical item we’ve discovered so far. In exchange for our help defending their village of older, non combatant goblins, their goblin leader gifted us each a clear stone with black clouds that summons a magical mount at will. Mine is a horse named Amariah. Areisa has a lizard. Frog, a goat. And Gothmog, a wolf. A portable horse, can you imagine!? How we befriended goblins is yet another story for another time.

I’m sure you have not missed my references to Tarwakem. It seems I will be returning with the healers and my companions at some point in the near future. But only after we explore the Navaresean ruins for their secrets. I can not rightly express on parchment how excited I am to see you both again. I have missed you both, yes, even you Oseye. I pray you both stay safe and healthy in the meantime.

Love,

Laena
