 Interludes Index

Standalone stories, backstory vignettes, letters, and side-scenes from the world of the Cycle Breaker Saga. Organized by character/POV.

---

## Ariesa
- [[Ariesa and Garreth]] — Two children sneak away from a frustrating magic lesson and dream about the heroes they'll become.
- [[Ariesa Leaves - Pappy's Appeal]] — Pappy makes his case to the Patriarch for why Ariesa should be allowed to go — and come back.
- [[Ariesa Leaves - Part 2]] — A stubborn granddaughter and a grumpy old man say goodbye the only way they know how.
- [[Ariesa Runs Away]] — Ariesa drags a dead blight out of the forest to prove a point, nearly bleeds out doing it, and starts packing the moment everyone's back is turned.
- [[Inn and Out - Part 1]] — After being paid and shuffled out the back door, Ariesa cleans up at the creek — and returns to find a familiar face stepping out of a very expensive carriage.
- [[Inn and Out - Part 2]] — Over stewed rabbit and hard truths, Ariesa and Tsua Zuberi reckon with the past and take their first small swing at changing things.

## Frog
- [[A Frog is Born]] — Jewel Siltborn, assassin turned penitent, gives birth in a marsh town and makes the most painful decision of her life. She walks away with a new name and nothing else.
- [[Argenta's Travels - Part 1]] — Traveling with the Moonlit Revel toward the birth of her child, Argenta confesses who she used to be — and discovers that the past has sent someone to find her.
- [[Frog in Exile - Part 1]] — Frog wanders alone through the Frostmarch, half-hoping the wilderness finishes what guilt started — until a captive woman's eyes remind her there are still reasons to survive.
- [[Quiss de Barghest's Last Day]] — From a clifftop perch above a miners' camp, Quiss executes the job she was born for. Something in her stomach says otherwise.

## Gothmog
- [[Climb]] — Ochmaa takes her son to the mountains to prove something. Halfway up the ice wall, she cuts his rope and walks away.
- [[The King of the Wood]] — M'ylaena has a book, a blood circle, and a plan. The King of the Wood has different ideas entirely.
- [[Theo and Gothmog - Part 1]] — Magic lessons over tea, and the lie Gothmog tells his mother about where he's been spending his afternoons.

## Laena
- [[A New Era Begins with a Party]] — The coronation celebration of a lifetime — and Laena spends the best part of it crammed under a desk, eavesdropping on men who are already planning their next move.
- [[A New Era Begins with a Party - Part 2]] — The conversation under the desk gets worse before it gets better. Laena makes her way to Tytos's temple with a lot to think about.
- [[Guidance]] — Alone in the Hall of Order for the first time in too long, Laena sits with her regrets and lets Tytos show her what might still be possible.
- [[Letters from Laena - Part 1]] — The letters Laena leaves behind when she slips out of Tarwa'kem before anyone can stop her.
- [[Letters from Laena - Part 2]] — A letter home from Laconia: strange companions, stranger adventures, and a portable horse she is unreasonably excited about.
- [[Sultana's Coronation]] — The Zahir family swears loyalty to a new Sultana — who wastes no time announcing exactly what kind of ruler she intends to be.

## Major Allies
- [[A Letter - Departing Avosa]] — Theo writes to Gothmog the night before sailing for Shurevaire, and tries not to make it sound like a goodbye.
- [[A Story Begins]] — A young half-elf who reads too many adventure stories boards a boat for his first real expedition, under a professor who finds him deeply irritating.
- [[After Hours]] — The bar is closed and the books are out. Mireya finally asks M'ylaena the question she's been sitting on for weeks.
- [[It Belongs Out of a Museum]] — Theo and Ylaena walk into Shurevaire's most secure archive with forged papers and a plan that requires Theo to swallow a staff.
- [[Packages and Politics]] — Mireya tries to get a word of warmth past Zeyn's armor. He has somewhere to be, and it is not somewhere she would approve of.
- [[Post-Dagozog - Garreth and Theo in the Woods]] — Hunting goblins in the dark after the battle, Garreth stumbles onto a conversation he was never meant to hear.
- [[Speaking Up]] — Facing a circle of Kula elders who want a scapegoat, Garreth opens his mouth and says the wrong thing — the right way.
- [[Tavern Tales]] — Jendrick de-escalates a bar fight before it starts, Sylviane escalates it anyway, and later by the fire he burns a letter he was never going to send.
- [[Update Letter - Aqualonde]] — Mireya writes to the party with good news, quiet worries, and the kind of warmth that makes Laconia feel real even from a distance.

## Other
- [[A Hungering Darkness]] — An elven family's wagon breaks down on the road through the Fauconwood, and something in the trees begins picking them off one by one.
- [[A Persistent Shadow]] — A goblin priest watches his people suffer and waits for the robed figure at his shoulder to simply fix it. The robed figure explains, patiently and coldly, why he won't.

## Vocirans
- [[Advenientis Aestus]] — Ignia, Serena, and their father sail toward the eastern colonies with a mission that none of them are entirely sure they can win.
- [[Pre-Cataclysm - Vociran Perspective]] — A dying soldier remembers the long march into Halinorum, the man he loved, and the night everything went wrong.
- [[Strangers in a Strange Land]] — A Vociran strike team picks their way across the dried-out lakebed of the Mare Sticaea, and a small goblin with terrible Common tries very hard not to get stabbed while warning them of danger ahead.
