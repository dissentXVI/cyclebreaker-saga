# A Letter — Departing Avosa

*Theo's letter to Gothmog, written the night before he and Ylaena board the boat to Shurevaire. Melancholy, fond, and a little anxious.*

---

Dearest Gothmog, 

Ylaena and I are boarding the boat to Shurevaire tomorrow and I find myself with a certain air of melancholy that the sights and sounds of Avosa’s dockyard markets could not banish. It is not as if we have time to tarry, of course, but one does lament the missed opportunity–and missed companions. Everything is reminding me of you. Even Ylaena is calling to mind your sometimes single-minded drive; all she wants to do is talk about the plan, plan, plan. 

Things are moving quickly, I suppose. I don’t regret the purpose–we are saving lives, of course. I just had hoped we might have had more time in Laconia. It had been a number of weeks since Yenden, Ylaena and I had gone ahead of you, then came the issue of the goblins, the Paragons, these robed whisperers… Alas. This has turned into a more melancholic letter than I’d anticipated. I think only fondly of you and wish for your safe travels. Though we be miles apart, our hearts and purpose are together.

With all my love,

Theo
