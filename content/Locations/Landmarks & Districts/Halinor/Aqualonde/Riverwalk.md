**Location:** Along a feeder stream of the [[Reflecion Lagunes]], heart of [[Aqualonde]]
**Affiliation:** Civic district of [[Aqualonde]]

## Description

Winding beside a crystal-clear stream, Riverwalk is Aqualonde’s sun-dappled promenade of salons, shops, and public terraces. Pale boardwalks and arched footbridges—some laid in carved riverstone with moonstone lintels—curve between tree-grown façades and vine-draped balconies. Lanterns of glassseed and reedglass sway from living arches, casting warm patterns on the water and on murals etched with motifs of wind, light, and story. The air smells of tea steam, flax dyes, and cedar; soft chimes answer the breeze. At one end, the Zephyr Exchange lifts airy platforms above the current; at the other, the Loomhall filters sunlight through woven canopies—bookends of motion and meaning.
## Significance

Riverwalk is the city’s civic artery and social commons, where discourse mingles with commerce and art leans into policy. Guild envoys meet in open pavilions, apprentices display first works, and festival banners debut before they become symbols in council. Rumors travel here faster than hawks, and consensus often begins as a conversation over tea along the rails. Processions to [[Eirune’s Gap]], memorial rites, and treaty receptions all stage from Riverwalk—making it the place where [[Aqualonde]]’s public life takes its first, most graceful steps.
