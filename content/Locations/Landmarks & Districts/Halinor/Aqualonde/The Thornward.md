**Location:** Western outskirts of [[Aqualonde]], hidden among thorned groves and overgrown terraces
**Affiliation:** [[The Brambleblades]]

## Description

Tucked within a thicket of briar and moss, the Thornward is [[Aqualonde]]’s concealed stronghold and training ground. Stone paths are half-swallowed by green, opening into practice circles ringed with living briarwalls and arching trellises. Barracks are woven into the roots of towering trees, while half-buried armories keep the earth cool for fitting [[Breathfold armor]] and maintaining warded kit. Obstacle fields shift with the seasons—fallen logs in leaf-fall, slick stepping-stones in the rains—teaching warriors to move with the forest, not against it. The air smells of loam and oil; commands are given in gesture and breath, and even the dummies are grown of root and reed to keep sound low and splinters kind.
## Significance

The Thornward is the city’s quiet blade: where [[The Brambleblades|Brambleblades]] take their oaths, Moonblade captains run readiness drills, and new tactics are tested before they ever see war. It anchors [[Aqualonde]]’s defense network with hidden caches, rally stones, and messenger perches for Windrider hawks, and serves as a muster ground when the Council calls the city to alert. Entry is restricted to those marked by [[The Moonblade Forge|the Moonblades]] or trusted by the [[Maylin Lurel|Seedspeaker]]; within, glamours are muted and loud steel is frowned upon—discipline and discretion are the first lessons. In the current unease, night drills have doubled, and the path-stones themselves are said to rearrange to turn intruders back into the thorns.
