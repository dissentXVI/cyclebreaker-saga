**Location:** Just off [[Riverwalk]], built into the fork of a colossal cedar above a narrow stream
**Affiliation:** [[Aqualonde]] and travelers within

## Description

The Bitter Bark Tavern and Inn is [[Aqualonde]]’s largest—and most beloved—gathering place. Grown and carved into the split trunk of an ancient cedar, it sprawls across stacked platforms and curved balconies linked by spiral stairways and railings of living wood. Lanterns sway from the boughs, ivy threads the beams, and the air is warm with spiced rootwine, roasted chestnut, and cedar smoke. The main hall is a vaulted chamber anchored by a living hearth—an ever-burning knot of enchanted wood that glows with gentle green flame—while cozy guest rooms nestle higher in the trunk, their round windows looking onto the stream below. It is a place of comfort and kinship where laughter travels easily and debates find soft landings.
## Significance

Travelers bring rumors, caravans arrange escorts, and more than one policy has started as a toast by the green hearth. House rules keep the peace—no drawn steel, no overt guild feuds, and “the hearth hears nothing”—making the Bitter Bark a trusted venue for candid words and lasting accords.
