**Location:** Northern edge of [[Riverwalk]], [[Aqualonde]]
**Affiliation:** [[The Starcaller Salon]]

## Description

Salon Stellaire is the luminous seat of the Starcallers—a grand hall of performance, debate, and subversion carved into a living crescent of silverbark trees. Its curved roof is a lattice of interlaced branches enchanted to mimic a star-flecked sky, constellations drifting faintly even at midday. Within, the space is part theater, part forum, and part gallery: tiered benches grown from living wood, a central stage that reshapes to suit the piece, velvet drapery and moonlit chandeliers, and masked statuary that watches from shadowed alcoves. Sound carries cleanly beneath the boughs, turning whispers into instruments and silence into punctuation.
## Significance

This hall is [[Aqualonde]]’s cultural fulcrum, where narratives are tested before they become policy. Satire, mythic reenactment, and masked debate staged here routinely ripple into [[Riverwalk]] and up to [[Eirune’s Gap]], shaping public mood and council agendas alike. Historic productions trace back to Carlotte Cordray’s resistance era, and the tradition endures; current seasons—like The Twelvefold Reprise—probe faith, power, and the city’s place in a changing world. Few laws feel “real” in [[Aqualonde]] until they have survived Salon Stellaire’s laughter or tears.
