**Location:** Hidden vale near [[Eirune’s Gap]], [[Aqualonde]]
**Affiliation:** [[The Grovebound Circle]]

## Description

The Petal Hollow is [[The Grovebound Circle|the Grovebound]]’s spiritual sanctum—a serene glade where the boundary between forest and fae grows thin. Pale wildflowers carpet the earth, their colors and patterns shifting subtly with the land’s balance. At the center lies a shallow spring fed by [[breathstone]] veins, ringed with standing stones etched in elven script and fae sigils. Rituals here are performed in stillness, scent, and seasonal offering rather than speech. Time seems to slow: birds hush, wind softens, and those who enter often find truths rising unbidden. Paths are unmarked; only intuition and acceptance grant passage.
## Significance

Here [[The Grovebound Circle|the Grovebound]] interpret omens, renew ancient accords, and mediate harmony between Aqualonde and the unseen. The Hollow’s bloom patterns serve as quiet auguries for the Council; misaligned petals or dimming springlight are read as warnings. Oaths sworn at the stone ring are considered binding across courts mortal and fae, and many civic rites begin with a silent vigil at the water’s edge. In the present crisis—the fae gone silent, disturbances from [[Chilmire]] to [[Serinvale]]—the Petal Hollow has become both listening post and litmus for the city’s well-being, its changing flowers a public rumor and a private reckoning.
