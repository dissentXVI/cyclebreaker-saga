**Location:** Eastern boughs of [[Aqualonde]], beneath a natural break in [[the Fauconwood]] canopy
**Affiliation:** [[The Starcaller Salon]] (public venue; [[The Tree Shapers Guild|Tree Shapers]] oversight)

## Description

Starfall Glade is Aqualonde’s sacred stage—a natural amphitheater cradled in a crescent-shaped clearing where the canopy parts to reveal open sky. This is the cherished domain of [[the Starcaller Salon]], where stories are not merely told but woven into memory. An earthen stage lies at the center, encircled by smooth, petal-shaped stone benches grown from the roots of ancient trees. At dusk, enchanted lanterns hover like fireflies, and the acoustics—shaped by wood and rock—carry even a whisper to the farthest listener. From operas to masked debates, mythic reenactments to biting satire, the Glade turns performance into policy… and silence into a verdict. It is said the Glade remembers every tale ever told beneath its stars—and that sometimes, the stars listen back.
## Significance

Premieres at Starfall Glade often set the city’s mood before council sessions, with themes and refrains echoing along Riverwalk and up to [[Eirune’s Gap]]. Cordray-era resistance pieces first sparked here, and modern seasons use the Glade for works too intimate—or incendiary—for [[Salon Stellaire]]’s hall. Civic readings, memorial nights, and festival oratories bind community across guild lines, while superstition holds that the lanterns dim when [[the fae]] are displeased. In an age of omens and unease, the Glade remains [[Aqualonde]]’s most trusted barometer of public conscience.
