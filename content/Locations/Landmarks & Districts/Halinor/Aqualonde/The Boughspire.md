**Location:** Western canopy rise of [[Aqualonde]], spiraling above the treeline
**Affiliation:** [[The Tree Shapers Guild]]

## Description

The Boughspire is a living tower grown over generations from a single colossal ash. Spiraling terraces wrap the trunk like rings of time, linked by root-bent ramps and vine-braided railings. Open platforms host seed archives, grafting chambers, and sapling sanctuaries, while hanging gardens spill bloom and moss over the walks. [[Breathstone]] crystals set deep in the wood give off a soft, sap-blue glow at dusk, and tiny bonsai alcoves line the galleries—some trees shaped across centuries like heirlooms in slow motion. The air is cool and resin-sweet; everywhere, the structure hums with the quiet patience of things that choose to grow rather than be built.

## Significance

Guildhall and monument in one, the Boughspire is where [[Aqualonde]]’s living architecture is planned, tended, and taught. Here the Tree Shapers issue growth charters, consult on bridges and homes, maintain seed-vaults and pattern groves, and preserve the guild’s bonsai lineage—the city’s botanical memory. Under [[Verdane Lysurel]], shapers also reinforce deep rootwork that stabilizes civic sites such as [[Eirune’s Gap]] and[[ Riverwalk]] approaches, answering disturbances in the breathflow before they become crises. Apprentices (Sapling Hands) earn their cords on these terraces, learning the Arborium Rite: not to carve a city from a forest, but to persuade a forest into a city—carefully, and for centuries.
