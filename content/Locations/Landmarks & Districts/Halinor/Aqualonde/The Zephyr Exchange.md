**Location:** Southeastern rise above [[Riverwalk]], [[Aqualonde]] (perched along a high canopy ridge)
**Affiliation:** [[The Windriders Guild]]

## Description

The Zephyr Exchange is [[The Windriders Guild|the Windriders]]’ elevated headquarters—an aerie of high platforms and sweeping terraces that drink the ever-shifting winds from [[the Fauconwood]]’s eastern rise. Suspended bridges stitch together slender towers of sky-colored timber set on riverstone footings, while sail-like awnings billow between spires. Glass windows and latticework glow faintly during storms; wind-etched markers and chimes whisper direction and speed along the walkways. Aerial message lines run from tower to tower, weathervanes track not only weather but pressure fronts of unrest, and hawk aeries crown the upper decks. The place is never sealed: doors are slatted, halls are open, and the whole complex breathes like a lung.
## Significance

Part guildhall, part relay center, the Exchange is where routes are charted, convoys mustered, and treaties drafted to keep Aqualonde’s lifelines open. Couriers take dispatches from the central rostrum while Aerie Wardens launch trained hawks on long-range relays; the Skybound Council reads stormpaths and political “weather” before advising [[Eirune’s Gap]]. When tensions rise, signal lines flare to [[The Moonblade Forge|the Moonblades]] and [[The Brambleblades|Brambleblade]]; when festivals bloom, banners unfurl here first to catch the wind. In all seasons, the Exchange affirms a Windrider creed: a sealed room is a sealed mind.
