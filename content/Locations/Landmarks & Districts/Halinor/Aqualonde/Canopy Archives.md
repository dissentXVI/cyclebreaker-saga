**Location:** Upper bough terraces above [[Riverwalk]], [[Aqualonde]]
**Affiliation:** [[Aqualonde]]

## Description

Built as a ring of terraced stone galleries anchored into [[The Elder Tree]]’s outer boughs, the Canopy Archives blends carved riverstone and pale moonstone with living roots. Buttressed arcades and leaf-relief columns support arched bridges between stacks, while glass windows and leaf-paned openings keep the air cool and resin-scented. Vines and rootlets thread through stone lintels to cradle codices, moss panels, and glassed pods that hum with faint recorded tones. [[Breathstone]] capillaries are set into the floors to stabilize the scripts, and subtle runes are chiseled along cornices to deter flame and damp. Cutting tools and open fire are forbidden at the entry.
## Significance

As [[Aqualonde]]’s civic memory, the Archives houses council tallies woven as sashes, treaties pressed into treaties, performance scores and [[The Sunweavers Guild|the Sunweavers]]’ pattern canons. The stonework skeleton serves both as a firebreak and a long-span support for the heavy, living stacks—symbolically grounding memory that might otherwise drift. Scholars and envoys consult its records before debates at [[Eirune’s Gap]], making the site a quiet fulcrum of consensus.
