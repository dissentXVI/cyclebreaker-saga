**Location:** Southern bend of [[Riverwalk]], descending from [[the Fauconwood]] uplands into a feeder of the [[Reflecion Lagunes]]
**Affiliation:** Public landmark closely tied to [[The Sunweavers Guild|the Sunweavers Guild]] ([[The Loomhall]] terraces overlook the cataract)

## Description

Lightreach Falls plunges in a long, silken sheet from a notch in the canopy cliffs, its waters fanning over terraced bowls of pale riverstone and root-grown ledges. Behind the curtain, breathstone veins catch the sun and split it into prismatic bands that rake across the spray—bright “threads” of pink, gold, and blue that drift over the Loomhall’s open floors. Mist keeps the air cool and flax-sweet; reedglass lanterns and carved steps trace safe paths to narrow balconies where the roar softens to a steady loom-like hum. In late afternoon, when the wind turns, the entire cascade glows amber, as if the falls themselves were weaving daylight into the city.
## Significance

More than a view, Lightreach is Aqualonde’s metronome and omen. [[The Sunweavers Guild|The Sunweavers]] time dye baths and warping by its rhythms, channeling rivulets to rinse river-silk and to humidify [[The Loomhall]]’s working looms. Civic rites—oath-sashes, memorial hems, and first-apprentice blessings—often take place on the spray decks when the prismatic “threads” align, a rare event called the Sunveil, believed to sanctify any pattern begun beneath it. Festivals and council processions stage from [[Riverwalk]] below the falls.
