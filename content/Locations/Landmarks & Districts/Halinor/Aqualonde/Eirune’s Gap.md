**Location:** High canopy clearing above [[Aqualonde]]
**Affiliation:** [[The Council of Guilds|Council of Guilds]] (wards tended by [[the Grovebound Circle]]; platforms maintained by [[The Tree Shapers Guild|the Tree Shapers]])

## Description

Eirune’s Gap is a natural opening in the forest canopy where the woven boughs part in a perfect circle, letting gold-warm daylight and open sky pour through. Suspended among massive branches and ancient platforms grown directly into the trees, the site is open to wind and stars. Along the outer ring, seven carved seats—each shaped to reflect a guild’s character—stand in symbolic equality. The place holds an attentive hush; even the leaves seem to pause for words worth hearing.
## Significance

This is the deliberative heart of [[Aqualonde]]. Here the seven seats convene under a rotating Convener of the Gap (currently [[Maylin Lurel]]), seeking consensus first; when accord fails, a binding measure requires five of seven votes with quorum present. Scribes of Record keep the tally and notes as the guild leaders deliberate. In the present crisis—[[fae]] silence and Vociran uncertainties, the Council meets here in increasingly frequent closed sessions, weighing whether Aqualonde will bend like branch or brace like root.
