**Location:** Southern bend of [[Riverwalk]], overlooking [[Lightreach Falls]]
**Affiliation:** [[The Sunweavers Guild]]

## Description

The Loomhall is the revered seat of [[The Sunweavers Guild|the Sunweavers]] where thread becomes language and fabric becomes legacy. Built around the cascade of [[Lightreach Falls]], its arched galleries and open-air work floors are shaded by sun-filtering canopies of woven flax. Tall looms stand like instruments; when the wind moves through, their strings hum softly and the air smells of dye, river-silk, and warm wood. Sunlight refracts off the falls to drift across polished planks, embroidered murals, and ritual drapery, so patterns seem to move as the day turns. Part atelier, part sanctum, the Hall holds both quiet industry and ceremonial gravity—every stitch guided by tradition.
## Significance

From the Loomhall, [[The Sunweavers Guild|the Sunweavers]] codify [[Aqualonde]]’s civic motifs and rites: oath-sashes, council mantles, funerary hems, and the history of Aspiria itself. Commissions for the Council and the Seedspeaker begin here, where [[Mithrilweave]]—exceptionally strong yet remarkably lightweight—is cut for ceremonial regalia and durable uniforms. The Pattern-Lore Keepers maintain archives of encoded threads used in diplomacy, and apprentices are tested on “speaking through cloth” before they earn their marks.
