###### Type and Location
Ancient living woodland • Eastern Halinor

## Overview

The Fauconwood is a sentient, ever-shifting forest whose breathflow nourishes the lands around it. Paths open for the welcomed and turn aside the careless; music and light travel farther here than iron and flame. At its heart stands [[The Elder Tree]] and the city grown in harmony around it, a culture that treats negotiation, art, and nature as the same conversation.

## Settlements & Notable Sites

[[Aqualonde]] — Forest-integrated city built around the Elder Tree, where salons and guilds govern by consensus.

[[Serinvale]] — Picturesque retreat-town founded by a splinter group of Carinthian elves, now rebuilding its elegant forest architecture after recent ruin. Sits at the base of the [[Chilneau Peaks]].

[[Elesatha]] — Gothic-tinged elven port famed for its Alchemy Guild and the annual Festival of Elixirs.

[[Reflecion Lagunes]] — Broad, mirror-clear lake beside Aqualonde that feeds Riverwalk's stream and anchors local trade and ritual.

[[Riviera Scintillante]] — Glittering trade river running from the [[Chilneau Peaks]] to the southern [[Halinor]] coast, a key Windrider corridor.

## Climate & Ecology

Temperate with long spring rains, warm summers, and mist-laced mornings; dusk leaf-gold is read as an omen. [[Pneuma]] leylines and [[fae]] presence seem to influence irregular growth and rare bioluminescence. Flora can be "persuaded" by rite ([[The Tree Shapers Guild|Tree Shapers]]), and fauna avoid loud flame and raw iron near sacred sites.

## Native Inhabitants

[[Fae]] courts and liminal spirits (dryads, sprites, will-o'-wisps), all the animals to make up a healthy ecosystem. Mortal communities include Aqualondians across districts and glades; lizardfolk from the [[Chilmire Bog]] occasionally range along the western margins as seasonal traders.

## Narrative Relevance

[[The fae]] have fallen silent, path-songs drift off-key, and bloom patterns misalign from [[The Petal Hollow]] to [[Serinvale]]. [[The Earthspeakers Circle|Earthspeakers]] report disturbances near the [[Chilmire Bog]]; Moonblades have doubled [[The Thornward|Thornward]] drills. What the Fauconwood withholds—or reveals—will steer [[Aqualonde]]'s next moves at [[Eirune's Gap]].
