**Location:** [[The Fauconwood]], base of the [[Chilneau Peaks]], eastern [[Halinor]]
**Affiliation:** [[Aqualonde]] (loosely; self-governing)

## Origin and Brief History

Serinvale was founded by a splinter group of the Carinthian elves who first migrated south during a period of upheaval in the north. While most of those early migrants eventually integrated into what became [[Aqualonde]], a small number refused — unwilling to surrender the social hierarchies and elevated status that Aqualonde's egalitarian philosophy would not accommodate. They broke away and settled deeper into the Fauconwood, founding Serinvale as a retreat for the prosperous: close enough to Aqualonde for trade and communication, far enough to live on their own terms.

The town they built reflects those origins. For generations it served as a refuge for the wealthy and influential of the region, and its reputation for opulence attracted more of the same. The Trembling Foundations damaged it significantly. Several founding families were lost. The **Valpeiros family** — once among Serinvale's most prominent — was nearly wiped out; their estate stands largely shuttered, tended by the sole surviving member. Serinvale is currently rebuilding, slowly and with limited resources, while the spreading signs of disturbance in the surrounding Fauconwood complicate recovery.

## Description

Serinvale's architecture blends Aqualondian elegance with a Carinthian love of deliberate beauty: living treehouses, woven bridges, and gazebos integrated into the canopy. Crystal-clear streams fed by springs from the [[Chilneau Peaks]] run through the town. It remains visually striking even in its diminished state, though scaffolding and unrepaired structures are common sights.

The town is self-governed by a council of elders. Its proximity to the [[Chilneau Peaks]] and the [[Chilmire Bog]] to the west places it near the regions where fae disturbances have been most acutely felt.

## Notable People

**Thalindra Portier** — The town's elder and council lead. An old elf whose knowledge of the Fauconwood predates most of Serinvale's current structures.

**Sorsana Valpeiros** — Last surviving member of the Valpeiros family. Rarely seen outside her family estate since the cataclysm.
