**Location:** Eastern shore of the [[Sticaean Abyss]], [[Halinor]]
**Affiliation:** Independent
**Status:** Abandoned; corrupted

## Origin and Brief History

Birview Lumber Mill was established after the Trembling Foundations by Eadric Birview, a lumberjack who arrived in the region during its recovery period and recognized both the abundant timber in the surrounding forest and the desperate need of nearby communities for building material. His reputation for providing free materials and labor to communities struck hardest by the cataclysm spread quickly, drawing skilled workers and people in need alike to the settlement.

The mill operated as a quiet haven of generosity until the rot came. [[Thessakor, the Rot that Hungers]], a death [[Paragons|Paragon]] that overtook the [[Chilmire Bog]], extended its corruption outward through the forest and consumed Birview entirely. The settlement is now abandoned — taken not by violence but by decay.

## Description

Birview no longer resembles the warm, purposeful place it was. The silence is the first thing noticed: no birds, no voices, no movement. Then the smell — sweet rot, thick and syrupy, clinging to everything. The buildings sag beneath their own warped weight, timbers blackened with moisture. Enormous mill gears lie choked by moss and mold, vines strung like tendons across walkways that were once tended with care. The flowers Eadric kept are gone — only shriveled stalks remain. Water laps at the base of structures where there should be none. A low, constant croaking echoes through the trees, never near, never far, resonating in the chest.

Birview is not dead in the way of abandoned places. It breathes — but not with life.
