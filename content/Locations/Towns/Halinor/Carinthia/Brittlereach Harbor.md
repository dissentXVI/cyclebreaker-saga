**Location:** Northern coastline of [[Halinor]], facing the Frozen Maw
**Affiliation:** [[Halinor]] (frontier, Carinthian-adjacent)

## Origin and Brief History

Brittlereach Harbor was built on the logic of the sea route. A band of enterprising fishermen established a modest harbor on the northern coast, and over generations it grew into a prosperous maritime hub connecting [[Halinor]]'s mainland to Carinthian ports via the Frozen Maw. Ships laden with trade goods made the crossing regularly, and the town grew the infrastructure to support them: drydocks, warehouses, chandleries, and the **Brittle Beacon** lighthouse.

The decline came swiftly when the Carinthian port on the far side of the Frozen Maw was destroyed by the dragons. Without a destination, most sailors and traders departed within a season or two. What remained was a skeleton community in an oversized harbor.

## Description

Brittlereach today carries the particular melancholy of a place built for more people than it holds. Buildings that once housed merchants and sailors stand shuttered or in disrepair. Fishing boats sit idle, their numbers much reduced. The small tavern — the **Frosty Mug** — remains open, though the crowd is thin.

The **Brittle Beacon** still burns each night, lit out of habit as much as hope. It remains one of the more reliable navigational landmarks on Halinor's northern coast.

## Notable People

**Captain Jean-Baptiste** — Last captain of the *Frostveil*, once Brittlereach's proudest trading vessel. He clings to hope that Carinthia will reopen its ports so he can sail the Frozen Maw again.

**Zoe Dupont** — Former shipwright who now maintains the idle fishing boats in harbor. Dreams of restoring Brittlereach to its former standing; met with skepticism by most remaining residents.
