**Location:** Southern edge of Carinthian territory, northern [[Halinor]]
**Affiliation:** [[Halinor]] (frontier, loosely Carinthian-adjacent)

## Origin and Brief History

Quickstall was founded by pioneers who recognized the value of a final stop before the northern crossing. Positioned just south of the snowbound forest that separates [[Halinor]]'s interior from the outer edge of Carinthian territory, it grew into a modest but reliable waystation — the last warm hearth for travelers heading into the cold, and the first for those descending from it. Its name comes from the rapid halt most made here before committing to the northern road.

Quickstall once handled significant commercial traffic. As Carinthia's isolation has deepened over the years, that traffic has thinned, and the town has contracted back toward its humble origins. It now sustains itself on the steady if modest flow of traders who still run the northern routes and the guides who walk them through the forest safely.

## Description

The town is characterized by sturdy log construction: closely-clustered cabins with steep snow-shedding roofs and stone chimneys smoking through most of the year. A central marketplace encircled by wooden stalls and lit by lanterns in the evenings serves as the community hub. The **Frostwarden Lodge** is its most prominent building — a well-regarded inn among traders and guides who know the northern routes.

Recent disappearances along the Carinthian approach have made reliable guides scarcer and more expensive. The Lodge's proprietors tend to be among the best-informed people in the region about current conditions in the forest and which routes are passable.

## Notable People

**Elara Stormchaser** — A rugged tracker and guide who navigates the Carinthian wilderness for paying travelers. Highly sought after since the disappearances began.
