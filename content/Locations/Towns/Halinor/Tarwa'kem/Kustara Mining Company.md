**Location:** Southern steppes near [[Tarwa'kem]], [[Halinor]]
**Affiliation:** [[Kustara Consortium]] ([[Ngor Azizi]])

## Origin and Brief History

The flagship enterprise of the [[Kustara Consortium]], the Kustara Mining Company was established decades ago in the mineral-rich steppes south of [[Tarwa'kem]] and has grown from a regional operation into the dominant force in Halinor's mining industry. The Trembling Foundations barely interrupted production. If anything, the cataclysm accelerated the workforce's expansion: D'yadyan refugees displaced by the Vociran occupation flooded into the region, and the company absorbed them by the hundreds. Labor costs are low; worker leverage is lower. Discontent is a permanent feature of the settlement's social climate.

## Description

The settlement is utilitarian to its bones — rows of worker housing, warehouses, processing facilities, and administrative buildings arranged for function. The defining features of the landscape are the mines themselves: open-pit excavations of staggering scale, and blasted tunnel networks descending deep into the rock.

> **GM Note:** Chief of security Yasmine Shadid is investigating a pattern of unexplained accidents and disappearances in the deeper shafts. What she is finding does not match any mundane explanation.

## Notable People

**[[Ngor Azizi]]** — Owner of the Kustara Consortium.

**Raffy Al Sadat** — Chief overseer of mining operations. Renowned for maximizing yields while minimizing costs.

**Yasmine Shadid** — Chief of security. Former soldier; lost her arm saving a laborer in a cave-in during the Trembling Foundations. Currently investigating unexplained incidents in the deep shafts.
