**Location:** Forest fringe near the [[Sticaean Abyss]], [[Halinor]]
**Affiliation:** [[Kustara Consortium]] ([[Ngor Azizi]])

## Origin and Brief History

The Kustara Logging Company was founded by [[Ngor Azizi]] as a diversification of the [[Kustara Consortium]]'s mining interests into the timber industry. It occupies the lush forest edge that once bordered Sticaean Lake — land made accessible and less contested by the cataclysm's disruption of prior settlement patterns. Much of its labor force consists of D'yadyan refugees who came to Halinor's interior following the Vociran occupation. Environmental opposition to the operation emerged early but was absorbed through Azizi's economic and political leverage.

## Description

The settlement is spare and functional: administrative buildings, warehouses, and worker housing arranged for efficiency. The forest around it bears the evidence of sustained harvesting — wide cleared zones, drag-roads cut through the undergrowth, the quiet that follows when old growth has been removed.

> **GM Note:** The Logging Company and [[Birview Lumber Mill]] operate in proximity with very different philosophies. The contrast can frame moral questions about labor, land use, and who benefits from reconstruction.

## Notable People

**[[Ngor Azizi]]** — Owner of the Kustara Consortium. Rarely present; manages by proxy.

**Anka Safar** — Chief forester overseeing day-to-day logging operations. Pragmatic and results-driven.
