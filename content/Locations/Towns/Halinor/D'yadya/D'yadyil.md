**Location:** Northern [[Halinor]], near the D'yadyan border
**Affiliation:** D'yadyan resistance (informal)

## Origin and Brief History

D'yadyil was founded by a handful of refugees who escaped the Vociran occupation of [[D'yadya]] and refused to abandon the claim to their homeland. It emerged as a sanctuary for those who still believed in a liberated D'yadya, and it has sustained that purpose ever since — small, diminishing in numbers, but unbroken in intent.

## Description

The settlement is unassuming by design. Homes are simple construction — makeshift in places, repaired repeatedly, built for function and concealment rather than permanence. A communal gathering space at the center doubles as a planning room, meeting hall, and place to mourn. The people here are few. For every refugee who finds D'yadyil and joins the cause, others pass through and continue on to the relative stability of [[Tarwa'kem]]'s [[Misrata]] district or the labor settlements of the [[Kustara Mining Company]].

The resistance operates modestly: gathering intelligence on Vociran movements, maintaining escape routes for D'yadyans who want to leave occupied territory, and disrupting supply lines when the opportunity is clean and the risk acceptable.

> **GM Note:** Scout Nahja al-Ralori is the sister of [[Gia Ralore]]. Her D'yadyan name is Gehja al-Ralori. The players have not established this connection yet.

## Notable People

**Kameel al-Mansur** — Charismatic resistance leader. Provides direction and inspiration for the settlement's cause.

**Nahja al-Ralori** — Skilled scout and tracker. Invaluable to the resistance for intelligence gathering and knowledge of the terrain.

**Shalika Essa** — Healer from [[Tarwa'kem]] who came to D'yadyil specifically to serve those who had nowhere else to go.
