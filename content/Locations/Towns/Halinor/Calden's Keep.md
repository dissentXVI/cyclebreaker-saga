**Location:** [[Renault Hills]], central [[Halinor]]
**Affiliation:** Independent (mercenary community)

## Origin and Brief History

Calden's Keep was constructed during the wars of the Age of [[Louis'roi Arch de Soleil]], when [[Heldenheim|Carinthia]]'s expansionist conflict brought destruction to surrounding regions of Halinor and people needed somewhere defensible to shelter. When the conflict ended, the keep did not empty — the mercenaries who had used it as a staging point found it too well-built and well-placed to abandon. A community formed organically around the fortification, drawing veterans, soldiers between contracts, and fighters from across Halinor who preferred garrison structure to civilian uncertainty. It has operated as an independent mercenary settlement ever since.

## Description

The keep is imposing: quarried-stone walls, tall watchtowers with clear sight lines across the Renault Hills, and an iron-bound gate. Inside, the space is organized around the realities of a working military community — barracks, training ground, mess hall, and residences for those who have brought families. Weapons and armor are displayed openly and worn casually.

The keep takes contracts but chooses them carefully. It has turned down offers — reportedly including from the Vociran Empire — that came with strings incompatible with its independence. That reputation for selective loyalty is itself part of what makes it valuable to those who hire it.

## Notable People

**Captain Olivia Delmalis** — Commander of the keep. Battle-hardened, tactically sharp, and fiercely protective of the settlement's independence.

**Ajmi** — The keep's healer. Skilled in both medicine and ancient herbal remedies; known for quiet wisdom.
