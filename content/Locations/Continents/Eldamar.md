## Summary
Eldamar is the eastern continent — a land of extremes, from the frigid tundra of its northern reaches to the open windswept steppes of its center and the temperate bamboo forests of its south. It is home to the [[Vociterra|Vociran Empire]], the most technologically advanced civilization in the known world, as well as the orcish warrior cultures of [[Tatarr'kha]], the occupied island nation of [[Ilsong]], and the subtropical forests of [[Gholmeshi]]. Where [[Halinor]] is a continent shaped by magic and religion, Eldamar is defined by industry, discipline, and the tension between empire and the peoples it seeks to absorb.

## Geography & Climate
- **General Terrain**: Cold mountain ranges in the north, open grassland steppes in the center, temperate bamboo forests and plains in the south, scattered island chains off the western coast
- **Climate Range**: Frigid tundra (north) to open windswept plains (center) to temperate forest (south); the eastern shore is dry and prone to extreme lightning storms
- **Notable Features**: The [[Branded Shore]] — an eastern coastal region scarred by perpetual lightning storms of unknown origin; the vast bamboo forests of [[Gholmeshi]]; the cold highlands that house Vociran infrastructure and military installations; the open steppes of Tatarr'kha, home to orcish tribal clans

## Regions & City-States
- **[[Vociterra]]** – Frigid northern Eldamar; industrial empire built on vulcaneum technology; capital Patria Noster
- **[[Tatarr'kha]]** – Central Eldamar; open windswept steppes; home to the orcish warrior clans
- **[[Ilsong]]** – Island off the western coast; temperate; capital Shoran — currently under Vociran occupation
- **[[Gholmeshi]]** – Southern Eldamar; temperate bamboo forests; capital Kazhar
- **[[The Branded Shore]]** – Eastern coastal region; dry, storm-ravaged, and sparsely inhabited

## Lore & Narrative Role
Eldamar is where the Vociran Empire was born and where it continues to grow. Ilsong has already fallen — Shoran was taken after the failed uprising of the Ilsong Liberation Front, and the nation now operates under Vociran authority. Gholmeshi has so far avoided direct occupation, its status relative to the empire an open and uneasy question. Like all continents, Eldamar has weathered the Cataclysms that have periodically reshaped the world — the specific toll on each of its regions is its own history, distinct from what was recorded in Halinor. The Branded Shore remains anomalous and poorly understood.
