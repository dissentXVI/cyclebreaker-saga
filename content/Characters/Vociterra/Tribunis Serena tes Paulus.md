**Title**: Tribunis Tertiari of the First Legion

**Allegiance**: [[Vociterra]]

**Race**: Human (Vociran)

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Early 40s

**Eyes**: Dark brown, almost black

**Hair**: Shaved bald

**Attire**: Reinforced lightweight armor with silvered plating; patterned face mask and silk-wrapped shoulders; twin gladii across her back

**Home**: Patria Noster

## Appearance

Serena's presence is defined by discipline.

She wears modified Vociran infiltration armor, built for flexibility, silence, and surgical efficiency. Her scalp is shaved, and her face mask — etched with silver vinework — conceals all but her eyes, which are calm, unreadable, and always watching.

Her movements are smooth, deliberate, and economical — whether in conversation or combat. When Serena enters a room, people go silent without knowing why.

She doesn't need height or size to intimidate. She has perfect posture and a predator's calm.

## Personality

Serena is cold, precise, and ruthlessly competent. She is not cruel — but she is efficient, and efficiency has a body count. She does not banter, does not boast, and does not tolerate theatrics. Every action she takes serves an objective. Every word she speaks serves a function.

Though seen as icy, she is deeply respected within the Legion, and even feared by some of her peers. Her loyalty is to [[Gallus gen Quietus|Gallus]], to [[Vociterra]], and to the Empire's highest ideals: order, merit, strength, and progress.

She is also the eyes of the operation — not just on the battlefield, but in the minds of her comrades. Nothing escapes her.

## Character History

Serena rose through Vociterra's intelligence division — not through the academy. She was first an observer, then an infiltrator, then an executioner. Her missions across the eastern borderlands are classified, but her kill count speaks for itself — and the fact she still breathes speaks louder.

She became a Tribunis not through glory, but through necessity. When plans fell apart, Serena was the one who restored them. She does not command legions — she ensures the ones that remain are loyal, sharpened, and silent.

She was assigned to the First Legion to counterbalance [[Tribunis Ignia tes Metellus|Ignia]]. One is the hammer. Serena is the knife.

## Current Status

Serena is currently deployed with [[Gallus gen Quietus]], [[Tribunis Ignia tes Metellus|Ignia tes Metellus]], and [[Tribunis Recius met Helvus|Recius met Helvus]] aboard the Iron Standard, en route to Halinor.

Her primary responsibilities are field stabilization, civilian suppression, and Paragon containment coordination should supernatural interference occur.

Unofficially, she's also there to watch the watchers — ensuring that the mission, the officers, and soldiers remain within imperial compliance.

And if they don't? She's already prepared a contingency.

## Combat Style

Serena fights like a whisper that becomes a scream.

She wields twin gladii, forged with vultek reinforcement and weighted for rapid redirection. Her armor includes embedded shock dampeners and stealth-rigging to avoid mechanical detection. She is nearly silent in motion.

She prefers close quarters — a flurry of cuts and pivots with no wasted strikes. She has training in unarmed subdual, sabotage, and interrogation.

Where [[Tribunis Ignia tes Metellus|Ignia]] charges a line, Serena disappears behind it.

## Notable Relationships

**[[Gallus gen Quietus]]** – Her commanding officer and adopted father. She respects him as the past and future of the Legion — and possibly the Empire.

**[[Tribunis Ignia tes Metellus]]** – Her sister and counterbalance. Where Ignia defends, Serena eliminates. Their bond is forged in silence and mutual reliability.

**[[Tribunis Recius met Helvus]]** – She finds him inefficiently loud. She tracks his body language like a threat profile but recognizes his genius. He is useful, and therefore protected — until he isn't.
