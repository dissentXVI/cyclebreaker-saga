## Appearance

Gallus gen Quietus is a figure carved from discipline — whether armored or not, his presence demands silence.

In the field, he wears full Vociran dread-plate: matte black, unadorned save for subtle engravings of imperial wings and battle honors. The armor is heavy, brutally functional, and unrelenting in profile — designed to project command before a word is spoken. His horned helm, with its faceless visor and filtered vox-speech, has become an icon among Vociran troops: the voice of order, the symbol of iron resolve.

Unarmored, he is no less formidable. Gallus is a tall man in his late fifties with a hardened, weathered frame. His skin is deep, cool brown, marred by sun and scar alike — most notably the long, vertical wound that cuts from jaw to collarbone. His iron-gray hair is tightly coiled and drawn back in short, functional locks. His pale green eyes contrast sharply with his complexion — piercing, unblinking, and always calculating.

Off the battlefield, he wears a high-collared black officer's coat without ornament or rank insignia. No medals. No flourishes. His legacy precedes him.

## Personality

Gallus gen Quietus is the embodiment of Vociterra's founding ideals: discipline, order, and absolute belief in mankind's supremacy.

He is austere and commanding — a man who views hesitation as weakness and sentiment as sabotage. Every word he speaks is measured, every movement purposeful. He holds protocol as sacred and obedience as virtue — not just from his soldiers, but from himself. In his view, the chain of command is the spine of civilization, and he is its iron vertebrae.

Gallus harbors no ambition beyond service. He does not seek glory, favor, or legacy. He serves the Emperor without question and believes wholeheartedly in Vociterra's mission to elevate humanity beyond gods, magic, and weakness. Conquest, to him, is not cruelty — it is stewardship by force.

Though respected by subordinates and feared by enemies, he remains emotionally distant, even from those who admire him. He expects excellence, punishes failure, and rarely grants praise. In return, he leads from the front and shares the burdens of every campaign he commands.

To Gallus, doubt is a disease. And mercy is a myth that weakens the steel of civilization.

## Character History

Born to a minor patrician house in the heart of [[Vociterra]], Gallus gen Quietus was a soldier before he was a man. He rose through the ranks of the imperial legions not through privilege or politics, but through flawless execution of duty. Every campaign, every city pacified, every border secured — his record is clinical in its completeness.

His loyalty to the throne has never faltered. He believes with absolute conviction that Vociterra's strength is mankind's only path forward, and that imperial dominion is not tyranny, but salvation.

As [[Legatus]] of the First Legion, he has led dozens of military campaigns, culminating in the pivotal Siege of the White Sands, where his brilliance shattered the region's resistance in weeks. Afterward, Gallus returned to the capital to prepare for future operations, entrusting control of the territory to his subordinate, [[Cassius gen Amata]].

Cassius's later betrayal and the tragedy of the Trembling Foundations brought dishonor to the legions, but Gallus saw it not as a failure of the Empire — only of Cassius alone. He never spoke the man's name again and moved forward with twice the resolve, determined to ensure no other commander would stain the Empire's purpose with weakness.

## Current Status

Gallus gen Quietus is currently en route to Halinor by sea, leading a covert detachment personally sanctioned by the Emperor. Accompanied by his Tribunari — [[Tribunis Serena tes Paulus|Serena tes Paulus]], [[Tribunis Ignia tes Metellus|Ignia tes Metellus]], and [[Tribunis Recius met Helvus|Recius met Helvus]] — he is bound for Fort Diadum, a forward colony outpost near the continent's eastern marshes.

He travels with only fifteen elite soldiers and no air support — by choice. The route ahead cuts through salt plains, marshland, and forest.

The Steel Bear moves once again. And this time, he does not move for conquest. He moves for unbinding.

## Combat Style

Gallus is a battlefield commander and a front-line juggernaut.

Wielding a Vociran thunder lance powered by vultek capacitors, he commands with presence and force. His armor is fitted with micro-hydraulic boosters to counteract the weight of his weapon and allow for sudden bursts of motion. Despite his age, he moves like a siege engine given form — deliberate, unstoppable, immune to fatigue.

He favors direct confrontation over tactical flourish. His strategies are elegant in their simplicity: overwhelm, isolate, annihilate. And when tactics fail, he simply walks through the fire.

## Notable Relationships

**[[Lucius sep Magnus]]** – The Emperor of Vociterra and Gallus's sovereign. Gallus serves him with unwavering loyalty and absolute conviction, believing Lucius to be the chosen architect of mankind's future. Lucius sees him not just as a trusted general, but as a brother.

**[[Caedus seq Magnus]]** – Gallus respects his power, but distrusts his aimlessness. The two have exchanged few words, but always speak as equals.
