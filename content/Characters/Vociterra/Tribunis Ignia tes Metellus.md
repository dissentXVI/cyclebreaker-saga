**Title**: Tribunis Secunda of the First Legion

**Allegiance**: [[Vociterra]]

**Race**: Human (Vociran)

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Mid-30s

**Eyes**: Blue

**Hair**: Ash-blonde, usually bound or hidden beneath her helm

**Attire**: Heavy dread-plate reinforced with vultek-weave musculature; iconic horned helm and crimson war-tabard

**Home**: Patria Noster

## Appearance

Ignia is a juggernaut in flesh and steel.

Encased in her personalized Vociran dread-plate, she cuts a fearsome silhouette: broad-shouldered, powerfully built, and unmistakable for her horned helm and blood-red war-tabard. Her armor is scored from battle but lovingly maintained — every dent a memory, every repair a promise.

Beneath the armor, she is tall and physically imposing, with a muscular frame forged through brutal training regimens and decades of campaign. Her blue eyes reflect the cold intensity of someone who has never known peace. Her ash-blonde hair is kept tight and utilitarian, rarely seen beyond the helm.

She speaks with a gravel-edged contralto, clipped and direct. She is not easily roused — but once engaged, she is a force of devastation.

## Personality

Ignia is disciplined, unyielding, and utterly loyal — to [[Gallus gen Quietus|Gallus]], to [[Vociterra]], and to the Emperor's vision. Where others posture, she performs. Where others hesitate, she charges.

Though blunt, she is not unkind. Her affection is expressed through protection, not praise. She has little patience for politics, ceremony, or speeches. What matters is action — and whether you can hold your line when the order is given.

Among her peers, she is quiet but respected. Among her enemies, she is feared. She does not take prisoners unless ordered. And she hates being ordered.

## Character History

Born in the industrial blocks of Patria Noster, Ignia came from a low military caste family with no social prestige — only duty. She enlisted into the Vociran legions at fifteen and was fighting on the Karthasic frontier before she could legally vote.

Her performance in the Battle of Kair Daxos caught the eye of [[Gallus gen Quietus]], who personally recommended her elevation. Since then, she has served as his shield arm — often deployed to hold the line when hope collapses.

She became a Tribunis by merit alone, the youngest in her cohort, and has held the rank longer than most survive to remember it.

Though not blood-related, she and [[Tribunis Serena tes Paulus|Serena tes Paulus]] are considered twins-in-command: one a scalpel, the other a hammer. They share meals, battlefields, and unspoken faith in one another.

## Current Status

Ignia is currently deployed alongside [[Gallus gen Quietus]], [[Tribunis Serena tes Paulus|Serena tes Paulus]], and [[Tribunis Recius met Helvus|Recius met Helvus]], en route to Halinor aboard the Iron Standard. Their destination: Fort Diadum and the inland forests beyond.

Her assignment is clear: protect the Legatus, eliminate resistance, and ensure the expedition completes its objective without external interference.

She sharpens her blade daily, even at sea.

## Combat Style

Ignia is a brutalist melee specialist who favors raw momentum and crushing impact over finesse.

Her weapon of choice is a massive vulcaneum-forged flail, engineered with a spiked, vultek-cored head capable of destabilizing enemy formations and crushing armor. The flail's weighted design takes advantage of Ignia's physical strength, allowing her to tear through shields, breach defensive lines, and send lesser combatants sprawling with a single blow.

Her fighting style is violent, methodical, and unrelenting — an advancing storm of steel and chain. She doesn't parry. She pulverizes.

Ignia does not duel. She dominates.

## Notable Relationships

**[[Gallus gen Quietus]]** – Her commanding officer, her adopted father, and the only man she has ever truly respected. She trusts him with her life — and would give it for his.

**[[Tribunis Serena tes Paulus]]** – Sister and closest comrade. Their bond is forged in silence and mutual reliability. Where Ignia defends, Serena eliminates. Together, they are balance incarnate.

**[[Tribunis Recius met Helvus]]** – She doesn't understand half the things he says and doesn't care to. She protects him anyway, because Gallus said so.
