**Title**: Research Scholar of the Laconian Field Initiative

**Allegiance**: [[Shurevaire]] (Independent Fellowship)

**Race**: Half-Elf

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: 22

**Eyes**: Ice blue

**Hair**: Long, raven-dark waves

**Attire**: Scholarly black and navy field garb with gold-thread hems, reinforced with subtle arcane lining; always carries a journal bound in worn leather

**Home**: The Gatehouse, [[Laconia]]

## Appearance

Ylaena Sharie presents a figure defined by restraint — short and slight, yet held together with unshakable precision. Her ice-blue eyes peer over narrow-rimmed spectacles with a calm that veers dangerously close to judgment. Pale-skinned and often marked by shadows beneath her eyes, she favors tight braids or practical updos. There's a quiet severity to her posture, as though every breath is rationed for something more important. Despite her youth, she gives the impression of someone who's already had to choose survival over softness — and made her peace with it.

## Personality

Ylaena is unapologetically serious, driven by a fierce dedication to knowledge and an almost ascetic discipline. Her humor is dry, her affections rare, and her patience threadbare — but those she allows into her life earn a loyalty so deep it terrifies her. She loathes inefficiency, superstition, and wasted time. Yet beneath her controlled exterior is a survivor's sensitivity, one that mistrusts sentiment but craves understanding. She rarely speaks of her past, but when she commits to a cause or a person, she does so with unflinching conviction.

## Character History

Born to unknown parentage and raised by a witch she refuses to name, Ylaena spent her early life not as a student, but as a servant — perhaps even a tool. She rarely discusses what happened before she fled south, but by her own account, she was taught obedience, not magic. Her arrival in [[Shurevaire]] was marked by feral academic hunger — she studied without sleep, spoke to no one, and treated even kindness with suspicion.

It was [[Theomaire Dalinestri]] who first breached that isolation, gently undoing her barriers with patience and wit. Through Theo, she met [[Gothmog Gansukh]], and what began as a solitary scholar became a trio — outcasts bound by understanding and strange loyalty. Ylaena takes pride in having pushed the two into a relationship, calling it her "Life's Work in the Field of Romance."

She now conducts fieldwork on behalf of [[Shurevaire]], operating from [[Laconia]] where the frontier meets a rising threat. Her studies focus on finding anthropological insights in various tribes around Halinor, magical instability, and relics.

## Current Status

Ylaena resides in [[Laconia]] and is one of the few scholars granted freedom to conduct autonomous investigations in the region, owing to her exceptional work — and a reputation for disregarding authority when it impedes progress. She often works alone but maintains correspondence with [[Theomaire Dalinestri]] and [[Gothmog Gansukh]], whom she considers her true family.

In recent months, her focus has shifted toward anthropological research — studying the beliefs, structures, and rituals of the various monster tribes in Halinor. She suspects that some may be developing the conditions necessary for Paragon summoning: mass trauma, spiritual convergence, and ideological unity. By cataloging their myths and cultural hierarchies, she hopes to identify warning signs before another Cataclysm begins. Her reports are dense, meticulous, and often ignored — but she writes them anyway.

## Combat Style

Ylaena is a ritual caster and field mage, specializing in warding circles, burst control magic, and planar diagnostics. She favors precision over spectacle, often relying on magical traps, seal glyphs, and arcane thresholds to manipulate a battlefield. Though not formally trained in combat, her spells are honed by necessity — clean, brutal, and fast. She does not fight to win; she fights to end the threat as quickly and cleanly as possible.

## Notable Relationships

**[[Theomaire Dalinestri]]** – Her first and closest friend. Theo is the light that thawed her — annoying, kind, persistent, and unshakably important.

**[[Gothmog Gansukh]]** – Her second closest friend and the one she trusts most with silence.

## Secrets

Ylaena still dreams of the woman who raised her — not as a mother, but as a force of control. Sometimes she wakes with foreign sigils carved faintly into her arms, gone by morning. She keeps detailed logs of these dreams but has yet to share them.
