**Title**: Speaker of the Pneumeric Concord

**Allegiance**: [[Shurevaire]]

**Race**: Tiefling

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Late 20s

**Eyes**: Amber

**Hair**: Dark brown, voluminous curls

**Attire**: Layered scholarly robes of burgundy and navy, embroidered with spell-thread sigils and gold piping; arcane spectacles enchanted for clarity and protection

**Home**: The Sonotte, [[Shurevaire]]

## Appearance

Diazana Trubough gives the impression of someone who dressed in a hurry — and still managed to look better than everyone else in the room. Her layered robes are elegant but unevenly buttoned, her curls half-pinned with a quill still stuck behind one ear, and her jewelry mismatched but meaningful. Her horns are polished but chipped in places, suggesting a life of motion over maintenance. Her spectacles slip down her nose during impassioned lectures, and she often forgets where she left her gloves, book, or teacup — though never her arguments. Her style walks the line between arcane scholar and academic whirlwind.

## Personality

Brilliant, irreverent, and endlessly curious, Diazana thrives in the space between structure and chaos. While she commands respect for her sharp intellect and formidable knowledge, she's equally known for improvising theories mid-sentence and solving problems with methods no sane scholar would endorse. She laughs often — sometimes at herself, often at others — and wields sarcasm like a scalpel. Her style is instinctive, her research unorthodox, and her teaching methods wildly unpredictable. What she lacks in traditional decorum, she makes up for in results.

## Character History

Born to a reclusive family of tiefling archivists in northern [[Halinor]], Diazana was recognized early for her affinity with pneuma and magical artifacts. She studied under Archmage [[Velethoui]], a legendary figure in pneuma theory and spellweaving. Alongside her peer [[Yenden Clearlade]], she trained in both arcane application and the philosophical responsibilities of power. The two students often clashed in method but shared a mutual respect and deep loyalty to their mentor.

Shortly before [[Velethoui Arsenault|Velethoui]]'s sacrifice during the emergence of [[Krisnarax]], both Diazana and [[Yenden Clearlade|Yenden]] appealed to the Council of Deans, urging [[Shurevaire]] to abandon its growing isolationism and begin cooperating with the other city-states to prevent an all-out war between Vociterra and [[Halinor]]. Their plea was dismissed. In the aftermath, [[Yenden Clearlade|Yenden]] was assigned to oversee the cataloging and containment of archaeological sites surrounding the [[The Cosmic Pillar|Watcher of the Lake]]. Diazana, however, was stripped of her official duties entirely. It wasn't until Velethoui's grandchildren, [[Sydnea Arsenault]] and [[Sylviane Arsenault]], expressed their desire to follow in their grandfather's footsteps and leave [[Shurevaire]] that Diazana found a new purpose. She chose to accompany the twins, both to guide their growth — and to ensure their safety in a world her mentor once died to protect.

## Current Status

Diazana now travels beyond [[Shurevaire]]'s borders for the first time in years, accompanying [[Sydnea Arsenault]] and [[Sylviane Arsenault]], the twin grandchildren of her late mentor, Archmage [[Velethoui]]. Though she claims her role is that of an advisor and guardian, those close to her suspect deeper motives — not least of which is ensuring the twins do not repeat the mistakes of the past.

She also bears a burden few know of: [[Duisaratte]], [[Velethoui Arsenault|Velethoui]]'s shattered staff, recovered from the [[The Cosmic Pillar|Watcher of the Lake]] after being stolen by [[Layla Basura]]. Aspiria's Chosen entrusted it to her in secret, believing her the most likely to unravel its broken pneuma structure and, perhaps, uncover remnants of her mentor's final thoughts. Though her ties to Shurevaire's institutions have grown cold, Diazana remains one of its most capable — and unpredictable — agents in the field.

## Combat Style

Diazana's magic is raw, intuitive, and turbulent — like the storm she embodies. A natural-born Storm Sorcerer, she channels volatile pneuma through instinct rather than study. Lightning dances at her fingertips when she grows agitated, and her presence in combat is like a sudden squall: loud, fast, and unpredictable. She avoids direct confrontation when possible, preferring to disorient foes with thunderous force and reposition allies through wind-guided movement. While she carries a wand for focus, her most dangerous spells come from within — especially when her emotions flare.

## Notable Relationships

**[[Velethoui Arsenault|Archmage Velethoui]]** – Her former mentor. His death defined the second chapter of her life. Though she rarely speaks of him directly, her current path is shaped by the questions he left unanswered.

**[[Yenden Clearlade]]** – Fellow student and longtime friend. Though their personalities diverge — Yenden methodical and reserved, Diazana impulsive and bold — the two have always complemented each other's strengths. Their debates are legendary in academic circles, but so is their loyalty. He tempers her chaos; she sparks his imagination. Even after their paths diverged following Velethoui's death, their trust remains unshaken.

**[[Sydnea Arsenault]] & [[Sylviane Arsenault]]** – Velethoui's twin grandchildren, now under Diazana's guidance. She sees flashes of both [[Velethoui Arsenault|Velethoui]]'s brilliance and his recklessness in them. Her protectiveness is fierce, even if she masks it with sarcasm.

## Secrets

She holds Duisaratte, Velethoui's staff, in order to find a way to repair it.
