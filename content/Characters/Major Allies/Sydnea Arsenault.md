## Appearance

Sydnea Arsenault carries the composed elegance of someone born to both expectation and etiquette. His pale blond hair frames a youthful, observant face, and his long elven ears accentuate his scholarly bearing. He typically wears finely tailored robes in Shurevaire's colors, embroidered with gold-leaf patterns that signal his ties to the Concordant Colleges. His green eyes often flick between his surroundings, taking in details others miss — not out of paranoia, but calculation. Though soft-spoken, he walks with the posture of someone trying to grow into the space once filled by greater names.

## Personality

Sydnea is intelligent, inquisitive, and fiercely principled. He thinks before he speaks, and when he speaks, he tries to get it right — not for show, but because he genuinely believes words matter. He often comes across as formal, especially to those less academically inclined, but beneath that careful reserve lies an earnest desire to help and to understand.

Having been sheltered most of his life within Shurevaire's halls, Sydnea is still adjusting to the disorder of the wider world. He doesn't always know how to act — but he always tries to do what's right.

## Character History

As the grandson of Archmage Velethoui Arsenault, Sydnea was raised in one of Shurevaire's most revered lineages. From a young age, he was groomed for scholarship, diplomacy, and legacy. But when Velethoui vanished during the emergence of [[Krisnarax]], and the University began to bury uncomfortable truths, Sydnea started to question the story he'd inherited.

Alongside his twin sister [[Sylviane Arsenault]], he chose to leave the safety of [[Shurevaire]] to learn the truth of their grandfather's final expedition — and the nature of the forces that took him. Their journey brought them to [[Laconia]], where they crossed paths with Aspiria's Chosen, tied to the [[Watcher of the Lake]], Duisaratte, and the deepening mystery of Halinor.

Though still learning the ropes of fieldwork, Sydnea brings with him a vast memory for cataclysmic history, pneumeric theory, and current events. He is no prodigy — but he is precise, committed, and painfully aware of the stakes.

## Current Status

Sydnea currently travels with Aspiria's Chosen, observing their actions and offering insight when needed. He is not a frontline presence, but his knowledge of Navarresean structure, Shurevarian theory, and academic precedent make him a valuable advisor.

He keeps detailed journals and correspondence with his sister, constantly revising his understanding of what [[Velethoui]] intended — and what he may have died for.

## Combat Style

Sydnea is not trained for combat, but he knows how to protect himself. He uses basic evocation and abjuration spells — light barriers, force pulses, and arcane locks — often more to buy time than end a threat. He prefers positioning himself behind those with greater skill, offering strategy and support. What he lacks in raw power, he makes up for in clarity of purpose and battlefield observation.

## Notable Relationships

**[[Sylviane Arsenault]]** – His twin and most trusted confidant. They balance one another: where she challenges, he grounds.

**[[Diazana Trubough]]** – A scholar he admires deeply. Her confidence both inspires and intimidates him.
