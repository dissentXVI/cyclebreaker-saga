## Appearance

Sylviane Arsenault strikes an immediate impression — elegant, formidable, and unmistakably direct. Her piercing green eyes gleam with unspoken challenge, and her posture reflects the discipline of a trained duelist rather than a cloistered scholar. She wears her platinum-blond hair in twin braids that fall over her armored shoulders, often adorned with charms picked up during her travels. Her clothing blends the academic colors of [[Shurevaire]] with practical field gear, allowing freedom of movement for both spellwork and swordplay.

She moves with fluidity — always half-ready to unsheathe her blade, half-ready to prove someone wrong.

## Personality

Sylviane is fearless, passionate, and impatient with excuses. She speaks her mind — especially when others hesitate — and thrives in situations that demand bold action. Where her brother debates, she acts. Where others study, she charges forward. Though headstrong, her motives are pure: she believes in justice, clarity, and protecting those too slow to protect themselves.

Her emotions run deep beneath her sharp tongue, especially when it comes to her family. She fiercely loves her brother, even as she clashes with his caution, and carries the weight of Velethoui's legacy like a sword at her side — not a relic to study, but a responsibility to wield.

## Character History

Raised in the high halls of [[Shurevaire]], Sylviane was trained in both spellcraft and fencing from a young age — a dual path she insisted upon despite academic advisors steering her toward safer disciplines. As the granddaughter of Archmage Velethoui, she was expected to embody grace, intellect, and diplomacy.

She chose confrontation instead.

When her grandfather vanished after the emergence of [[Krisnarax]], Sylviane demanded answers. She refused to accept the University's silence and quickly aligned herself with [[Diazana Trubough]], who shared her distrust of institutional inaction. Alongside her twin [[Sydnea Arsenault]], Sylviane traveled to [[Laconia]], determined to uncover what happened at the site of Krisnarax's arrival.

While Sydnea made cautious appeals to local scholars and authorities, Sylviane began scouting ruins and questioning survivors. She believes Velethoui's end is linked to something far greater — and that sitting on the sidelines is no longer an option.

## Current Status

Sylviane is currently conducting a self-appointed investigation into the Sticaean Abyss, determined to uncover any trace of her grandfather's fate. She believes the truth lies underground — in the collapsed tunnels, forgotten breathstone veins, or sealed ruins hidden beneath the former lakebed. She fully intended to explore it alone, unwilling to wait for permission or institutional support.

[[Jendrick Ironbrook]] volunteered to accompany her, recognizing both the danger and the fire in her resolve. She accepted, though made it clear she would have gone without him. Together, they now delve into the unstable caverns, where whispers of old pneuma and deeper threats linger in the dark.

## Combat Style

A trained bladesinger, Sylviane blends precise swordplay with arcane precision. Her fighting style is fast, elegant, and reactive — using minor enchantments, illusion magic, and shield runes mid-duel to gain the upper hand. She is most dangerous when cornered, able to deflect a spell and land a riposte in the same breath.

While she prefers one-on-one engagements, she adapts quickly in group battles, often rushing high-priority threats or shielding wounded allies with a single sharp command: "Move."

## Notable Relationships

**[[Sydnea Arsenault]]** – Her twin and constant foil. She loves him dearly but wishes he would get out of his own way.

**[[Diazana Trubough]]** – A mentor in philosophy and frustration. Sylviane admires her fearlessness and shares her distrust of ivory tower politics.
