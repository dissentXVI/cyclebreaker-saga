**Title**: The Voice of the Spire

**Allegiance**: [[Durnirax]]

**Race**: White Dragon

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Adult

**Eyes**: Bright gold

**Scales**: Pale white with silver-blue sheen, like fresh snow under moonlight

**Home**: The Skyrend Peaks of Eisfeld

## Appearance

Laufraxes bears her father's majesty with a gentler bearing. Her scales glisten with the iridescence of frost at dawn, and her lithe frame speaks of grace rather than brute power. Her wings, vast and luminous, ripple like silk in flight. Her eyes are alert, often contemplative — a dragon more given to watching than roaring.

## Personality

Born of one of the most powerful beings in Aspiria, Laufraxes surprises many with her restraint. She is contemplative and kindhearted, balancing the severity of her bloodline with a disarming warmth. She holds a fascination with mortal philosophies and believes bridges might yet be rebuilt — not through dominance, but dialogue.

Laufraxes is loyal to [[Durnirax]] but is not blind to his rigidity. She walks a fine line: never betraying his trust, yet quietly daring to hope for peace.

## Character History

As the daughter of [[Durnirax]], Laufraxes was raised in a time of rising distrust between dragons and mortals. While her elder kin waged wars or forged empires, she was appointed as a watchful sentinel over the Skyrend Peaks — a role that would eventually shape her identity.

During the early peace efforts between [[Saint Iseult]] and the dragons, Laufraxes was often the first and only point of contact between mortals and her father's domain. She developed a cautious respect for Iseult, and even after the Church declared her a heretic, Laufraxes remembered her words.

She has long feared what [[Wodirethrax|Wodr]]'s rage would awaken. With her aunt [[Skadraxes]] slain and war burning anew, she remains perched at the edge of a precipice — one step away from either intervention or resignation.

## Current Status

Laufraxes continues to dwell in the Skyrend Peaks, serving as messenger and emissary for [[Durnirax]]. She maintains a distant watch on the mortal world and the unfolding [[Wyrmsfall War]]. Though she has not yet acted, some believe she is one mortal tragedy away from defying her father — or being used against her will.

She often appears to those lost in the mountains, never speaking, only watching. Whether it is mercy or warning is left unknown.

## Combat Style

Though not inclined toward violence, Laufraxes is no pacifist. When forced to battle, she favors precision, misdirection, and control. Her breath freezes not just bodies but pneuma itself — a chilling stillness that numbs magic and memory. She flies with terrifying silence, her wings slicing snowstorms from the sky, and can conjure illusions with her voice alone.

## Notable Relationships

**[[Durnirax]]** – Her father and sovereign. She serves him loyally, though their visions of the world differ.

**[[Wodirethrax]]** – Her uncle, once a fierce protector, now a force of terrifying vengeance. She mourns what he's become.
