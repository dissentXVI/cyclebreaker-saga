**Title**: The Radiant Beast

**Allegiance**: None

**Race**: Bronze Dragon

**Gender**: Male

**Age**: Deceased

**Eyes**: Once emerald, now hollow with corruption

**Scales**: Bronze with greenish undertones that once shimmered with auroral energy

**Home**: Kokyotos (in life); the [[Sticaean Abyss]] (in undeath)

**Status**: Deceased — slain by the [[Navarresean Empire]]; shade later bound in the [[Sticaean Abyss]] by Archmage Velethoui

## Appearance

In life, Krisnarax was glorious — a sleek, sinuous wyrm with aurora-glazed wings and gleaming bronze scales that sparked with breathstone light. His body coursed with raw pneuma, and his voice could call lightning from still skies.

In undeath, he is a twisted echo of himself, his form bound in arcane manacles, scales turned sickly and tarnished, his once-mighty eyes hollowed and glowing with the corruption of forbidden summoning.

## Personality

Once bold, charismatic, and fiercely protective of his kin, Krisnarax was beloved not only by his siblings but revered by early cultures across Kokyotos. He spoke to mortals often, teaching them how to navigate storms and command lightning.

Now, as a shade bound in the [[Sticaean Abyss]], all that remains is a vestige of who he was — rage, memory, and pain, screaming beneath the surface of arcane restraint.

## Character History

Krisnarax lived in Kokyotos, the southern archipelagos, where he was considered a patron of seafarers and storm-callers. When the Navarreseans began their campaign against the dragons of the north, he flew to aid his kin — despite [[Nottraxes]]'s pleas to remain behind.

He was slain by the [[Navarresean Empire]], and his death shattered his sister, [[Nottraxes]].

## Legacy

Krisnarax's shade was released during the cataclysm known as the Trembling Foundations, summoned through forbidden rites by [[Vociran Military Ranks & Terms|Legatus]] Cassius tes Amata. The Radiant Beast's shade erupted into the skies, searing the land with corrupted pneuma and near-divine wrath.

It was Archmage Velethoui who halted the devastation. With the last of his life and knowledge, he rebound Krisnarax in a desperate act of arcane mastery, casting the shade into the depths of the [[Sticaean Abyss]]. Though sealed, Krisnarax's pneuma still pulses below the lakebed, and echoes of his presence can be felt throughout the shattered landscape.
