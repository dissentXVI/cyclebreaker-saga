**Title**: The Hidden Flame of [[Mera To Haj]]

**Allegiance**: Secret Patron of Saptha Sindhu

**Race**: Brass Dragon

**Gender**: Male

**Age**: Ancient

**Eyes**: Amber-gold, glowing with soft internal fire

**Scales**: Gleaming brass, darkened at the edges

**Home**: Sealed caverns beneath [[Mera To Haj]], Saptha Sindhu

## Appearance

Alvaderax is an imposing yet graceful dragon, his form burnished like a living monument to the sun. Brass scales cover his long, muscular body, glinting in shades of gold, bronze, and copper as they catch the light. His wings are wide and fan-like, mottled with desert hues and edged in soot-dark tips. Twin horns arc back from his brow, and his deep-set amber eyes convey immense age and unspoken judgment.

## Personality

Alvaderax is patient, deliberate, and distant. He holds a deep reverence for knowledge and measured change, disdaining impulsiveness and blind zeal. Though some might call him cold or indifferent, those few who have earned his counsel know his restraint hides deep conviction.

He often speaks in parables and riddles, preferring mortals to arrive at truth through effort rather than instruction. He rarely takes direct action, believing in the subtle hand over the crushing claw.

## Character History

As one of [[Ymirethor]]'s First Brood, Alvaderax chose to observe rather than conquer. Drawn to the southern isle of Saptha Sindhu, he wandered the dunes and learned the pulse of the land. There, he found mortals on the brink of collapse — their wars endless, their gods silent.

Through secret intervention, Alvaderax elevated one of the weaker dynasties, whispering wisdom into their dreams and guiding them toward unity. His influence became legend, then myth, and eventually a forgotten truth. When the divine pantheon rose and new powers laid claim to the world, Alvaderax descended into the depths of [[Mera To Haj]] and disappeared from mortal sight.

## Current Status

Alvaderax remains in hiding, watching from afar. His agents — sages, hermits, and veiled prophets — act in his stead. Though silent, he remains deeply attuned to the shifting tides of Aspiria, and may soon be forced to rise again.

## Combat Style

Alvaderax avoids violence, but when roused, he is devastating. His breath is not a searing flame but a radiant brass conflagration that induces hallucinations, memory visions, and soul-deep introspection. Enemies may fall without a wound, undone by their own revelations.

He uses illusion, tremor magic, and sand-based manipulation to control terrain, disorient foes, or vanish entirely. When forced to strike, his talons and tail crush stone and steel alike.

## Notable Relationships

**[[Ymirethor]]** – Revered as progenitor and moral compass.

**Rulers of Old Saptha Sindhu** – Few know how many dynasties owe their rise to his influence.
