**Title**: The Pacifist

**Allegiance**: [[Saint Iseult]]

**Race**: Gold Dragon

**Gender**: Male

**Age**: Ancient

**Eyes**: One molten golden eye

**Scales**: Gleaming gold with pale feathered wings and tail tufts

**Home**: The Spire of Last Light, an unscalable mountain deep in Eisfeld

## Appearance

Radiating nobility and restrained power, Durnirax is a vision of regal divinity. His golden scales catch the light like polished sun-metal, yet his wings are feathered in white and gold — a rare mutation even among dragons, more angelic than draconic. Though aged, he is unbent. The few mortals who have seen him describe him as awe-inspiring, like a sunrise frozen in form.

## Personality

Durnirax is a paradox of radiance and regret. Though he is the most refined of the First Brood, time has carved weariness into his spirit. He once believed in the mortal soul's potential — believed it so deeply, he bound one to his own. Now, he exists in self-imposed exile, reluctant to hope again.

He is wise, serene, and restrained — yet beneath his golden exterior lies the quiet ache of an ancient love lost to time and choice. His kindness has hardened into stoicism, and his dreams into memories.

## Character History

Long ago, during the fragile era of peace brokered by [[Saint Iseult]], Durnirax did the unthinkable — he fell in love with a mortal.

Iseult, bold and luminous, touched something in him that none — not even his kin — ever had. They considered each other soulmates, twin flames born of flesh and flame. But she was mortal, and he was not.

Knowing time would tear them apart, Iseult pleaded with Durnirax to consume her soul, that she might dwell within him forever. Reluctantly — agonizingly — he agreed.

Her essence remains within him, a luminous presence that brings both comfort and sorrow. With her passing, he withdrew from the world, a dragon broken by the very love that once made him feel whole.

When [[Wodirethrax|Wodr]] came to him after [[Skadraxes]]' death, eyes torn from his skull by Carinthian blades, Durnirax could not bear to destroy the nation his beloved once called home. He gave his eye so [[Wodirethrax|Wodr]] might live — but refused to join his brother in vengeance. That decision shattered their bond.

## Current Status

Durnirax dwells in mourning atop the Spire of Last Light, far from the affairs of the world. His voice has not been heard in centuries. Mortals speak his name with reverence, dragons with silence.

He watches the [[Wyrmsfall War]] unfold like a storm beneath his perch, refusing to intervene. His love for [[Saint Iseult]] — and his guilt for outliving her — binds him to his solitude.

## Combat Style

Durnirax is an embodiment of precision and permanence. He does not lash out — he pronounces. His breath is not flame, but pure radiant force that reduces lies, illusions, and hope to dust. His presence alone bends reality, a steadying force against chaos. He commands light, gravity, and reflective pneuma, able to turn spells back upon their casters and erase movement from the battlefield.

He fights only when necessary — and when he does, it is absolute.

## Notable Relationships

**[[Saint Iseult]]** – His most beloved. Her soul dwells within him still.

**[[Wodirethrax]]** – His elder brother. Once close; now estranged by grief and the cost of a single eye.

**[[Laufraxes]]** – His daughter. He trusts her loyalty, though their visions for the world are not the same.
