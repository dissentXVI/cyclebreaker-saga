**Title**: The Kaleidoscopic One

**Allegiance**: None

**Race**: Unknown-type Dragon

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Ancient

**Eyes**: Shifting hues of violet, blue, and silver

**Scales**: Constantly changing — rippling with iridescent patterns that shift through pearlescent pinks, deep blues, silver-white, and shadowed black

**Home**: Formerly the isles of Kokyotos; now bound deep within [[Taras'Nula]]

## Appearance

Nottraxes defies mortal classification. Her scales shift ceaselessly — ocean-glass one moment, starlit amethyst the next — refracting light into impossibly delicate patterns. Her wings are translucent and prismatic, appearing as shimmering veils of dreamstuff.

Even in stillness, she seems in motion — a creature unstuck from linear time. Her gaze pierces veils of illusion and identity alike, revealing the many faces of a soul.

## Personality

Once a beacon of insight and wonder, Nottraxes now bears the weight of sorrow beyond reckoning. She is introspective, mournful, and often distant — her words woven with regret and veiled meanings.

Though her voice can still soothe, and her mind still sees far, she speaks less than she once did. When she does, it is with the tone of one who knows that truth, once bent, may never fully return.

## Character History

Nottraxes was one of the most mystical of [[Ymirethor]]'s children, revered in the isles of Kokyotos as a goddess of dreams, change, and truth. Her closest bond was with her brother, [[Krisnarax]], whose joy and courage anchored her wandering spirit.

When [[Krisnarax]] flew north to defend their kin against the rising [[Navarresean Empire]], she did not stop him — nor did she foresee the price. The empire, having unearthed and reverse-engineered the remains of Omnistrix, a dormant Telothian hunter, used its technology to slay him.

Overcome with grief, Nottraxes was approached by an [[The Archorans|Archoran]] who promised a way to bring [[Krisnarax]] back. She agreed. The ritual succeeded — but what returned was not her brother, but a shade, a hollow mockery of what he had been. This false [[Krisnarax]] was twisted, bound, and ultimately used to power the [[Watcher of the Lake|Cosmic Pillar]] — a structure of immense arcane significance.

Wracked by shame and horror at what she had unleashed, Nottraxes gave up her freedom. She flew to the empire's doorstep and allowed herself to be taken. Bound in the heart of [[Taras'Nula]], she was repurposed into a living source of energy and insight, her body tethered and her dreams mined.

## Current Status

Imprisoned in [[Taras'Nula]], Nottraxes remains trapped in a state between waking and dreaming. Her consciousness still pulses through the arcane grid of the facility, warping pneuma and disrupting time within its walls. Though her body lies dormant, her guilt and vision stretch far beyond her cage. She believes this imprisonment is penance for tarnishing her brother's memory.

## Combat Style

**Breath of the Endless Prism** — A radiant exhalation that inflicts visions of alternate lives, fates, or timelines. Targets may lose touch with reality entirely.

**Fractal Displacement** — Her form fractures, multiplying in mirrored reflections that confuse and misdirect.

**Dreambound Pulse** — Erodes mental defenses, instilling visions of deep personal trauma or joy. Often ends fights before they begin.

## Notable Relationships

**[[Krisnarax]]** – Her beloved brother. His death shattered her. His corrupted return broke her.
