**Title**: None

**Allegiance**: None

**Race**: Black Dragon

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Juvenile

**Eyes**: Pale gold

**Scales**: Onyx-black with a faint violet sheen in sunlight

**Home**: Formerly [[Chilmire Bog]]; currently a frozen cave in northern Halinor

## Appearance

Sigraxes is smaller and leaner than her elder kin, her onyx-scaled form often mistaken for shadow until the glint of her eyes or the shimmer of her wings gives her away. Her horns are short but jagged, and her frilled tail tapers to a narrow, spiked end. Despite her youth, her claws are sharp, and her gaze carries a wary intelligence.

## Personality

Cautious but deeply curious, Sigraxes is still forming her opinions of the world. Her trust is hard-earned, shaped by betrayal and injury, but once given, it is loyal and fierce. She prefers solitude but not isolation — watching from afar, sometimes mimicking mortal customs with awkward earnestness. She carries the trauma of her flight from [[Chilmire Bog]] but yearns for connection and purpose.

## Character History

Sigraxes was born in the fetid shadows of [[Chilmire Bog]], a region steeped in necrotic breathstone. Her brood held no great name and was content in obscurity — until the lizardfolk began their rituals. They summoned something vast, croaking, and hungry — a death god cloaked in moss and decay. The air grew cold. Her elders fell silent.

Fleeing alone, Sigraxes flew north but was soon wounded by Carinthian scouts who mistook her for a threat. She limped from one frozen ridge to another until finally finding a small, hollow cave near a frozen lake. There, she was found by the Icehearts — heretics to the [[The Light of Alophia|Light of Alophia]] but kind to her. [[Renai Aymere]], the Ice Witch, treated her wounds and spoke to her like a person, not a monster.

Eventually, she met Aspiria's Chosen, who offered her something she hadn't dared hope for: a chance to reclaim her home. A promise was made — they would face the death god in the bog, and she could return.

## Current Status

Sigraxes remains in hiding near the Iceheart sanctuary, still recovering from her wounds. She awaits word from Aspiria's Chosen. If they succeed, she will fly home — but if they fail, she may have nowhere left to go.

## Combat Style

Sigraxes is not yet fully grown, but her venomous breath and swift tail make her a capable skirmisher. She favors hit-and-run tactics and often uses illusions or terrain to confuse foes. She avoids direct confrontation unless desperate or defending someone she trusts.

## Notable Relationships

**[[Renai Aymere]]** – The first mortal to ever show her kindness. Renai called her "clever girl" and left food by the lake. Sigraxes listens when Renai speaks, even if she doesn't understand everything.

**[[Aspiria's Chosen]]** – She sees them as strange but possibly true to their word. Their promise is a beacon: a way back to the only home she's ever known.
