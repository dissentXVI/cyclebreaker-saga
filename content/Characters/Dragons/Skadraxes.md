**Title**: The Betrayed

**Allegiance**: Her kin, and briefly, the emissaries of [[Carinthia]]

**Race**: Copper Dragon

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Ancient at time of death

**Eyes**: Once opalescent lilac — now lost

**Scales**: Burnished copper veined with pale green, faded from long years in high places

**Home**: Skyborne crags in Eisfeld

**Status**: Deceased — slain by [[Archbishop Renard Louis]] and the [[The Dawnbound|Dawnbound]] of [[Carinthia]]

## Appearance

Skadraxes was lithe and long-necked, her form more elegant than most of the First Brood. Her copper scales carried a soft patina, weathered by sun and wind, and her wings were long and narrow like banners on the wind. Her horns were uneven — one broken in her youth during a game with [[Wodirethrax|Wodr]] — and her claws were etched with centuries of carvings she made herself.

Her eyes, once her most striking feature, shimmered with a pale lavender light said to calm storms and reveal hidden truths. They are now gone.

## Personality

Skadraxes was curious, open-hearted, and bold — the most social of [[Ymirethor]]'s children. Where her siblings guarded, she shared; where others threatened, she laughed. She delighted in learning from mortals and believed in the fragile potential of peace.

But beneath her warmth lay a fierce pride. She saw compromise as strength, not weakness — and those who mocked that ideal found her fangs not far behind.

## Character History

During the Fourth Radiant Age, in the time of peace following the early dragon-elven wars, Skadraxes enjoyed the company of mortalkind. She made contact with the [[The Light of Alophia|Church of Alophia]], then led by [[Archbishop Renard Louis]], who proposed a meeting of kin and clergy.

But it was a trap. The [[The Dawnbound|Dawnbound]] — elite knights of [[Carinthia]] — fell upon her as she landed. [[Archbishop Renard Louis]] himself struck the final blow, gouging out her luminous eyes to empower a divine relic. Through divine prayer, these eyes were used to transform him into [[Louis'roi Arch de Soleil]], the so-called "Panoptic Will of God."

Skadraxes's death shattered the fragile peace. Enraged beyond reason, [[Wodirethrax|Wodr]] declared war upon [[Heldenheim|Carinthia]] and all elvenkind, launching what would become the [[Wyrmsfall War]].

## Legacy

Slain, her remains lie entombed in a secret reliquary beneath [[Heldenheim]], kept hidden by the [[The Light of Alophia|Church of Alophia]]. Her bones are ritually bound, her name stricken from official record. Only a few remember her as anything other than a monster — and fewer still dare speak the truth of her death.
