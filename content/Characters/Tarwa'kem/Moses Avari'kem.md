**Title**: Master of [[Sha'khaw Smithing Company]]; Councilor of the [[Monetarist Council]]

**Allegiance**: Himself, the Avari'kem lineage, and the integrity of dwarven craft

**Race**: Dwarf

**Gender**: Male

**Age**: Late 50s

**Eyes**: Deep bronze

**Hair**: Iron-gray, thick and matted into ceremonial locks

**Attire**: Formal dwarven robes layered with steel-threaded sashes, his chest always adorned with heirloom medallions and the sigil of his house

**Home**: [[Tarwa'kem]] (resides in [[Historical Quarter]], keeps quarters in [[Providence]] for council obligations)

## Appearance

Moses Avari'kem is a mountain carved into the shape of a man. With a wide chest, broad shoulders, and callused hands that once shaped weapons of kings, he wears the weight of history like armor. His beard is silver and streaked with coal dust, meticulously braided with precious stones — each marking a contract fulfilled, a war won, a legacy honored. His expression is unflinchingly stern, but never cruel.

## Personality

Moses is a man of iron principles and granite silence. He values tradition, merit, and accountability, and has no patience for frivolity. Yet beneath his heavy brow is a fiercely intelligent mind and a deep, unyielding pride in dwarven craftsmanship and heritage. Though others on the [[Monetarist Council]] scheme in the shadows, Moses plays no games — he simply outlasts. He speaks rarely in council, but when he does, it is with the weight of an avalanche.

## Character History

A descendant of the ancient Avari'kem line, Moses grew up in the forges of Sha'khaw, raised among embers, anvils, and whispered stories of the throne his ancestors once held. His rise to prominence came not from politics, but from excellence — every sword, gate, and mechanism forged under his seal became a thing of legend. His name alone commands premium prices in markets across Halinor.

Though his family lost royal status centuries ago, Moses has kept the Avari'kem name relevant through trade, discipline, and unwavering standards. His seat on the [[Monetarist Council]] was not granted — it was demanded by the reality of his influence.

## Current Status

Moses represents the interests of industrial and crafting guilds across [[Tarwa'kem]], particularly among dwarven artisans and deep-forge syndicates. While most nobles eye the future, Moses ensures the past is not buried beneath it. He maintains personal oversight of [[Sha'khaw Smithing Company|Sha'khaw]]'s operations and is often seen at dawn, inspecting shipments himself. He rarely speaks in political debates unless the dignity of dwarven labor is at stake — at which point, he makes himself impossible to ignore.
