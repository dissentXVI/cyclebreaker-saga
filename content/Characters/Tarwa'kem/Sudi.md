**Title**: Family Healer of House Zahir

**Allegiance**: [[House Zahir]]

**Race**: Human

**Gender**: Male

**Age**: Late 70s

**Eyes**: Cloudy brown

**Hair**: Long, gray-white, uncut and unruly; full beard stretching to his chest

**Attire**: Simple linen robes faded with age, reinforced at the knees and elbows; carries a satchel of dried herbs and worn scrolls

**Home**: A modest room on the Zahir estate, filled with books, dried poultices, and ancestral keepsakes

## Appearance

Sudi is hunched with age but unbroken. His frame, once lean, has weathered into that of an old tree — knotted, stubborn, and deeply rooted. His beard and hair flow like smoke, tangled and dignified. His hands shake when still but steady when in service. He walks slowly, often leaning on walls, but his presence is still a welcome relief for those who rely on him.

## Personality

Wise. Stoic. Deeply principled. Sudi is a man of older traditions, valuing spiritual balance as much as physical treatment. He believes that healing the body must come with attention to the soul, and that medicine without meaning is simply surgery. Though he speaks slowly and softly, there is gravity behind every word — he rarely wastes a breath.

Sudi is not quick to challenge others, but neither does he coddle. His guidance is given like bitter tea: strong, bracing, and not to everyone's taste — but good for the soul, if accepted.

## Character History

Sudi served the Zahir family long before it was noble. He was once the apprentice of a renowned desert healer, learning both traditional herbcraft and ritual-based medicine. When [[Omar Zahir]] needed a healer for his expanding family, he chose Sudi not just for his skills, but for his discretion and spiritual grounding.

Over the decades, Sudi became part of the household, aiding in births, tending to ailments, and quietly teaching each generation the fundamentals of life, death, and wellness. Though no longer as agile, he still oversees [[Monifa Zahir|Monifa]]'s care with unshakable resolve and refuses to let younger physicians override his judgment without reason.

## Current Status

Sudi remains one of the few constants in the Zahir household. He tends to [[Monifa Zahir|Monifa]] daily and has taken a more observant role in the estate's changing energy since [[Laena Zahir|Laena]]'s departure. He speaks rarely on politics, but those who listen closely know he is aware of more than he lets on.

While some see him as a relic, those in the know recognize Sudi as a moral anchor for the family — a living memory of a time before titles, before gilded halls, when survival and faith walked hand in hand.

## Notable Relationships

[[Omar Zahir]] – An old bond of trust. Sudi served Omar before the Zahir name carried power. They speak less now, but their shared silence means more than words.

[[Monifa Zahir]] – His closest charge. Sudi treats her with immense care, not pity. He believes she has a rare spirit, and speaks to her more gently than anyone else.

[[Laena Zahir]] – Respected her intelligence, though disapproved of her recklessness. He knew she would leave before anyone else did.

[[Aziza]] – Mutual respect. She manages the house; he keeps its heart beating. Their unspoken alliance holds the estate together.

## Secrets

In the quiet hours of night, Sudi sometimes communes with the ancestral breath left behind in family relics — bones, hair, or garments long kept by the Zahir line. He believes he can glean fragments of memory or intent from the departed. Recently, he attempted a quiet rite near [[Monifa Zahir|Monifa]]'s bedside, hoping to awaken protective ancestral spirits — but whether it succeeded or not, he has told no one.
