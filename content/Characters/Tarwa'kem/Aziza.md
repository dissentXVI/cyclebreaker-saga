**Title**: Head of Household, Zahir Estate

**Allegiance**: [[House Zahir]]

**Race**: Human

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Late 40s

**Eyes**: Pale blue

**Hair**: White

**Attire**: Gold-trimmed servant robes bearing the Zahir crest, adorned with tasteful jewelry denoting her station

**Home**: [[Providence]], [[Tarwa'kem]]

## Appearance

Aziza's presence commands the room long before she speaks. With alabaster skin, a sharp jaw, and an imposing halo of white curls, she carries the weight of both dignity and duty. Her keen blue eyes miss nothing, and every detail of her appearance — from her finely pressed robes to her perfectly positioned accessories — reinforces her authority. She does not simply manage the estate; she rules its inner world.

## Personality

Aziza is all business. She expects perfection from the [[House Zahir|Zahir]] domestic staff and accepts no excuses — though she does reward competence with fierce loyalty. Her tongue is razor-sharp and famously quick with comebacks, particularly when faced with gawkers or those who underestimate her. Though rarely warm, she is not cold; her care is measured in protection, pride, and the rare smile offered when no one else is looking.

## Character History

Aziza was born into the servant class of [[Tarwa'kem]] and rose quickly due to her intellect and commanding nature. After decades managing smaller noble households, she was handpicked by [[Rhaena Zahir]] to bring order and precision to the newly elevated Zahir estate. Some say she runs it with more discipline than any noble could. She is fiercely loyal to the family — particularly the girls, whose upbringing she oversaw as closely as their tutors did.

## Current Status

Still reigning over the Zahir household with an iron gaze and sharper tongue, Aziza has grown increasingly anxious about [[Laena Zahir|Laena]]'s sudden disappearance. She is fully aware that the family is covering up the truth of Laena's departure — and though she says nothing, her every action is bent toward ensuring the estate remains stable during this time.
