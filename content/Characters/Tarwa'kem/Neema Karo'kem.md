**Title**: Sultana of [[Tarwa'kem]]

**Allegiance**: [[The Karo'kem Dynasty]], [[Tarwa'kem]]

**Race**: Human

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: 18

**Eyes**: Dark brown

**Hair**: Long, thick black curls, often crowned or braided with gold

**Attire**: Royal vestments in midnight blue and gold, adorned with solar and ancestral symbols, always regal yet practical

**Home**: The [[Royal Palace]], [[Kemtabi Shelf]], [[Tarwa'kem]]

## Appearance

Sultana Neema is grace honed into strength. Though youthful, she carries herself with confidence born of survival, her presence regal without excess. She wears traditional royal attire — layered linen, embroidered lapis thread, and solar-emblazoned adornments — but never without intention. Her expressions are rarely unreadable: when she listens, she listens, and when she commands, there is no mistaking it.

## Personality

Neema is idealistic yet sharp, tempered by loss and an acute awareness of the political machinery around her. She believes that a ruler must see her people to lead them and does so — frequently appearing in the streets of [[Tarwa'kem]], speaking with laborers, merchants, and farmers. She is beloved by the lower classes and mistrusted by the upper crust. Though she presents a calm demeanor, she is unyielding once her mind is set, and behind her youth is a strategic instinct honed by years of political instruction under duress.

## Character History

Neema's mother, Nena, died in childbirth. Her father, Sultan Shakir Karo'kem, was assassinated a year later — an act only the Vocirans would make. With no other heirs, the [[Monetarist Council]] assumed control of the nation until Neema came of age. For much of her early life, she was a symbol rather than a sovereign, tutored in isolation while the council vied for control. When she turned sixteen, she reclaimed her throne — not by force, but by command of her birthright and the support of the common people.

Her boldest act as Sultana was severing trade with the [[Vociterra|Vociran Empire]], citing ethical and political concerns. This angered the [[Monetarist Council]] and major merchant families, but gained her the devotion of the disenfranchised. Her elevation of [[Tarik Tazi]], once a bloodied champion in [[Meninos' Theater]], to General of the [[Gilded Legion]] further shocked the aristocracy but cemented her rule among the people.

## Current Status

Neema governs from the [[Royal Palace]] but is known to frequently visit districts such as the [[Shai Fields]] and the [[Ash Ward]]. Her inner circle is small, with [[Tarik Tazi]] acting as her primary protector and advisor. She continues to battle the council's resistance, playing a delicate game of balancing reforms with tradition. Tensions are high — but so is her popularity. Her future depends on whether she can outmaneuver the old guard without sacrificing her principles.
