**Title**: Secretary of the Zahir Estate

**Race**: Human

**Age**: Mid-30s

**Eyes**: Dark brown

**Attire**: Embroidered tunics and tailored coats, always fashion-forward but appropriate

**Home**: [[Providence]], [[Tarwa'kem]]

## Appearance

Khafra is the very picture of a man who knows everyone's business — and looks good doing it. With a confident smile, expressive eyes, and an easy laugh, he brings warmth to the otherwise high-strung halls of the Zahir estate. Always impeccably dressed, his style rides the line between noble and pragmatic. His every movement has intent, and his voice carries like honey through a crowded room.

## Personality

Khafra is known for his charm and insight, equally comfortable talking to nobles and stablehands. He has a natural gift for placing people where they'll thrive, and makes his work look effortless. Beneath his friendly demeanor lies a shrewd observer — someone who listens twice and speaks once, and never forgets a detail. He flirts freely but never crosses a line, and many underestimate how sharp his instincts truly are.

## Character History

Born the son of a merchant-accountant, Khafra rose quickly in administrative roles across [[Tarwa'kem]]. His sharp mind and disarming nature made him a favorite among mid-tier noble houses. When House Zahir's fortunes grew, [[Oman Zahir]] recruited him personally to help manage the complex network of trade, staff, and diplomacy that came with noble elevation. Khafra has since become the nerve center of the estate's operations — and something of a legend among the staff.

## Current Status

Khafra remains [[Oman Zahir|Oman]]'s right hand in all domestic and external affairs. With [[Laena Zahir|Laena]]'s departure and [[Monifa Zahir|Monifa]]'s worsening condition, his workload has increased, but he has not faltered. He helps maintain the illusion of normalcy and has privately assured [[Aziza]] that the staff is being kept in line. He suspects something more is going on but is smart enough not to pry — for now.
