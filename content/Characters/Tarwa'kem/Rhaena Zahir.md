**Title**: Lady of House Zahir

**Allegiance**: [[House Zahir]]

**Race**: Human

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Early 50s

**Eyes**: Brown

**Hair**: Silver-white, worn in elegant styles that reflect both grace and age-earned authority

**Attire**: Flowing noble gowns in gold and ivory, richly embroidered with subtle motifs of medicine, sunbursts, and courtly lineage; always wears layered jewelry of polished silver and pearl

**Home**: [[Providence]], [[Tarwa'kem]]

## Appearance

Rhaena Zahir is grace turned to steel. She stands tall with an effortless bearing that speaks of generations of prestige. Her silver-white hair is styled with impeccable precision, and her garments are never loud — but always perfect. Her presence in a room causes others to stand straighter, speak softer, and listen harder. Though not a queen, she carries herself like one.

## Personality

Refined and watchful, Rhaena commands influence with charm laced in subtle control. She is not ruthless, but she is formidable, skilled in the unspoken power plays of noble life. Raised in a long-established noble family, Rhaena was groomed from a young age to preserve legacies, navigate courtly politics, and elevate her station's reputation. While she cares deeply for her daughters, she holds them to impossible standards — not out of cruelty, but because she believes they must earn their place in history.

## Character History

Rhaena was born into one of the oldest noble families in [[Tarwa'kem]], wealthy and respected for generations. When she married [[Oman Zahir]], it caused a stir — he was a merchant's son from a newly risen house, ambitious but untested. Many assumed it was a political move. But Rhaena knew the truth: she married him because she admired his gentleness, his vision, and his belief that nobility could be earned, not just inherited. Over the years, their mutual respect grew into true partnership.

## Current Status

With [[Oman Zahir|Oman]] away in [[Avosa Tesota]], Rhaena manages the family's presence in [[Tarwa'kem]]. She regularly attends elite gatherings, temple events, and private audiences with key political figures.
