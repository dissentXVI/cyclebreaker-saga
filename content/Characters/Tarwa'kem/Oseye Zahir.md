**Hair**: Dark, tightly coiled, often pulled back or wrapped beneath fine scarves

**Attire**: Elegant but bold — often dark fabrics with gold detailing, styled to command attention without overstepping tradition

**Home**: [[Providence]], [[Tarwa'kem]]

## Appearance

Oseye carries herself with the confidence of someone who wants to be underestimated — so she can outshine expectations. Her fashion leans sharp and striking, reflecting her personality. She stands tall, with a noble bearing sharpened by quiet defiance. Her features favor her father's lineage: strong brows, clear eyes, and a cool, composed face that rarely gives more than she chooses.

## Personality

Clever. Observant. Underestimated. Oseye is a young woman who has lived too long in the shadows of her sisters. She resents being forgotten — not out of bitterness, but out of belief: belief that she could be more if only someone had truly seen her. While she still cares for her family, she no longer depends on their recognition for her worth. She intends to earn her own name, her own legacy — one way or another.

Oseye is not malicious, but she is calculating. She watches closely, speaks little in public, and listens to whispers in the hallways. There is a hunger in her: not to tear her family down, but to stand beside them as an equal — or above them, if it comes to that.

## Character History

Born into rising nobility, Oseye was raised with education and privilege — but not attention. As [[Laena Zahir|Laena]] was groomed for leadership and [[Monifa Zahir|Monifa]] protected as the family's treasure, Oseye was often treated as fine. Not a problem, not a priority. Even her talents — linguistics, dance, rhetoric — were acknowledged but never celebrated.

Her frustration sharpened into ambition after Laena left. To Oseye, it was abandonment. Laena left behind her duties, her betrothal, her family. And still, all anyone could talk about was her courage. Oseye saw an opportunity: if Laena would not carry the family name forward, then perhaps Oseye should — not out of loyalty, but because no one else was stepping up.

## Current Status

Still residing at the Zahir estate in [[Providence]], Oseye has begun quietly networking through informal social circles — nobles, fringe merchant heirs, and young scholars. She is particularly interested in medicine, politics, and art, but more importantly, in being seen. She has also begun attending gatherings at the [[Crown and Coin Casino]] without permission and is rumored to have made contacts in less formal corners of [[The Promenade]].

Unknown to her family, Oseye has begun compiling her own proposal to represent the Zahir name at a diplomatic function — hoping to prove her worth where Laena left a void.
