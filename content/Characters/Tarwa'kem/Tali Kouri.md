**Title**: Director of the [[Ahura Antiquarium]]; Member of the [[Monetarist Council]]

**Allegiance**: The [[Order of Ahura]]

**Race**: Dwarf

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Mid-50s

**Eyes**: Deep black, watchful and unreadable

**Hair**: Shaved sides, silver coils pulled into intricate braids beneath a silk-wrapped headdress

**Attire**: Robes of white and copper-gold trimmed with deep ochre; adorned with relic jewelry and medallions tied to archaeological expeditions

**Home**: [[Providence]], [[Tarwa'kem]]

## Appearance

Tali Kouri radiates silent authority. Though not physically imposing, her presence is enough to still a room. Her movements are minimal but precise, and her expression rarely shifts from measured neutrality. Adorned with relics that serve both symbolic and practical purposes, she is a walking archive of [[Tarwa'kem]]'s curated — and sometimes stolen — history. Her voice is quiet, her words deliberate, and her eyes miss nothing.

## Personality

Tali is enigmatic, calculating, and deeply reserved. She believes knowledge is power, but only when properly safeguarded. She avoids social politics unless required, preferring to manipulate outcomes through information, whispers, and the careful placement of ancient truths. Tali's faith in the mission of the [[Order of Ahura]] borders on spiritual — she sees herself not as a collector of artifacts, but as a steward of civilization's memory.

She has little interest in fame or fortune, and views public spectacle with intellectual disdain. Yet few council members wield as much quiet power as she does. When she speaks, it is for a purpose — her own.

## Character History

Tali rose through the ranks of the [[Order of Ahura]] not by charm or politics, but through unrelenting scholarship and fieldwork. She led expeditions through forgotten crypts, flooded tombs, and ruined strongholds across the Dust Coast before being appointed Director of the [[Ahura Antiquarium]]. Under her tenure, the museum transformed from a noble curiosity into a cultural citadel — equal parts gallery, vault, and political lever.

Her elevation to the [[Monetarist Council]] was not expected, but not contested. She rarely attends in person, instead sending exquisitely crafted notes or appearing through illusion. When she does attend, the room listens — if only to decipher her silence.

## Current Status

Tali remains cloistered within the [[Ahura Antiquarium]], overseeing the rotation of exhibits and the deployment of new expeditions across Aspiria. She is rumored to have a secret vault beneath the Antiquarium housing the most dangerous or politically sensitive relics — those too valuable or volatile to display. Her influence has only grown since Sultana [[Neema Karo'kem]] restricted the [[Order of Ahura|Order]]'s presence in Laconia.

Though her loyalties to [[Tarwa'kem]] are clear, many suspect she would sooner side with knowledge than any monarch.
