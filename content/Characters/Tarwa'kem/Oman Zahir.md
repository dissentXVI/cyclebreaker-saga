**Title**: Head of House Zahir

**Allegiance**: [[House Zahir]], the [[Hall of Order]], and his family's continued ascent

**Race**: Human

**Gender**: Male

**Age**: Late 50s

**Eyes**: Brown

**Hair**: Black, streaked with gray, always neatly combed

**Attire**: Impeccably tailored noble robes in white and gold, with measured flourishes of violet — an homage to both medical and legal tradition

**Home**: [[Providence]], [[Tarwa'kem]]

## Appearance

Oman Zahir stands tall and square-shouldered, a man of visible discipline and authority. His movements are efficient, his speech deliberate. His robes are always pristine, and every ring or chain he wears has meaning — most denote patronage, alliance, or scholarly accomplishment. Though not the most expressive man, his presence is immediately commanding, and his gaze has a weight few can endure unflinching.

## Personality

Oman is stoic, calculating, and deeply committed to duty. While not unkind, he rarely indulges in sentiment, preferring action and results over discussion and drama. He believes his family's noble rise must be justified daily — not just through wealth, but through service, education, and public integrity. His expectations for his children are immense, especially [[Laena Zahir|Laena]], in whom he sees both legacy and potential. Beneath his rigid exterior is a man who harbors immense love, but expresses it through strategy, not softness.

## Character History

Oman married into power when he secured the hand of [[Rhaena Zahir|Rhaena]] — a move that elevated the Zahir name from rising merchant to noble. It was his father, [[Omar Zahir|Omar]], who originally lifted the family through favor with the previous sultan. Oman capitalized on that legacy with methodical precision: expanding the family's influence, sponsoring clinics and medical ventures, and positioning himself as a loyal servant of [[Tytos]] and the Council.

He has long fought for a seat on the [[Monetarist Council]], but is constantly blocked — likely due to behind-the-scenes maneuvering from [[Amasis Ramses]]. This exclusion has made Oman cautious, though not bitter. He is patient, and he plays the long game.

## Current Status

Oman is currently in [[Avosa Tesota]] on business, overseeing the establishment of a cross-peninsula supply network for Zahir medical goods. Officially, the mission is about logistics and diplomacy. Unofficially, Oman is courting potential foreign allies and information brokers who may help dislodge [[Amasis Ramses|Ramses]]' grip on council influence.

## Secrets

Knows the full truth of his father [[Omar Zahir|Omar]]'s past as a professional poisoner — and has spent his adult life constructing a flawless public image to conceal it.
