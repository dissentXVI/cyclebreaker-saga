**Title**: Lord Proprietor of the West Halinor Trading Company

**Allegiance**: Independent ([[Tarwa'kem]] citizenship)

**Race**: Human

**Gender**: Male

**Age**: Late 60s

**Eyes**: Amber-brown, sharp and calculating

**Hair**: Silver-white, well-kept with a slight wave

**Attire**: Tailored travelwear reinforced with hidden defense — not a soldier, but always prepared

**Home**: [[Providence]], [[Tarwa'kem]]

## Appearance

Amasis Ramses exudes control and command, wrapped in the attire of a man who walks comfortably among kings, scholars, and assassins alike. His wardrobe is cosmopolitan — elegant coats lined with gilded thread, foreign silks, and functional travelwear tailored with absurd precision. He wears shaded lenses, not out of vanity, but to ensure no one ever sees his eyes unless he chooses it. His posture is erect, his frame lean and deliberate, every gesture calculated to project confidence and capability. Though born in the sun-carved halls of [[Tarwa'kem]], his style speaks of roads walked all over Halinor and beyond.

## Personality

Ruthless. Controlled. Unspeakably shrewd. Amasis is a master of diplomacy by omission, flattery by silence, and warfare by economics. He rarely raises his voice, for he has learned long ago that the right whisper can collapse empires. He believes loyalty is a currency more volatile than gold, and he spends both wisely. To his allies, he is a generous benefactor; to his rivals, a storm cloaked in civility. There are few games he hasn't already won.

## Character History

Born to a modest family in [[Providence]], Amasis was never meant for power. But he understood early that gold opens more doors than birthright ever could. Through shrewd deals, silenced enemies, and leveraged secrets, he founded the West Halinor Trading Company, transforming it into one of the most influential trade networks in Aspiria. His ships sail from Gholmeshi to Aqualonde, and his warehouses span cities that pretend not to need him. He is rumored to fund multiple political factions — sometimes opposing ones — always hedging his bets.

It is whispered in certain circles that he played a hand in keeping the [[Zahir family|Zahir]] family off the [[Monetarist Council]] — though no evidence has ever surfaced.

## Current Status

Amasis resides in [[Providence]], though he's rarely home. As a seated member of the [[Monetarist Council]], his voice carries considerable weight across Halinor. His influence shapes tariffs, trade routes, and foreign policy — all without ever raising his voice. Despite his age, he shows no signs of stepping down or slowing, only consolidating.

It's clear he is making several moves against [[Ngor Azizi]] and his businesses.

## Combat Style

Amasis avoids direct combat at all costs. But when cornered, he employs enchanted jewelry, concealed devices, and hired protectors — often before his attacker even realizes a confrontation has begun. His true weapon is information: he is known to have ended more threats with a document than a dagger.

## Notable Relationships

[[Neema Karo'kem]] – Formal but distant. Amasis respects her ideals but finds her unity ambitions naive.

[[Ngor Azizi]] – Longstanding rival on the [[Monetarist Council]]. Azizi is bold, brash, and openly ambitious; Ramses is subtler, preferring quiet finesse to force. Their games often clash in public and private arenas alike.
