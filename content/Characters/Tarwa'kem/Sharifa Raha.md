**Title**: Monetarist Councilor; Owner of the [[Crown and Coin Casino]]

**Allegiance**: Herself, the [[Monetarist Council]]

**Race**: Human

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Late 40s

**Eyes**: Brown

**Hair**: Cropped short and perfectly styled, often adorned with gold pins or gemstone accents

**Attire**: Ornate and fashion-forward — layered black and gold gowns with asymmetrical cuts, gemstones, and embroidery that signal wealth without apology

**Home**: [[Providence]], [[Tarwa'kem]]

## Appearance

Sharifa is the embodiment of high [[Tarwa'kem]] glamor — dressed in sleek silhouettes and gilded accents, she walks like she owns every room she enters because, in some way, she probably does. Her sharp, angular features are always composed beneath a practiced smile. She accessorizes with intention: each earring, necklace, and ring sends a message, whether of status, mood, or manipulation.

## Personality

Magnetic. Ruthless. Brilliant. Sharifa Raha is a master of political maneuvering through charm rather than brute force. She is fluent in the language of power — coin, rumor, beauty, and spectacle — and wields each like a weapon. She is loyal to [[Tarwa'kem]], but her vision of loyalty includes grandeur, indulgence, and an iron grip on influence.

She enjoys being underestimated by conservative peers, only to leave them dazzled or outmaneuvered. She can compliment you with one hand and bankrupt you with the other, all while making you thank her for the lesson.

## Character History

Born to a merchant family that clawed its way into the aristocracy through trade and spectacle, Sharifa took the family legacy further than anyone expected. She transformed the [[Crown and Coin Casino]] into more than a den of wealth — it became the nexus of power in [[Tarwa'kem]]. There, she built her network: nobles, foreign diplomats, monetarists, priests, gladiators, and entertainers all passed through her domain. Many left owing her something.

When she purchased a seat on the [[Monetarist Council]], few were surprised — but many were outraged. She has used that platform to champion elite interests, expand trade control, and subtly redefine Tarwa'kem's luxury culture as a form of patriotic pride.

## Current Status

Sharifa is thriving. With her casino now a political and social hub, she effectively holds court nightly beneath stained glass and illusions. She is involved in council debates around economic reform, border trade, and the role of aristocracy in public life — all while overseeing extravagant themed galas that draw the eyes and wallets of the entire city. She watches Sultana [[Neema Karo'kem]] closely — not yet an enemy, but a disruptor — and has begun making quiet moves to insulate herself if tides turn.
