**Title**: Lady's Maid to Laena Zahir

**Allegiance**: [[House Zahir]]

**Race**: Human

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Early 20s

**Eyes**: Hazel

**Hair**: Wavy dark brown, usually tied back or adorned with gold pins

**Attire**: Practical but refined servant's robes with personal touches — embroidered hems, gold-accented jewelry, and a sash gifted by Laena

**Home**: [[Providence]], [[Tarwa'kem]]

## Appearance

Sera has a natural confidence that shines beneath her station. She carries herself with alert poise, always polished, always present — but never invisible. Her olive-toned skin is marked with faint freckles, and her sharp hazel eyes miss very little. While her uniform speaks of duty, her personal style hints at independence — subtle jewelry, clever layering, and a knowing smile that makes nobles underestimate her at their peril.

## Personality

Loyal. Clever. Grounded. Sera is a woman who knows exactly where she stands in the hierarchy — and how to move within it. She is devoted to [[Laena Zahir|Laena]], but not blindly so; her loyalty is tempered by realism, discretion, and quiet strength. She is observant, with a sharp memory and a talent for subtle manipulation when needed. Sera speaks softly, listens always, and knows how to keep a secret longer than most nobles have titles.

## Character History

Sera was born into a merchant household with moderate means. When her family struck a deal with [[Omar Zahir]], part of the arrangement included Sera entering service to the noble house — specifically as [[Laena Zahir|Laena]]'s attendant. The two girls were close in age and, over time, grew into an unusually tight bond. Sera became Laena's shadow, confidante, and quiet co-conspirator, trusted with thoughts and plans Laena never shared with her family.

Though her role is "just a servant," Sera holds more influence in the Zahir household than most realize. She moves between classes with ease, respected by staff and acknowledged — if grudgingly — by nobility. If something happens in the household, Sera either saw it, heard it, or already has a plan to manage it.

## Current Status

She remains watchful for any letters or signs from [[Laena Zahir|Laena]] and has begun gently probing the city for information on behalf of Laena and her companions.

## Notable Relationships

[[Laena Zahir]] – Fiercely loyal. Sera knows her rhythms, moods, and secrets better than anyone. She would follow her into danger, but also knows when to protect her from herself.

[[Oseye Zahir]] – Cordial but cautious. Oseye sees Sera as "Laena's girl," though Sera suspects Oseye is watching her more closely than usual now.

## Secrets

Occasionally pretends to pray to [[Tytos]], but her true belief may lie elsewhere — possibly with [[Haelaxa]], goddess of trickery and subtlety.

Writes fictional stories in secret — romantic epics loosely based on the nobles around her. [[Laena Zahir|Laena]] was the only one who ever knew.
