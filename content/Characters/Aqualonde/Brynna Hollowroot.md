**Title**: Hollow Seer of [[The Earthspeakers Circle|the Earthspeakers]]

**Allegiance**: [[The Earthspeakers Circle]] ([[Aqualonde]])

**Race**: Elf

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Appears early 30s

**Eyes**: One moss-green, one milky white

**Hair**: Tangled dark brown, woven with roots and pale blossoms

**Attire**: Bark-woven shawl, moss-draped robes, bare feet stained with soil

**Home:** [[The Rootwell]], an underground grove beneath [[Aqualonde]]’s [[The Elder Tree|Elder Tree]]

## Appearance

Brynna looks like she stepped out of a forgotten myth. Her hair is thick and uncombed, shot through with thin roots and forest blossoms that seem to grow directly from her scalp. Her skin is bark-pale in some places, earthen-dark in others, with faint fractal lines like living cracks running beneath the surface.

She wears a robe of living moss and mycelium threads that shift subtly with mood. Her eyes rarely focus the same way twice—one sees the world as it is, the other as it was or might become.
When she speaks, her voice sounds like soil being turned.
## Personality

Unnerving. Profound. Unfiltered. Brynna is less a person and more a vessel—so say those who’ve spent time near her. She is deeply connected to the pulse of the land and often speaks in riddles, dreams, or memories that are not hers. Conversations with her can feel like arguments with time itself.

Despite this, she is kind in a primal, unsettling way—more forest than friend. She comforts the grieving, blesses births, and occasionally vanishes for days only to return with cryptic warnings no one understands until it’s too late.

Though most avoid her company for long, her words carry weight. Especially when the roots begin to stir.
## Character History

No one is certain where Brynna was born. She was found by [[The Earthspeakers Circle|the Earthspeakers]] as a child, cradled in the hollow of a tree. From a young age, she demonstrated the ability to commune with spirits others could not see. When asked about her past, she gives different answers: “I was born from a buried name,” “The forest made me,” or “I was a lie that blossomed.”

Initiated into the Earthspeakers, she rejected most rites but remained revered—especially after predicting the collapse of the Elder Branch during [[the vineblight plague]]. Her visions are never requested; they simply arrive.

She refuses leadership, but [[The Earthspeakers Circle|the Earthspeakers]] often defers to her anyway.
## Current Status

Brynna has grown increasingly agitated in recent months. She has warned of something unrooting beneath [[the Chilmire Bog]]—“a hunger that sings”—and has begun carving runes into trees to “ward the memories.”

She has also taken a strange interest in [[Aspiria’s Chosen]], calling them “the ones we forgot to dream.” She appeared uninvited in one of their chambers and uttered a single phrase before vanishing: “There is a song at the edge of silence. When it finishes, nothing will want to be.”

She is currently believed to be living beneath [[The Rootwell]], trying to commune with the fae. She’s been unsuccessful so far.
