**Title**: Mistress of the Trade Winds

**Allegiance**: [[The Windriders Guild]] ([[Aqualonde]])

**Race**: Elf

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Appears late 30s

**Eyes**: Sea-glass green

**Hair**: Sandy blonde, braided

**Attire**: Tailored traveler’s coat stitched with route runes, wind-etched jewelry, and a high-collared diplomat’s mantle

**Home:** [[The Zephyr Exchange]], [[Aqualonde]]

## Appearance

Tirenne is sleek, swift, and sharply composed. Her clothing straddles utility and elegance: light, windproof, and enchanted for long travel. Her movements are efficient, her posture alert—she carries herself like a windblade: graceful, but always ready to cut. The small, swirling tattoos behind her ears mark her as a Skybound—those licensed to speak to wind spirits.

She wears a series of rings linked by silver chain around her gloved right hand, a symbolic bond to the contracts she’s sworn.
## Personality

Tirenne is disciplined, perceptive, and resolutely practical. Among [[Aqualonde]]’s idealists and mystics, she stands as a firm advocate for outreach and preparation. She values caution, but not hesitation; diplomacy, but not appeasement. Her tone is often cool and clipped, but never cruel—every word is weighed like a ledger entry.

She believes [[Aqualonde]] must engage with the wider world or risk fading into it. Her vision is not of conquest or wealth, but of influence—and protection. She’s seen what happens to isolationists when the tide turns.

Tirenne also keeps meticulous personal notes and has a remarkable memory for voices and phrases. She has, on occasion, recited treaties verbatim—out of spite.
## Character History

Born to river-wardens in the southern wetlands, Tirenne came of age ferrying messages and goods between [[Aqualonde]]’s scattered settlements. She joined [[The Windriders Guild|the Windriders]] as a young adult, specializing in navigation through unpredictable routes—coastal roads, misted deltas, cliffside trails once mapped only by spirit-song.

She rose quickly through the ranks, becoming Mistress of the Trade Winds at just over sixty years of age—a youthful age among elves. Her ability to speak with elemental spirits of wind and storm earned her praise, but it was her command of contract law and negotiation that made her indispensable.

She studied language-encoding under [[Maevienne Narelith]] and later trained briefly in [[Shurevaire]], mastering their diplomatic customs and passive-aggressive etiquette.
## Current Status

Within [[Aqualonde]], she has expressed growing concern about the Emerald Ledger—[[Aqualonde]]’s sacred trade records—which may have been subtly altered. She suspects internal manipulation and is investigating discreetly.

Though she supports [[Maylin Lurel]], she has urged the Seedspeaker to more openly align with [[Neema Karo'kem]]’s proposed alliance—a position that has stirred debate among the guilds.
