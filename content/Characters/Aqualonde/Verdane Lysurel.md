**Title**: Green Hand of [[The Tree Shapers Guild|the Tree Shapers]]

**Allegiance**: [[The Tree Shapers Guild]] ([[Aqualonde]])

**Race**: Elf

**Gender**: Male

**Age**: Appears late 60s 

**Eyes**: Pale green with flecks of amber

**Hair**: Long, silvery-green, worn loosely bound with ivy thread

**Attire**: Moss-dyed robes, bark-patterned sashes, carved wood-bead cords woven into his sleeves

**Home:** [[The Boughspire]], [[Aqualonde]]

## Appearance

Verdane Lysurel is the image of stillness made flesh. His tall frame is slightly stooped from decades bent in reverence to roots and growth. His arms are covered in silver bark-like tattoos—not inked, but grown—a result of sacred communion with the Arborium Rite. His fingers are perpetually stained green from tending sprouts, and leaves sometimes sprout briefly from his robes as if the forest recognizes him as one of its own.
He smells faintly of damp earth, pine sap, and blooming magnolia.
## Personality

Soft-spoken, contemplative, and impossibly patient, Verdane speaks as though every word matters—and often, it does. He is slow to anger, but slower still to forget. His mind is a forest of quiet thoughts, roots of memory winding deep into [[Aqualonde]]’s oldest decisions.

He is known for long silences, for letting others speak themselves into their own clarity, or their own folly. He rarely offers direct answers—but his guidance, when it comes, often proves prophetic. He has a dry sense of humor, shared rarely but warmly.

To the young, he is archaic. To the wise, he is indispensable.
## Character History

Verdane was born during the closing decades of the Sixth Radiant Age, the son of a quiet woodturner and a mid-ranking member of [[The Earthspeakers Circle|the Earthspeakers]]. From an early age, he showed a rare affinity not for the glamour of nature magic, but for its patience. He was often found alone beneath trees, listening, carving, or planting, while others sparred or sang.

He joined [[The Tree Shapers Guild|the Tree Shapers]] in his forties and rose slowly—not through ambition, but through unwavering dedication. While others mastered techniques, Verdane mastered time. His bonsai creations span entire generations. 
## Current Status

Verdane remains in [[The Boughspire]], overseeing the sacred bonsai galleries and deep-tree stabilizations that prevent [[Aqualonde]]’s forest-city from overrunning itself. He consults rarely—but when [[Maylin Lurel|Maylin]] or [[Brynna Hollowroot|Brynna]] call upon him, he listens.
