**Race**: True Elf (Navarresean)

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Unknown — a senior member of the Blessed during Amastacia's reign; has been kept artificially alive for over two thousand years since

**Eyes**: Blue — clear and watchful, the eyes of someone who has not stopped paying attention

**Hair**: White-blonde, worn loose past her shoulders

**Attire**: Dark scholar's robes with gold embroidered neckline — the dress of the Blessed, worn with the quiet practicality of someone who chose it for function rather than display. Small gold earrings.

**Home:** [[the Cosmic Pillar]] (formerly); currently [[Laconia]]

## Appearance

Alyndra looks like someone who has been waiting a long time and has not entirely given up. White-blonde hair, blue eyes, freckles across her nose and cheeks — an approachable face that her expression quietly contradicts. She is measured rather than warm, watchful rather than suspicious. The kind of person who listens to what you say and also to what you don't. Her robes are unadorned compared to more status-conscious members of the Blessed. She never needed the performance.

What she looks like after two thousand years suspended in one of [[Thalaniel Shamashur|Shamashur]]'s Life pneuma machines is a question the party has already had to reckon with.

## Personality

Alyndra was a woman of principle operating inside a system that had stopped rewarding principle. Her journal — hidden within [[the Cosmic Pillar]] and recovered by [[Aspiria's Chosen]] — is one of the few surviving first-person accounts of the empire's final years from inside the Blessed, and it reads as the work of someone who had not yet given up but could see the moment coming when she would have to.

She was not naive. She understood the Inquest, used her Damned as invisible agents precisely because they moved unseen through the tower, and took care to hide her writing behind an enchantment she believed would go undiscovered by investigators focused on their "manifest destiny." But she also walked directly into the Empress's presence with accusations against [[Thalaniel Shamashur]] — knowing, on some level, what the absence of surprise on Amastacia's face would mean.

Her core conviction was that Life and Law — the empire's two highest elements — had been abandoned in everything but name. She believed that any victory won through Shamashur's methods would be hollow. She was right. She did not get to see it.

What two thousand years of suspended consciousness, Life pneuma continuously drained and re-injected, has done to her mind is a question without a clean answer. Whether she is the same person who wrote that journal — whether anyone could be — remains to be seen.

## Narrative Relevance

Alyndra is alive. She was kept in one of [[Thalaniel Shamashur|Shamashur]]'s Life pneuma machines within [[the Cosmic Pillar]] for over two thousand years — not as a subject of mercy, but as a resource. Her journal was found in the tower by [[Aspiria's Chosen]], written in the expectation that she would fail and someone else would have to carry what she knew.

She did not fail. She was simply taken before she could finish.

She is currently in [[Laconia]], having been removed from the tower before [[Reyn'lael Joran]] took the throne and vanished with it. What she knows about the final years of the [[Navarresean See]], about Shamashur's work, about what Amastacia was really trying to do — and what she has been aware of, or dreaming of, or mercifully unconscious of, for two millennia — makes her one of the most significant living sources of Navarresean history in Aspiria.
