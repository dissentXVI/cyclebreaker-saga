## Appearance

Laena is tall and slim with a golden dark skin tone and white wavy hair. She carries herself with confidence and almost entitlement as she was raised to believe she belongs anywhere and everywhere. She stands tall and speaks loudly. As a default, she will be looking good and smelling good, adorning herself with a variety of jewels and perfumes. Due to the desert climate, she can often be found in loose, flowing materials made of silk or linen.

## Personality

Laena is a confident first born child who is used to speaking with authority and being obeyed. She worships [[Tytos]] and believes in law and order. Noblesse Oblige and the Hippocratic Oath are guiding philosophies for her. She  believes society works pretty well when everyone is following the rules. While she is confident, she also has both an innate and trained ability to be diplomatic and smooth over ruffled feathers. 

She can be naive about the world and is not particularly street smart, although she is traditionally educated. 

Over the course of our adventures, Laena has developed an appreciation for the fineries that were just a given at home. She will indulge herself where applicable, but this has also made her have a tight reign over the group’s purse strings. 

She believes everyone deserves the basics in life (medicine, food, shelter, clothes), but that anything beyond that you have to earn through hard work. If you told her she’s inheriting her wealth, she’d say yeah but I work hard to keep it running, that kind of thing.
## Character History

Laena Zahir (meaning: to manifest) is the first born daughter to her parents [[Oman Zahir|Oman]] and [[Rhaena Zahir]], living and ruling in [[Tarwa'kem]]. Laena has two younger sisters, [[Oseye Zahir|Oseye]], and [[Monifa Zahir|Monifa]]. Laena is 21. Oseye is a late teens and Monifa is 12. Laena is the heir to the Zahir noble title, lands, and responsibilities and takes them very seriously. It was Laena’s grandfather, [[Omar Zahir|Omar]], that lifted them from a merchant class, selling both magical and mundane medical cures, to the title of nobility

Her father [[Oman Zahir|Oman]] married well outside his league to [[Rhaena Zahir|Rhaena]] who belongs to an extremely wealthy and long titled family, thereby increasing the Zahir line’s prestige. 

Which leads to Laena. As the presumed heir, she has been given lessons in many subjects from economics, art, science, math, literature, agriculture, and even warfare. She speaks Dwarven and plays cards for fun. 

She has always been extremely dedicated and loyal to her family. With her parent’s encouragement, she decided to train under the temple of [[Tytos]] and gained the order domain. As far as Laena can see, society works pretty well with people in their proper place and when they follow the laws and justice of the land and of [[Tytos]]. This inspired her to train there. They reinforced these ideals. 
## Current Status

Newly single and un-fianced. Currently traveling with Aspira’s Chosen AKA FLAG on the road from [[Aqualonde]] to [[Avosa Tesota]]. The group is on a diplomatic mission to unite the cities and regions under a military alliance against the Vociran.
## Combat Style

Lanea receives her spellcasting abilities from the graciousness of [[Tytos]]. As a cleric, Laena’s primary goal in combat is to keep everyone alive, most especially herself. If the healer’s down, that’s very bad. Sometimes her self preservation comes at the cost of lives, like it did in Laconia. 

On the offense, Laena has a handful of spells she prefers, guiding bolt, blindness/deafness, spirit guardians, and inflict wounds are almost always prepared for the day. 

One thing for sure, she is paying attention to every person on the battlefield, and tries to keep her distance where possible.
