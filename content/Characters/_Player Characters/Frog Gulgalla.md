**Race**: Human

**Age**: 47

**Eyes**: Grey, flecked with hazel and green

**Hair**: Long, black, and disheveled with grey streaks

**Attire**: Light armor fitted with dark furs and moss with a thick cloak and hood of the same, optimal camouflage for the Frostmarch Forest

**Home:** [[Frostmarch Forest]], though raised in a small fishing village south of [[Crowtown]] on the shore of the former [[Sticaea Lake]]

## Appearance
When Frog started this adventure, she was a frightening sight to behold. Her long dark hair was wild and matted. She deeply shadowed her eyes to conceal her features and appear more intimidating. Being taller for a human woman, her skulking posture did little to diminish her forceful strides. With her long fur cloak, concealing a massive arbalest, and clink of gear, to her adversaries or a passerby with a curious eye, she could be a hag risen from the depths of a murky swamp. No one to invite conversation, certainly. Time traveling in society has cleaned her skin, straightened her posture, and smoothed her tangles.
## Personality
Though she grew to be brash and cocky as a feared mercenary, exile has transformed Frog into a cooler, more reflective person. Sometimes she does not speak simply as a matter of having been out of practice, willing to let the eagerness of youth (her young traveling party) make up for her taciturnity and patience. When she does speak, it is laconic and to the point. Frog’s phlegmatic demeanor, experience, and yes, even regrets, make her the voice of wisdom in a group of adventurous youngsters. Her wisdom does not extend to matters of money, however….
## Character History
Frog was born Jewel Siltborn in a now abandoned fishing village called East Reedwash a day’s travel south of Crowtown on the eastern shore of the great Sticaean lake. Not wanting to get tied down in a backwater with the child of an often absent, and occasional layabout drunk, fisherman, her mother fled the village after Frog was born. Frog’s father taught her how to fish and use a crossbow at an early age – which gave her something to do and practice in all the endless days she spent in East Reedwash – when he wasn’t away on a fishing boat, but it was her wise and caring maternal grandmother, Tadami, that raised her.

When Frog was old enough to look after herself, she took after her mother and skipped town, taking up odd jobs in Crowtown for a year or more. Her vision of the world expanded. She changed her name to Quiss de Barghest to leave her boring life behind, a name that seemed more exotic for the world she was now in. One day when working as a maid, she defended a friend from sexual abuse by a traveling sellsword, holding her own against the man until more of his group joined in. That’s when she met Sharl van der Cleev, who joined her side in the fracas after seeing her mettle and spirit. Frog was instantly smitten and joined him as a mercenary.

For many years she traveled with Sharl as a duo mercenary band. Sharl drew her out of her shell for her personality to match her chosen cognomen, refined her fighting skills, and soon they were a power couple on the soldier of fortune scene. The two grew especially close as they came to rely on each other on the battlefield. In time, Sharl’s charisma attracted more talented warriors and refugees into their fold until they were a formidable brigade known as the Crimson Cleavers. Following Sharl’s meeting and recruitment of Chauni Stag-Eye on a mission and his subsequent conversion to her Meninos cult, the Cleavers made a turn toward greater brutality and ruthlessness, which was eventually true of Frog individually as well. Upon discovery of her pregnancy from Sharl, Frog had a sudden and irrevocable change of heart about who she and Sharl had become. 

She fled the Cleavers forever that day. Perhaps by luck or fate, she was discovered by the Midnight Revel, a traveling band of Petra worshippers, who disguised her and took her in while her pregnancy came to term. While traveling with the Petra priestesses, she took the name Argenta in their order. Frog delivered her daughter Tadami while the Revel were in Quagton. From there, she left her daughter in their care – with knowledge of the orphanage Tadami would be raised in – and took on the name Frog, as she started her life in exile in the deep Frostmarch Forest.

The dangers and challenges of exiled life in the cold forest were unexpected and numerous. For years she lived in an abandoned fishing hut north of the forest on the shore of the Silvery Expanse. Eventually she was discovered by an elder half-elf ranger, an exile himself, who taught her true ranger skills, natural magics and the way of nature. He passed after some years. Later, she was frequently commissioned by Carinthian Inquisition knights to do reconnaissance and take part in guerilla attacks and kidnappings against a dragon-worshipping cult that operated in the woods. It was at this time she learned Draconic and earned the name Gullgalla from the cult. 

News travels slowly through uncivilized lands, and Frog infrequently visited Quagton or logging camps, so it was not until years after the fact she by chance heard of the cataclysm that drained the Great Sticaean Lake, reducing shoreside settlements to ghost towns and ruins. She left immediately to assure the safety of her daughter, traveling discreetly to the bustling hub town of Laconia for clues to Tadami’s whereabouts.
## Current Status
Though she has finally confronted and killed [[Sharl van der Cleev|Sharl]], and what remained of her old mercenary brigade, Frog continues her adventures to prevent Cataclysms and halt the advance of the Vociran Empire in the company of her new boon companions [[Gothmog Gansukh|Gothmog]], [[Laena Zahir|Laena]], and [[Ariesa Hecate|Ariesa]], collectively known as [[Aspiria’s Chosen]]. One of the chief threats to her daughter is gone, but her whereabouts are still unknown to Frog. She now travels to [[Avosa Tesota]] to secure that nation’s contribution to [[Tarwa'kem]]’s war effort.
## Combat Style
Marksmanship and range have defined Frog’s fighting style for as long as she could hold a crossbow. A gifted sniper, Frog prefers to blitz the opposition preemptively with a deadly volley of precision strikes from great range, while hidden if possible, with her large arbalest, Thorn, capable of piercing even heavy armor. She is not afraid of mixing it up in melee if the situation calls for it and has trained in swordsmanship. Her nimbleness, athleticism, and the judicious trap allow her to escape most pinches she finds herself in. Most magic she uses in combat accentuates her mobile ranged style, but meager healing is not out of her repertoire in an emergency.
