**Title**: Congresswoman of the Crimson Tide; Captain of *The Siren’s Smile*  

**Allegiance**: [[Avosa Tesota]]

**Race**: Human

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Late 30s

**Eyes**: Light brown

**Hair**: Dark brown streaked with copper, worn loose with braids tied in coins and coral
 
**Attire**: Black naval coat trimmed in crimson, gold earrings, leather corset and weathered tricorn hat with a ruby pin

**Home:** [[The Pearlsteps]], [[Avosa Tesota]]

## Appearance

Solana Varros commands attention like a storm breaking over the bay. Her sun-bronzed skin bears the faint silver scars of a hundred duels, each worn proudly. Her coat—dark, frayed, and heavy with medals she never earned legally—swings like a banner of defiance. 

She wears her wealth the way others wear armor: gold coins braided into her hair, coral beads wrapped around her wrists, and a shark-tooth pendant said to have belonged to her first victim. Her grin is the kind that makes rivals nervous and admirers reckless.

## Personality

Solana is a born rebel—a pirate with a stateswoman’s polish. She speaks like a poet and drinks like a brawler, weaving charisma and cruelty in equal measure.  

She believes [[Avosa Tesota|Avosa]] has grown soft under the Admiral’s idealism. To her, the city’s strength was forged on salt and plunder, not charters and elections. She preaches that the old ways of piracy—bold, untamed, and unapologetic—are the truest form of freedom.  

Yet beneath her bravado lies cunning and calculation. Solana knows the power of spectacle and how to stoke nostalgia into fervor. She plays to the crowd’s hunger for danger and pride, offering them the dream of wild seas instead of moral restraint.

## Character History

Born to a dockworker’s family in the lower quarter of Crescent Port, Solana ran away at fourteen and joined a pirate crew known as **The Crimson Tide**. She rose quickly through mutiny, charisma, and ruthless efficiency, eventually claiming her own ship—*The Siren’s Smile*—after slaying her former captain in single combat.  

She built her legend raiding Vociran and Eastern ships during her early career, becoming infamous across [[Halinor]]. When [[Corella Marante|Admiral Marante]] rose to power and outlawed privateering under the new **Charter of Dignity**, Solana refused to bow. Instead, she turned her notoriety into influence, secured a Fleet Captain seat in the Congress of Captains through her command of The Siren’s Smile.

## Current Status

With **[[Corella Marante|Admiral Marante]]’s re-election** looming, Solana has become their fiercest rival. Her platform—“Freedom Without Chains”—has struck a chord with captains weary of tithes, laws, and diplomacy.

She has been **courting[[ Santigo del Sol]]**, lavishing him with attention and indulgence: private concerts, extravagant dinners, and parties where his name lights up banners of flame. She intends to secure his endorsement and use his voice to drown out Marante’s message.

While she presents herself as the pirate queen of nostalgia, she secretly funds smuggling operations through **[[The Red Net]]** and has ties to [[Serpiosa]]’s black markets—though she publicly denounces the recent rumors of slave trafficking as “a false news.”  

Her charisma and ruthlessness have made her the greatest threat to [[Avosa Tesota|Avosa]]’s democratic experiment since its founding.

## Combat Style

Solana is a brutal and theatrical duelist, favoring a curved saber named **Bloodwake** and a flintlock pistol engraved with the motto *“Mercy Drowns.”* She fights with showy, overwhelming precision—taunting her enemies mid-strike and disarming them both literally and emotionally.  

Though she leads from the front, she’s not reckless; every duel, every brawl, every drunken tavern brawl she “accidentally” starts is calculated to fuel her legend.

## Notable Relationships

- **[[Corella Marante |Admiral Corella Marante]] (they/them)** – Once allies in Avosa’s reform years, now bitter political rivals. They represent opposite ideals: Marante’s structured freedom versus Solana’s anarchic liberty.  
- **[[Santigo del Sol]]** – The key to her victory. She’s spent months charming him through lavish parties, flattering praise, and endless indulgence. Whether out of desire or calculation, her fascination with him borders on obsession.  
- **[[Isaacs Cabres]]** – Despises her. He sees her as a populist demagogue who threatens Avosa’s stability. She calls him “The Goat Clerk.”  
- **[[Reyes Lobo]]** – The warden who once hunted her crew. Their enmity is bitter and personal; she mocks him in her speeches as “the jungle’s pet.”  
- **[[The Red Net]]** – Operates under her tacit approval. While she denies involvement, many of its smugglers owe her their livelihoods.

## Secrets

**Unlawful Funding:** Solana’s campaign is partially financed by Red Net smuggling profits. She conceals this through falsified ship registries and falsified cargo manifests.
