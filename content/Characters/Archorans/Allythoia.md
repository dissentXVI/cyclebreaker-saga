## Appearance

Allythoia is a figure of deliberate concealment. A deep black hood shrouds her head entirely, and her eyes are hidden behind a blindfold mask of dark gold filigree — ornate and unsettling, suggesting not blindness but a different kind of sight. Beneath the cloak she wears black armor engraved with intricate scrollwork, the gold detailing catching light like embers. Her lips are the only skin visible — composed, faintly curved, never hurried.

## Personality

Patient, precise, and quietly ruthless. Allythoia does not force outcomes — she creates conditions. She identifies what a person most desperately wants and offers it to them, knowing exactly how the gift will land. Her manner is calm, even kind. The cruelty is entirely in the result.

## Narrative Relevance

Allythoia's most consequential known act was her intervention in the final years of the [[Navarresean See|Navarresean Empire]]. She approached [[Thalaniel Shamashur]] and assisted him in solving the problem of cloning — specifically, the cloning of the dying Empress [[Amastacia Lael'Nathor]], who had spent her final years consumed by a terror of death. The clone was successfully produced. It was a grotesque mockery of the woman it was meant to replicate.

Allythoia then brought it before [[Thallan Lael'Nathor II]] — the grieving Emperor Consort who had been waiting, hoping, for his mother's return.

He was sickened by what he saw. Convinced that death was inescapable and life therefore meaningless, Thallan II turned the power of [[The Cosmic Pillar]] upon the world and brought down the [[The Tempest of Madness|Tempest of Madness]], ending the Navarresean Empire entirely.

> [!dm] DM Only — Not Revealed to Players
> The cloning was never meant to succeed cleanly. Allythoia's intervention was deliberate and precisely calibrated — she did not help Shamashur out of curiosity or generosity, but to ensure the clone would be exactly wrong enough to destroy Thallan II's hope rather than restore it. The Tempest of Madness was the intended outcome. It was an Archoran operation from the beginning, and Allythoia was its instrument.
