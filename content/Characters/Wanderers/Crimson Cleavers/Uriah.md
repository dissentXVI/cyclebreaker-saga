**Title**: Twin Fang of the Crimson Cleavers

**Allegiance**: [[Crimson Cleavers]] (dissolved)

**Race**: Elf

**Gender**: Male

**Age**: Appears late 20s

**Eyes**: Teal

**Hair**: Dirty blonde, tousled but styled with intent

**Attire**: Aqualondian duelist leathers, curved blades, embroidered green-and-gold accents

**Home**: Formerly [[Aqualonde]]; whereabouts unknown

## Appearance

Uriah is everything elegance fears: too sharp, too lean, too practiced. His turquoise eyes gleam with mischief and method. His hair falls in casual waves, more art than disorder. He dresses like a nobleman's disappointment — fine green and brown dueling gear, detailed with subtle gold Aqualondian patterns, reinforced for speed and flourish rather than durability.

His voice is smooth as oil and twice as slick. He rarely smiles without showing teeth.

## Personality

Uriah is charm dipped in poison — clever, eloquent, and always testing the emotional temperature of those around him. While [[Ariel]] is the outwardly cruel twin, Uriah is the dissection: precise, observant, and almost gentle as he gets beneath someone's armor — emotionally or otherwise.

He enjoys theatrics, masks, and whispered secrets. But beneath the performance lies a cold hunger — not for blood, but for control. People fascinate him, especially as they fall apart.

He and [[Ariel]] speak often in gestures and glances. When they talk aloud, it's usually for someone else's benefit.

## Character History

Uriah and his twin [[Ariel]] were once beloved prodigies of a powerful noble house in [[Aqualonde]] — wealthy, radiant, and scandal-ridden. Their pleasures became twisted. Their politics became dangerous. And when one too many rivals disappeared during a party they hosted, the twins were exiled, names stripped from the registries.

Where others saw disgrace, [[Sharl van der Cleev|Sharl]] saw utility. He gave them what they truly wanted: license. In the [[Crimson Cleavers]], they found a stage — one drenched in blood and painted with screams.

They've never looked back.

## Current Status

Uriah escaped the final confrontation at [[Sharl van der Cleev|Sharl]]'s Fauconwood stronghold. [[Ariel]] did not.

He has never been separated from his twin before. What Uriah is now — without the organization, without Sharl, without the other half of the only symmetry he ever trusted — is an open question. The Crimson Cleavers are gone. [[Ariel]] is dead. Whether grief makes him more dangerous or simply unmoored is unknown.

His whereabouts are currently unknown.

## Combat Style

Uriah is a rogue through and through — a dancer with daggers, a ghost with a grin. Where [[Ariel]] overwhelms, Uriah unravels. He uses illusion, misdirection, and off-angle strikes to bait defenses and gut soft spots. Every movement is deliberate and efficient, often fighting back-to-back with his sister in mirrored choreography.

Without her, his combat style has a half that no longer exists.

## Notable Relationships

**[[Ariel]]** – His twin. His soulmate. The only symmetry he ever truly had. She is dead, and he escaped without her.

**[[Sharl van der Cleev]]** – Dead. The man Uriah worshipped with a child's wonder and a conspirator's clarity.

**[[Dyna Dee]]** – The other survivor. Whether they find each other, and what they do if they do, is yet to be seen.
