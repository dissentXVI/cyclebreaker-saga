## Appearance
Molko Beambearer is a living mountain of muscle and scar tissue. His ashen white skin is crisscrossed with pale red marks — faded wounds, burns, and ritual cuts — each hinting at some brutal story long buried. His face is expressionless, his mouth tight, eyes heavy-lidded and rimmed in a permanent red glaze. He wears no shirt, only thick leather wrappings around his wrists and a heavy fur belt with a broken ram's skull as the buckle.

Molko towers above even other orcs. Despite his size, his movements are unsettlingly quiet — slow, deliberate, aware.

## Personality
Molko does not speak. Or if he ever did, no one remembers hearing it. He communicates through glances, gestures, and silence thick enough to stop a conversation. He shows no signs of rage, joy, or fear — only focus. Whether in battle or in stillness, Molko radiates quiet patience and latent destruction.

He answered only to [[Sharl van der Cleev|Sharl]]. Most of the Cleavers viewed him with awe or nervous tolerance. To [[Dyna Dee]], he was a pet. To [[Chauni Stag-Eye]], a sacred beast. To Sharl, a promise kept in flesh.

## Character History
Molko's origin is a mystery. Some say he was born into a [[Tatarr'kha]] clan but exiled for a deformity or curse. Others claim he was captured and sold into sideshow slavery, paraded as the "White Beast of the East." Either way, it was in such a pit fight that [[Sharl van der Cleev|Sharl]] found him.

Sharl stepped into the ring — not to kill Molko, but to tame him. He disarmed him with pain, locked eyes, and whispered something no one heard. Molko submitted.

Since that day, he followed Sharl like a quiet shadow, obliterating enemies with a maul nearly as tall as a man and shielding the Cleavers' most vital operations without a word.

## Current Status
[[Sharl van der Cleev|Sharl]] is dead. The [[Crimson Cleavers]] are gone.

Aspiria's Chosen released Molko rather than kill him at the Fauconwood confrontation.

Where Molko goes now, or what he does with a freedom he did not ask for and a purpose that no longer exists, is unknown as are his whereabouts.
## Combat Style

Molko fights like an avalanche — slow until he isn't, and then it's too late. His signature weapon is a massive maul nicknamed "Earthbreaker," wrapped in cold iron and banded with bone. He uses sheer force to break formations, scatter shield walls, or pulverize monsters twice his size.

Molko doesn't dodge. He endures. He walks through fire, magic, and steel, ignoring pain and resistance until the world yields. It usually does.

He is nearly impossible to stagger, and while unarmored, his resilience borders on unnatural. Theories abound — curses, experiments, blessings — but Molko never confirms anything.

## Notable Relationships
**[[Sharl van der Cleev]]** – Master, liberator, or something older. Molko obeyed him without hesitation. He is dead now.

**[[Dyna Dee]]** – Called him "Molko the Mountain." She escaped the battle. He was released. They are both out there, unaccounted for.
