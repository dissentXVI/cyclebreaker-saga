**Title**: Demolitions Expert (formerly of the Crimson Cleavers)

**Allegiance**: [[Crimson Cleavers]] (dissolved)

**Race**: Halfling

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Early 30s

**Eyes**: Bright emerald green

**Hair**: Sandy blonde, messy ponytail

**Attire**: Brass-plated engineering leathers, soot-streaked lab coat, oversized goggles

**Home**: Originally [[D'yadya]]; whereabouts unknown

## Appearance

Dyna is a walking contradiction: beaming, brilliant, and absolutely unstable. Standing barely over three feet tall, she's perpetually smudged with soot or acid stains. Her wide green eyes gleam with delight behind reinforced brass goggles — cracked on the left lens and repaired with resin and wire. Her armor is cobbled together from salvaged pieces: bombproof plating over a thick-leather corset, with dozens of belts, pouches, and vials strapped across her frame like a mobile alchemy lab.

Her smile rarely means you're safe.

## Personality

Unhinged, gleeful, and highly intelligent. Dyna lives for invention, experimentation, and the delightful chaos of discovery. She doesn't truly believe in good or evil — only in potential. That said, she's fiercely loyal to those who protect her curiosity and let her work unchecked. [[Sharl van der Cleev|Sharl]] gave her resources and freedom. That was enough.

She talks constantly, often to herself, and frequently changes subjects mid-thought. She has no filter, no fear, and no clear line between brilliance and madness.

## Character History

An orphan of the D'yadyan collapse, Dyna was raised in Vociran custody and assigned as a lab assistant to a prominent war engineer. Her intellect quickly outpaced her master's — a fact he realized moments before he was obliterated in one of her early experiments. Officially, she died in the same explosion.

Unofficially, she escaped through the blast hole in the wall and reinvented herself. She made her way west, freelancing as a tinker, saboteur, and alchemist-for-hire before being recruited by the [[Crimson Cleavers]]. [[Sharl van der Cleev|Sharl]] recognized her raw potential and gave her what no one else dared: an unlimited budget and no ethical constraints.

## Current Status

Dyna escaped the final confrontation at [[Sharl van der Cleev|Sharl]]'s Fauconwood stronghold. Sharl is dead. The Cleavers are gone. Her unlimited budget and ethical-free laboratory exist no longer.

What Dyna does next is anyone's guess — including Dyna's. She has her mind, her tools, her vials, and her considerable talent for survival. She has never needed much else. Whether she finds a new patron, goes freelance, or simply vanishes into the next interesting problem, she will be fine.

She always lands on her feet. Usually with something exploding behind her.

Her whereabouts are currently unknown.

## Combat Style

Dyna is not a front-line combatant — she is a walking catastrophe. Armed with grenades, incendiary flasks, smoke bombs, and pressure-reactive globes, she controls the battlefield through pure chemical madness. She's known to lob napalm across fortifications, dissolve enemy armor with corrosive acid, or ignite retreat paths as a farewell gift.

When cornered, she pulls out a backup wand, an electrified gauntlet, or just lights the entire tent on fire and runs. There's always a backup plan. It usually explodes.

## Notable Relationships

**[[Sharl van der Cleev]]** – Dead. He gave her resources and freedom. She won't find another patron like him — and she is not sure she wants to.

**[[Molko Beambearer]]** – Her favorite sounding board and occasional test subject. He was released by Aspiria's Chosen. She escaped. They are out there somewhere, unaccounted for.

**[[Uriah]]** – The other survivor. Whether they find each other is unknown.
