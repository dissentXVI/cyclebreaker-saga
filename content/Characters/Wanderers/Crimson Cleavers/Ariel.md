**Title**: Twin Fang of the Crimson Cleavers

**Allegiance**: [[Crimson Cleavers]]

**Race**: Elf

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Appears late 20s

**Eyes**: Teal

**Hair**: Platinum blonde

**Attire**: Form-fitting battle leathers tailored in asymmetrical Aqualondian fashion, accented with metal barbs and silk wraps

**Home**: Formerly [[Aqualonde]]; formerly the Fauconwood

**Status**: Deceased — killed by Aspiria's Chosen

## Appearance

Ariel is a striking vision of feral elegance — all lean sinew, sharp lines, and impossible poise. Her pale skin and silver-blonde hair contrast against the grime of her surroundings, a cultivated image of beauty sharpened into menace. Her leathers are custom-fit and designed to enhance movement, not modesty. Twin curved daggers rest sheathed at her hips, their hilts encrusted with small sapphires. A single black rose is tattooed behind her left ear.

She and her twin, [[Uriah]], often move in choreographed synchronicity, exchanging glances instead of words.

## Personality

Ariel is cruel, playful, and deeply theatrical. She lives in performance — each gesture, gaze, and breath calculated for effect. She delights in beauty and destruction in equal measure, often confusing the two. With outsiders, she is mocking and sadistic. With [[Uriah]], she is devoted. With [[Sharl van der Cleev|Sharl]], she is infatuated.

Ariel is intelligent, but emotionally stunted, having grown up in a bubble of privilege and punishment. Her sense of morality was amputated, then replaced with irony.

## Character History

Once scions of a powerful Aqualondian house, Ariel and her twin [[Uriah]] were the center of scandal after their aristocratic games spiraled into something monstrous. Disowned and hunted, they fled west and vanished into mercenary life — until [[Sharl van der Cleev|Sharl]] found them.

He didn't rehabilitate them. He gave them stage and steel. With the [[Crimson Cleavers]], they rose quickly: Ariel as interrogator, assassin, and ambassadress of fear. Her old life burned behind her like a velvet curtain. She never looked back.

## Legacy

Ariel was killed by Aspiria's Chosen during the final confrontation at Sharl's Fauconwood stronghold. [[Uriah]] escaped. They had never been separated before. Whether he survived the aftermath of that separation — in any meaningful sense — remains to be seen.

## Combat Style

Ariel is a rogue refined to surgical lethality — all flicker, feint, and finesse. Her true terror lies in her ability to manifest phantasmal echoes of herself across the battlefield. These illusory doubles move with the same grace and precision, creating a dance of blades that defies spatial logic.

She flits between positions unpredictably, swapping places with her echoes to avoid strikes, confuse enemies, or deliver lethal blows from impossible angles. Each step is a misdirection, each flourish a potential feint. Her attacks are swift and calculated — precise enough to sever tendons or silence a throat in a single motion.

Ariel is at her most deadly when fighting alongside [[Uriah]]. Together, with her echoes in play, it becomes impossible to tell which twin is real — until it's too late.

## Notable Relationships

**[[Uriah]]** – Her twin and co-dependent mirror. Their bond was inseparable and occasionally telepathic in all but name. He is now the surviving half of something that was never meant to be divided.

**[[Sharl van der Cleev]]** – The only man she ever respected. She died in his service.

**[[Chauni Stag-Eyes]]** – Tolerated but distrusted. Ariel suspected Chauni saw more than she let on. She was right.
