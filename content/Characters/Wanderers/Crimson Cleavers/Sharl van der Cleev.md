**Title**: Commander of the Crimson Cleavers

**Allegiance**: [[Crimson Cleavers]]

**Race**: Human

**Gender**: Male

**Age**: Appears early 30s

**Eyes**: Piercing ice blue; shift to bottomless black under certain conditions

**Hair**: Deep red, long and layered, tied at the nape with loose framing bangs

**Attire**: Crimson leather armor with silver-studded detailing, jaguar pelt cloak, filigree gauntlet

**Home**: The Fauconwood

**Status**: Deceased — killed by Aspiria's Chosen

## Appearance

Sharl van der Cleev is sculpted charisma and barely-veiled threat. Tall and lithe, his armor is an elegant warlord's regalia — crimson leathers stitched with silver studs, the pelt of a [[Gragato Jungle]] jaguar draped across one shoulder. Twin curved blades hang low at his hips. His left arm bears an ornate gauntlet that flexes like bone and silver muscle.

Most arresting are his ice-blue eyes, calm and cutting — until they shift to bottomless black, pulling at the edges of perception. He is always composed. He is never harmless.

## Personality

Sharl is a master manipulator: eloquent, predatory, and impossible to ignore. He speaks in tones that soothe even as they command, with language chosen for maximum intimacy or control. He is not sadistic — he simply sees most people as temporary things.

He cultivates loyalty by making himself indispensable. He rewards submission with purpose, and punishes resistance not with anger, but with inevitability. Beneath it all lies an absolute conviction: the weak should serve or die. The strong ascend.

## Character History

Much of Sharl's past is either speculation or silence. He speaks little of it. What is known is that he emerged from nowhere, rose through mercenary ranks, and took command of the [[Crimson Cleavers]] by sheer force of will. Under his hand, they grew from a squabbling band of raiders to one of the most feared private armies in Halinor.

Everything changed after his journey into the Fauconwood, where he vanished for three days and returned blood-soaked and cold-eyed. Some claim he struck a bargain with a dead god. Others believe he devoured something sacred — or let something devour him.

He doesn't deny the rumors. He simply watches.

After learning that Quiss de Barghest — [[Frog Gulgalla]] — had left the Cleavers carrying his child, something shifted in Sharl that none of his lieutenants fully understood. He scaled back the Cleavers' operations, made a home in the Fauconwood, and devoted himself to finding them. Not to reclaim a weapon. To build something he had never had: a family.

Whether that impulse was genuine, or simply another form of possession, is a question that will now go unanswered.

## Legacy

Sharl van der Cleev was killed by Aspiria's Chosen in a direct confrontation at his Fauconwood stronghold, alongside the majority of his named lieutenants. With his death, the Crimson Cleavers — the organization he built from nothing and shaped through sheer force of will — effectively ceased to exist as a unified force. [[Uriah]] and [[Dyna Dee]] escaped. [[Molko Beambearer]] was released.

The rest are dead.

## Combat Style

Sharl fights like a serpent dancing on the edge of a blade. He dual-wields rapiers or cutlasses with precision that borders on divine, weaving between strikes with inhuman grace. His left gauntlet acts as both a parrying claw and a magical conduit — snapping steel from the air or snaring the soul mid-scream.

He is a duelist, but also a performer. He kills like he's proving a point. And the point is: you were never going to win.

Even the air seems to yield to his tempo.

## Notable Relationships

**[[Chauni Stag-Eye]]** – Saved his life in the Fauconwood. Possibly the only one who ever saw him clearly.

**[[Frog Gulgalla]]** – Known to him as Quiss de Barghest. Former companion, trusted operative, and the only person who ever left him. He spent his final years searching for her and their daughter. He never found them.
