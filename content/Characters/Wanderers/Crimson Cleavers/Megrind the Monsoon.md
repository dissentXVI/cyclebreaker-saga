**Title**: Arcane Stormwright

**Allegiance**: [[Crimson Cleavers]]

**Race**: Human

**Gender**: Male

**Age**: Late 50s

**Eyes**: Piercing gray

**Hair**: Dark, streaked with silver; slicked back

**Attire**: Embroidered violet-and-gold robes, Avosan-style

**Home**: [[Avosa Tesota]] (exiled); formerly the Fauconwood

**Status**: Deceased — killed by Aspiria's Chosen

## Appearance

Megrind bears the harsh, sun-lined features of a man forged by sea air and burned bridges. His craggy face, severe hooked nose, and sharply trimmed goatee give him a hawkish appearance. His long, slicked-back hair is streaked with silver and beginning to thin at the crown — though he hides it with careful combing and posture. His robes are Avosan in cut and proudly embroidered in deep purple and black, lined with gold-stitched runes of tempest. A jeweled pendant shaped like a lightning-bolt compass hangs from his throat — a relic of his seafaring past.

When he speaks, his voice cuts like a harpoon.

## Personality

Precision is Megrind's religion. Calculating, fastidious, and often disdainful, he treats most people as unstable variables in otherwise elegant equations. He does not shout — he corrects. He does not demand — he enforces.

Despite his coldness, Megrind is not cruel. He simply believes he knows better than anyone else in the room — and often, he does. His Avosan upbringing bred a love for grand gestures and public triumphs, but years in exile have reduced his theatrics to bitter efficiency.

He views most magic as wasteful. His own spellwork is lean, engineered, and devastating.

## Character History

Megrind was once one of [[Avosa Tesota]]'s most celebrated elementalists — a naval battle-wizard with a penchant for controlling entire weather systems to shield fleets or strike down blockades. He rose to prominence through the Merchant Navy, eventually earning his own ship and leading an elite crew of arcane mariners.

But his greed spiraled. Megrind began selling stormfronts to pirate captains and foreign dignitaries, trading weather for wealth. When the High Admiralty discovered his betrayals, he was condemned in absentia and hunted by his own former allies. His flagship vanished in a magical squall during his escape.

He washed ashore in Halinor with nothing but his spellbook and bitterness. [[Sharl van der Cleev|Sharl]] offered him coin, protection, and a war to shape. Megrind never looked back.

## Legacy

Megrind was killed by Aspiria's Chosen during the final confrontation at [[Sharl van der Cleev|Sharl]]'s Fauconwood stronghold. He died as he operated — with precision and without apology, likely standing still until the last possible moment.

## Combat Style

Megrind specializes in environmental control and devastating weather effects. He crafts precision storm spells — fog banks that mask troop movement, localized quakes, deafening thunderbursts, and spears of lightning that leap from iron to blood.

In combat, he remains calm, deliberate, and static — a living fulcrum that controls the chaos around him. When necessary, he casts from scroll-anchored glyphs sewn into his robes, minimizing motion to avoid counterspells or distractions.

If touched, he panics. If cornered, he becomes catastrophic.

## Notable Relationships

**[[Sharl van der Cleev]]** – Values Sharl's ruthless clarity. Saw him as a capable warlord and worthwhile investor.

**[[Dyna Dee]]** – Utter disdain. He called her "a gnome in denial." She referred to him as "Captain Moist" and sabotaged his tea.
