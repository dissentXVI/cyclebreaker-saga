**Title**: Former Adventurer, Healer for Hire

**Allegiance**: None (currently working in [[Tarwa'kem]] under House Zahir's patronage)

**Race**: Half-Elf

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Late 20s

**Eyes**: Amber

**Hair**: Ash-brown, sun-faded and tied loosely

**Attire**: Practical, weathered traveling gear, bandages and old talismans

**Home**: Once the open road — now the back alleys and clinics of [[Tarwa'kem]]

## Appearance

Eda bears the look of someone who survived something awful and never stopped carrying it. Her clothes are worn from the road and her arms bear faint marks of healed burns and claw scrapes. Her stance is restless — always ready to move but never quite knowing where. She keeps a red sash tied around her waist, once part of her old adventuring uniform. She still wears her old holy symbol, though its chain is rusted and the emblem dulled.

There's kindness in her face — but it's buried deep beneath guilt.

## Personality

Eda is soft-spoken, cautious, and deeply burdened. She avoids eye contact when speaking about her past, and tends to deflect compliments or reassurances. She is wracked with survivor's guilt, convinced her failure as a healer led to the death of everyone she cared about.

When engaged, she is competent, generous, and thoughtful — but haunted. Beneath her humility is a growing desperation: a belief that there must be a way to make it right. That healing doesn't have to stop at death.

She doesn't talk about the place her party fell. Not yet.

## Character History

Eda Braelyn once traveled with a small adventuring band — a scrappy group of explorers and treasure-seekers. She served as their cleric, medic, and heart. Among them was her fiancé, a swordsman named Mikel. Together, they delved into a forgotten ruin near the Irinshire, chasing rumors of ancient relics.

Only she came back.

Wounded, half-mad, and with her god silent, Eda stumbled into [[Tarwa'kem]] with no coin and no direction. Reduced to begging, she nearly came to blows with another desperate soul before a stranger — [[Ariesa Hecate|Ariesa Lightbringer]] — intervened.

It was Ariesa who reminded her what hope felt like. It was [[Laena Zahir]] who gave her a chance — a job in a local medical ward and a reason to try again.

## Current Status

Eda now works under a temporary appointment in one of [[Tarwa'kem]]'s lower-tier healing houses, supported by [[Laena Zahir]]'s coin. She's a quick study, quiet but skilled, and has earned the respect of some of the more jaded street medics.

## Combat Style

Eda is a divine caster and support specialist, trained in restorative magic and protective wards. At present, she avoids violence — but her instinct for battlefield healing remains sharp. She uses binding magic, protective charms, and can still wield a mace if forced.

## Notable Relationships

**[[Ariesa Hecate|Ariesa Lightbringer]]** – The first kind voice after her fall. Eda clings to this kindness like a thread to sanity.

**Mikel** – Her deceased fiancé. She speaks to him at night in prayer — and listens as if he might answer.

## Secrets

Eda has begun recording necromantic notes in a cipher borrowed from a former patient's tattoo.

She believes the ruin where her party fell is calling her back — and that something waits beneath it.
