> "All children are sacred. Even the unwanted. Especially the unwanted."

**Title**: Wandering Midwife of Petra

**Allegiance**: The Goddess Petra

**Race**: Elf

**Gender**: Female

**Age**: Appears late 60s

**Eyes**: Storm-gray

**Hair**: Silver-white, braided beneath her veil

**Attire**: Modest sky-blue robes with moonstone embroidery

**Home**: Formerly of the [[Laconia]] region; last seen in [[Snowfinch Rest]]

## Appearance

Ashera wears the gentle dignity of a healer who has stood beside birth and death in equal measure. Her flowing robes, pale blue with silver edging, are adorned with simple symbols of Petra — crescent moons, small birds, and teardrop pendants. Her hair is always covered, and her hands bear the faint calluses of a lifetime tending wounds and cradling newborns.

Her voice is soft, but firm. Her presence calms the frightened — and disarms the wicked.

## Personality

Ashera is profoundly devout — not in ritual or hierarchy, but in service. Her faith in Petra, goddess of the moon, love, and children, manifests in quiet acts: delivering babies, comforting the grief-stricken, and defying laws that endanger the vulnerable.

She does not preach. She helps. And if her help angers men of power, so be it.

Though hunted, she does not act like a fugitive. She walks as though her purpose is divine — and non-negotiable.

## Character History

Ashera once served quietly in the orphanage wards near [[Laconia]], tending to abandoned infants and the dying alike. Years later, she felt Petra's call northward, toward [[Carinthia]]. She traveled to [[Snowfinch Rest]], intending to cross the Crystal Bridge and offer her services to expecting mothers in a land that forbade certain practices and saw every child as a soldier in the making. The laws did not deter her. She treated quietly, and in secret when needed.

## Current Status

Ashera was eventually discovered near [[Snowfinch Rest]] by operatives of the [[Crimson Cleavers]] — specifically [[Pierre Poinsard]] and [[Megrind the Monsoon]] — who seemed to know exactly who they were looking for.

Her capture was not public, but Aspiria's Chosen suspect she is now in Cleaver custody — not for punishment, but because of what she knows. She has spoken little since her apprehension.

Her heart remains with the children she helped — and the one she let go.

## Combat Style

Ashera does not fight, but she does protect. She uses divine magic tied to Petra's aspects: calm emotions, warding charms, and lunar blessings that obscure her and others from harm. In moments of desperation, she can summon bursts of radiant moonlight to blind or repel.

## Notable Relationships

None known.
