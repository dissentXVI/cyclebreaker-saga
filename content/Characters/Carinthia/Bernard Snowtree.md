**Title**: Captain of the Guard, [[Snowfinch Rest]]  

**Allegiance**: [[Carinthia]] (Templars, Secular Arm)  

**Race**: Elf  

**Gender**: Male  

**Age**: Late 40s  

**Eyes**: Sky blue  

**Hair**: White-blond, shoulder-length, windswept  

**Attire**: Steel and chainmail with navy cloak and fur mantle  

**Home**: [[Snowfinch Rest]], Northern [[Halinor]]  

## Appearance

Bernard is tall and broad-shouldered, clad in practical winter armor reinforced with chainmail and wolf-fur. His warm expression and disarming smile stand in contrast to the harsh landscape around him. He keeps his blade on his back and his shield nearby, but often fights without a helm — choosing trust over intimidation. His house crest is unknown.

## Personality

Bernard is sincere, loyal, and fiercely protective of those under his command. He is an idealist — not in the sense of being naive, but because he chooses to believe in people even when it would be easier not to. Where others see risk, he sees opportunity for mercy; where others see heresy, he sees potential for redemption.

He is fond of banter and warmth, though he never lets levity distract from duty. When pushed, he shows a steel resolve and surprising tactical clarity, particularly in defense of the vulnerable.

To many, Bernard is the "heart" of [[Snowfinch Rest]] — a knight not just of skill, but of soul.

## Character History

Bernard was raised as a commoner in the northern province. His mother was a peasant healer who died when he was young; her name is remembered only in the journals Bernard keeps hidden in his quarters.

His first command was a disaster — a caravan ambushed by bandits that led to the deaths of civilians and friends. Instead of breaking, Bernard became resolute. He reformed Snowfinch's defensive doctrine, trained militia from among the townsfolk, and forged bonds with both Church officials and the locals.

He now commands [[Snowfinch Rest]], the last major Carinthian hold before the Crystal Bridge. Despite the growing pressure from [[The Inquisition]] and supply shortages due to the rising cult activity, Bernard has kept the settlement stable and its people hopeful.

## Current Status

Bernard is currently overseeing the defense and daily administration of Snowfinch Rest. He serves directly under Lord Commander [[Adrien Frost]], who trusts Bernard's judgment more than any local noble.

Though not formally knighted by any High House, Bernard's actions and reputation have earned him informal honors across the frontier. He walks a tightrope between military command, church oversight, and civilian needs — often serving as mediator, peacemaker, and sword-arm all at once.

He is quietly investigating Iceheart activity in the region, suspecting that their recent actions are more than just religious rebellion — something is stirring in the north, and Bernard intends to meet it head-on.

## Combat Style

Bernard is a front-line commander. He wields a longsword and shield in classic Carinthian style, favoring defensive formations and quick counters. His strength lies in battlefield awareness — knowing when to hold, when to charge, and when to retreat with dignity.

He trains with his men daily and often leads drills himself, earning their respect not with speeches, but sweat and shieldwork.

## Notable Relationships

[[Adrien Frost]] – Bernard is unshakably loyal to [[Adrien Frost|Adrien]], seeing in him the leader Carinthia needs rather than the one tradition demands. [[Adrien Frost|Adrien]], in turn, trusts Bernard implicitly and shields him from political threats where he can.

[[The Inquisition]] – Bernard tolerates them out of necessity. He sees their value but mistrusts their zeal. He quietly undermines their more brutal measures, always placing the safety of Snowfinch's people above theological purity.

[[Greta Wundt]] and [[Tomas Wundt]] – The innkeepers of the [[Pale Roost]] and longtime friends. Greta supplies Bernard with information and a place to relax, while Tomas serves as a quiet confidant. They're among the few who treat Bernard as a man, not a title.
