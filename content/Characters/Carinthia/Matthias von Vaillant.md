**Title**: Heir of House Vaillant

**Allegiance**: Carinthia (Secular Arm, High House Vaillant)

**Race**: Elf

**Gender**: Male

**Age**: Late 20s

**Eyes**: Deep grey

**Hair**: Dark brown, worn long and straight

**Attire**: Steel-and-crimson officer’s cuirass with gold etchings

**Home**: [[Vaillant Hall]], [[Heldenheim]]

## Appearance

Matthias stands tall and lean, with sharp, noble features and a gaze that’s often unreadable. His attire balances function and form: crimson and black fabrics trimmed in fur, layered over well-kept armor. His expression rarely softens in public, often appearing reserved or quietly severe. Snow and wind do little to disturb his composed bearing, and his posture reflects both training and pride.

## Personality

Matthias is dutiful, serious, and burdened by expectation. As the eldest legitimate son of Lord Gerard von Vaillant, he holds himself to impossibly high standards — and expects the same of others. His tone is formal, his judgments cautious but firm, and his loyalty to Carinthia is absolute.

Though he sometimes lacks his father's warmth or Bernard's easy charisma, Matthias is an effective leader who leads by example. He struggles with self-doubt, particularly in the shadow of his father and [[Adrien Frost|Lord Commander Frost]], but refuses to let it show.

Behind closed doors, those close to him know him as introspective and even gentle — though he rarely allows himself that luxury in public.

## Character History

Born the first legitimate son of Lord Gerard von Vaillant, Matthias was raised for command. His education was rigorous — military strategy, elven history, and political diplomacy were drilled into him before he came of age. His early service was in the rear guard of northern patrols, but his breakthrough came during the defense of Eir’s Watch, where he successfully led a flanking maneuver against a draconic swarm, earning the respect of hardened Templar veterans.

He has since served in key roles throughout the Wyrmsfall War, often dispatched by [[Adrien Frost]] as an officer capable of executing precise and honorable battlefield strategy.

Though born to nobility, Matthias has never rested on his name. He fights hard to prove his worth and uphold the ideals of House Vaillant — duty, discipline, and sacrifice.

## Current Status

Matthias currently serves as a senior field officer within the Knights Templar, stationed along Carinthia’s central-northern corridor. He is tasked with coordinating forward defenses, responding to dragonkin incursions, and maintaining discipline among mixed-rank units stretched thin by the war.

He frequently reports to both Lord Commander [[Adrien Frost]] and his father, Lord Gerard von Vaillant, often caught in the ideological tension between the two men. Matthias respects both immensely but is quietly conflicted over which legacy to follow.

He has recently returned from a mission near Varrick’s Crossing, bearing reports of strange cult activity interwoven with the dragon threat — a matter he is investigating discreetly, wary of both the Inquisition’s interference and the growing instability across the front.

## Combat Style

Matthias is a refined duelist and tactician. He wields a longsword of Vaillant steel with exceptional precision, preferring traditional Carinthian styles over flamboyant techniques. In battle, he is a calm presence — measured, adaptive, and never wasteful.

He excels at leading small-to-medium scale operations and training younger officers, often balancing brutal realities with lessons in honor and restraint.

## Notable Relationships

[[Lord Gerard von Vaillant]] – His father and mentor. Matthias strives to live up to Gerard’s legacy and moral clarity. Though Gerard rarely praises Matthias openly, there is deep mutual respect. Matthias often wonders whether his father views him as a true successor — or merely as a steward of the family name.

[[Adrien Frost]] – Matthias admires [[Adrien Frost|Adrien]] deeply, perhaps more than he would admit. He views [[Adrien Frost|Adrien]] as the kind of leader he hopes to become. Though unaware of [[Adrien Frost|Adrien]]’s true parentage, Matthias senses a quiet kinship between [[Adrien Frost|Adrien]] and his father that he does not share, which leaves him conflicted.

[[Bernard Snowtree]] – His half-brother - Matthias respects Bernard’s service record and character but keeps their interactions formal.

[[Claudia Metellus]] – A superior officer he holds in cautious esteem. Matthias respects her battlefield skill and strict order, though he finds her hard to read. She reminds him of the side of war he tries not to become.
