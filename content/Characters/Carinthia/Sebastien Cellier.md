**Title**: The Lightwarden

**Allegiance**: [[Carinthia]], under direct command of Lord Commander [[Adrien Frost]]

**Race**: Elf

**Gender**: Male

**Age**: Late 40s

**Eyes**: Steel gray

**Hair**: Wavy black, shoulder-length

**Attire**: Ornate blue and gold plate with Carinthian etchings

**Home**: [[Heldenheim]], [[Carinthia]]

## Appearance

As the Lightwarden, Sebastien is a towering figure in ornate, frost-trimmed armor. His full helm conceals his identity, and while his plate bears the artistry of Carinthian craftsmanship, it bears no twelve-pointed star of [[Alophia]] — a subtle, deliberate choice. The armor is symbolic, but also functional — built for swift, acrobatic movement. When unarmored, he is lean and sharp-featured, with pointed elven ears and a solemn, striking presence. His black hair and piercing gaze evoke both nobility and severity, a man forged in war and sharpened by duty.

## Personality

Taciturn and unflinchingly loyal, Sebastien is a blade in the hands of [[Carinthia]] — and wields himself as such. Though he answers directly to [[Adrien Frost]], his devotion to the defense of the realm borders on religious. He holds little patience for politics or priests, seeing doctrine as useful only when it protects the innocent. A soldier first, and a symbol second, Sebastien speaks rarely but never wastes words. He is haunted by his past, and every scar he hides beneath his armor is a penance for a mistake he refuses to name.

## Character History

Sebastien Cellier was born to a minor noble house in eastern [[Carinthia]], known more for its martial traditions than its wealth. Orphaned during a draconic raid in the early years of the [[Wyrmsfall War]], Sebastien was raised in the garrison at [[Somerthal]] and quickly showed a gift for combat — particularly against wyrms. He was handpicked by [[Adrien Frost]] after the [[Battle of Vaeld's Crossing]], where he saved a full squadron from a drake ambush. Named the Lightwarden after singlehandedly slaying a skyborn tyrant above the [[Crystal Bridge]], he has since become both myth and warning to Carinthia’s foes.

## Current Status

Sebastien serves as [[Carinthia]]’s Lightwarden, the personal spear of [[Adrien Frost]] and protector of [[City-State]]. He operates outside the Inquisition’s purview, trusted to act with lethal discretion. While he rarely speaks at court, his presence commands immediate attention. His current mission is currently unknown.

## Combat Style

An aerial spearmaster trained in the Dragoon tradition, Sebastien is a blur of motion in battle. His signature weapon, Dawnpiercer, is a relic-tier lance blessed by the Light of Alophia. He favors quick, high-impact strikes, vaulting from impossible heights with divine precision. He fights with brutal elegance, unrelenting and merciless, and when he dons his helm — he does not speak.

## Notable Relationships

[[Adrien Frost]] – His commander and the only man Sebastien trusts without hesitation. They share a quiet, battlefield-forged bond.

[[Bernard Snowtree]] – Mutual respect. While not close, Sebastien sees Bernard’s compassion as a necessary counter to his own severity.

[[Aedelweg von Achaard]] – Tolerated. Sebastien respects the Archbishop's authority but often avoids ecclesiastical entanglements.

[[Renai Aymere]] – An adversary. Their clashes are rare but cataclysmic; Sebastien believes her beliefs pervert Saint Iseult’s legacy.

## Secrets

Has recurring visions of a pale dragon that calls him “brother” — a remnant of contact with a dying wyrm years ago.

Keeps the torn blue cloak of a fallen comrade beneath his armor — a reminder of the one person he failed to protect.

Trained secretly in the old style of the Dawnbound before it was reserved solely for Church inquisitors.
