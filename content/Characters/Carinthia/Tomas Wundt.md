**Race**: Human

**Gender**: Male

**Age**: Late 40s

**Eyes**: Dark, quietly watchful

**Hair**: Thinning black, combed back

**Attire**: Plain working clothes beneath a worn apron; never overdressed, never underdressed

**Home:** [[The Pale Roost]], [[Snowfinch Rest]]

## Appearance

Tomas is a wiry, pale man with the particular leanness of someone who has spent decades on his feet and never quite stopped to eat. His thinning black hair is combed back with care, and his face carries the still, unremarkable quality of a man who has learned that being unnoticed is a useful skill. He moves quietly through the Pale Roost, refilling cups and clearing plates with an economy of motion that borders on invisible.

## Personality

Polite, reserved, and deeply observant. Tomas never speaks more than the moment requires, and what he does say tends to land with more weight than its brevity suggests. He has a habit of beginning his responses with a slow, thoughtful *"Mm..."* — a sound that can mean agreement, hesitation, or careful evasion depending on the company. When inquisitors are in the room, the *"Mm..."* becomes a near-constant refrain, and his answers grow shorter by half.

He is a good listener, a poor gossip, and an excellent keeper of other people's business. He does not volunteer information — but he notices everything.

## Narrative Relevance

Tomas and his wife [[Greta Wundt]] run the [[The Pale Roost]] in [[Snowfinch Rest]], one of the few reliably warm and honest establishments near the northern frontier. He serves as a quiet confidant to [[Bernard Snowtree]], who trusts him with things a captain cannot say aloud. For the players, Tomas is the kind of man who will answer a direct question carefully, deflect an indirect one gracefully, and remember everything they said three days later.
