>"We temper peace before we temper steel."

## Overview

**Founders:** Early master-smiths of [[The Gloam Smithy]], united under the first Grand Forgewright during the Fifth Radiant Age  
**Primary Location:** [[The Gloam Smithy]], moonlit cliffs of northern [[Aqualonde]]  
**Current Leader(s):** [[Jannalor Rokas]]  

## Structure and Ranks

- **Grand Forgewright** – Supreme head of the Moonblades, responsible for strategy, armament design, and guild direction.
- **Forge Wardens** – Senior smiths and tacticians overseeing specialized divisions of weaponcraft, armor-forging, and ward construction.
- **Journeyman Smiths** – Fully trained craftsmen producing Moonblade-standard arms, armor, and defensive wards.
- **Apprentices** – Novice smiths and recruits undergoing martial and technical training.

## History

The Moonblades trace their roots to the Fifth Radiant Age, when the first Grand Forgewright unified several master-smiths of the Gloam Smithy to provide [[Aqualonde]] with a dedicated martial arm. Rejecting the excess of flashy enchantments, they developed the **Elemental Steel** tradition—fusing [[pneuma]] with metal through deliberate forging cycles, creating arms of exceptional resonance and control.

Historically, the Moonblades have acted as [[Aqualonde]]'s last line of defense, standing fast during the vineblight plague when their wards preserved [[The Elder Tree]] from collapse. While they rarely participate in [[Aqualonde]]'s salon politics, they have consistently influenced the city's survival, building the fortifications and crafting the weapons that allow other guilds to thrive.

Under [[Jannalor Rokas]], the Moonblades have expanded their scope, discreetly preparing for greater instability across Halinor. This includes forging **Breathfold armor** and constructing hidden vaults for relics, breathstone reserves, and sacred texts.

## Narrative Relevance

The Moonblades remain a quiet but critical force in [[Aqualonde]]'s security, balancing readiness with restraint. Their work at [[The Gloam Smithy]] and [[The Thornward|Thornward]] shapes both the city's defenses and its martial traditions. With rumors of unrest and external threats mounting, the Moonblades' preparations may determine whether [[Aqualonde]] survives the next crisis.
