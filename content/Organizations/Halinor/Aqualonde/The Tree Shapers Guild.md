>"We do not build. We teach the roots where to grow."
## Overview

**Founders:**  A coalition of master arborists, druidic artisans, and structural weavers in early Fifth Radiant Age Aqualonde.
**Primary Location:**  The Boughspire, a living tower shaped from a single colossal tree over centuries.
**Current Leader(s):**  [[Verdane Lysurel]] - Green Hand of the Tree Shapers

## Structure and Ranks

- **Green Hand** – The guiding figure of the guild, responsible for overseeing sacred shaping projects and mediating disputes between nature’s will and civic needs.
- **Boughwrights** – Senior shapers trusted with large-scale works, such as redirecting root systems, shaping arboreal bridges, or stabilizing the Elder Tree’s growth.
- **Knotbinders** – Skilled journeymen who tend the city’s living structures and train apprentices in the Arborium Rite.
- **Sapling Hands** – Apprentices learning the principles of living architecture, guided through years of patient shaping.
- **Caretenders** – Civil liaisons tasked with ensuring public safety and maintenance of the guild’s living works.

## History

Formed during the city’s first major expansion, the Tree Shapers arose to prevent [[Aqualonde]]’s forest dwellings from becoming unstable or invasive to one another. Their early generations pioneered techniques for coaxing trees into walls, bridges, and galleries without severing their life force—a blend of druidcraft and engineering now known as the Arborium Rite.  

[[The Boughspire]] itself remains their greatest work, a spiraling sanctum that houses the guild’s records, bonsai galleries, and seed-vaults. Though often seen as slow to act, the Tree Shapers’ work is built to last centuries, not seasons
