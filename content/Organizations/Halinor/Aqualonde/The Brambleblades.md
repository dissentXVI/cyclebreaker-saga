>"Invisible in peace, inevitable in peril."

## Overview

**Founders:**  Early city wardens of [[Aqualonde]]; reformed and professionalized under [[Jannalor Rokas]] after the vineblight crisis
**Primary Location:**  [[The Thornward]] (training grounds and barracks); liaison armory at [[The Gloam Smithy]]
**Current Leader(s):**  Strategic command by [[Jannalor Rokas]]; field leadership by rotating Brambleblade captains (seconded Bladewardens from [[The Moonblade Forge|the Moonblades]])

## Structure and Ranks

- **Strategic Commander (Grand Forgewright)** – Sets doctrine, readiness, and kit standards ([[The Moonblade Forge|Moonblade]] oversight).
- **Bladewardens** – Veteran [[The Moonblade Forge|Moonblade]] officers attached as field commanders and trainers.
- **Bramble Captains** – Career officers commanding companies and regional watches.
- **Path-Sergeants** – Squad leaders; masters of glade tactics, ambush discipline, and nonlethal control.
- **Glade-Scouts** – Recon and tracking specialists; coordinate with [[The Windriders Guild|Windrider]] hawks for sky relays.
- **Thornwall** – Shield-and-spear units for holding lines and chokepoints.
- **Crescents** – Fast assault cadres wielding crescent blades and thorned spears
- **Novitiates** – Recruits in Thornward gauntlets; rotate through silence drills, terrain craft, and ward etiquette.

## History

Raised from early wardens and militia, the corps proved its worth in border frictions with Carinthia but nearly broke during [[the vineblight]]. In the aftermath, [[Jannalor Rokas]] rebuilt the force from the roots up—standardizing [[Breathfold armor]], integrating [[Elemental Steel]] forms, and embedding the Brambleblades within [[The Moonblade Forge|the Moonblade]] logistics network. Since [[The Trembling Foundations]], the corps has trained as small, adaptive units that treat [[the Fauconwood]] as ally and shield, striking precisely and vanishing without scarring sacred ground.

## Narrative Relevance

With [[the fae]] silent, and rumors of Vociran movement, night drills at [[The Thornward]] have doubled and caches along [[Riverwalk]] approaches are quietly restocked. The Brambleblades’ doctrine—defend without domination, strike without desecration—positions them as the Council’s first responder and last resort. Whether [[Aqualonde]] bends or braces, these are the hands that will hold the line.
