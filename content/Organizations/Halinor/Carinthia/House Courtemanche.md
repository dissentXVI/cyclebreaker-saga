> "Their power lies in what they know — and what they choose never to reveal."

## Overview

**Founders:** Founding Dawnbound knight (ancestor of current line); early seers and archivists of Saint Carine's exodus  
**Primary Location:** Spire of Vesper, [[Heldenheim]]  
**Current Head:** [[Ismérie von Courtemanche]]  
**Coat of Arms:** A silver-bordered black shield bearing a silver crescent moon, flanked by two blue stars  
**Domain:** Arcane scholarship, cosmological navigation, esoteric knowledge

House Courtemanche is Carinthia's most revered house of arcane scholarship — a family whose legacy has been built not on the battlefield, but in shadowed libraries and silent observatories. Where other houses claim valor, labor, or devotion, Courtemanche commands the power of knowledge, and guards it jealously.

Their high towers rise like lances into the fogged skies of [[Heldenheim]], symbols of vision beyond the mortal veil.

## Structure and Ranks

House Courtemanche provides the realm's most skilled mages, scribes, star-priests, and ward-architects to the [[The Light of Alophia|Church of Alophia]]. Their libraries are closed to the public; their observatories are forbidden to all but the most senior ecclesiastical figures. No war effort or coronation proceeds without a Courtemanche signature somewhere on the parchment.

Members serve as arcane advisors to inquisitorial operations and Dawnbound campaigns, confirming miracles, identifying heresies, and anchoring divine rites in sigilcraft.

## History

House Courtemanche traces its origins to the first generation of seers who followed [[Saint Carine]] during the exodus north — archivists and astronomers who mapped the stars and tracked divine alignments believed to reflect Alophia's will.

Over the centuries, the house became synonymous with theoretical magic, cosmological navigation, and the creation of warding and divine matrix systems. They were instrumental in protecting [[Heldenheim]] from early dragon incursions — not through force, but through glyphs and veilcraft known only to them.

## Leadership and Lineage

**[[Ismérie von Courtemanche]]** — Current head. Presides from the Spire of Vesper, an ancient observatory-fortress carved into the eastern cliffs of Heldenheim. Renowned as a master archivist, astrotheurge, and silent judge of knowledge; admired and feared in equal measure. Credited with restoring the *Index Nocturne*, a collection of forbidden theological texts. One of the few non-clerical figures permitted to annotate Church doctrine. Her rule is defined by secrecy, legacy, and control.

**[[Gisele von Courtemanche]]** — Her younger son. A scholar-mage of great renown and one of the [[The Dawnbound|Dawnbound]], known as the *Seeker of Heavenly Mysteries*. Serves as both combat support caster and field theologian. Maintains quiet devotion to his mother's legacy while privately questioning how much truth can be withheld in the name of order.

**[[Théodore von Courtemanche]]** — Eldest son and heir. A cold, doctrinaire magister stationed at the Cathedral of Karsmont. Believes knowledge must be bound by chains and law. Views Gisele's field deployments as reckless — possibly heretical — and considers himself the true guardian of Courtemanche tradition.

## Narrative Relevance

House Courtemanche represents the tension between knowledge and power in Carinthia — the question of what the realm's most learned figures actually know, and what they have chosen to bury. Their libraries are said to hold truths too dangerous to speak aloud; others believe they merely maintain the illusions that hold Carinthia together.
