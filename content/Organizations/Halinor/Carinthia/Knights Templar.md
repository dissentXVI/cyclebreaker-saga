## Overview

**Founders:** Established in the aftermath of the [[Wyrmsfall War]]; formalized under [[Louis'roi Arch de Soleil]]'s consolidation of Carinthian power  
**Primary Location:** [[Skyward Keep]], [[Heldenheim]]  
**Current Leader(s):** Lord Commander [[Adrien Frost]]

The Knights Templar are Carinthia's standing military order — the professional armed force of the kingdom and the secular counterpart to the Church's holy orders. While the [[The Dawnbound|Dawnbound]] serve as elite champions and the [[The Inquisition|Inquisition]] roots out heresy from within, the Knights Templar are the shield of Heldenheim itself: garrisoning its walls, commanding its armies, and keeping order across what remains of Carinthian territory.

Headquartered at [[Skyward Keep]] — the command citadel of the Lord Commander — the Templars operate under secular authority but are expected to uphold the faith and answer to the Archbishop when matters of doctrine intersect with matters of war.

## Structure and Ranks

The Templars function through a formal military hierarchy, with the Lord Commander holding supreme operational authority over all armed forces in Carinthia. Beneath him:

- **High Marshals** — Regional commanders responsible for fortress garrisons and field campaigns
- **Knight-Commanders** — Officers leading individual detachments; often drawn from or affiliated with the [[High Houses of Carinthia|High Houses]]
- **Knights Templar** — Full members; consecrated warriors who have sworn dual oaths to crown and faith
- **Squires and Men-at-Arms** — Supporting soldiers and those in training for full knighthood

Many Templar officers maintain close ties to the [[High Houses of Carinthia|High Houses]], particularly [[House Vaillant]], whose secular martial tradition forms the backbone of Templar culture. Many Vaillant-trained knights refuse the title "holy knight," preferring the Templar designation, which they view as less doctrinally presumptuous.

## History

The Knights Templar were formalized as a distinct institution during and after the era of [[Louis'roi Arch de Soleil]], who needed a military body that could enforce his governance without being wholly consumed by the Church hierarchy. Over generations, the Templars evolved into a professional corps capable of defending Carinthia's borders, conducting campaigns against the dragonkin of the [[Wyrmsfall War]], and maintaining internal order.

Their relationship with the Church has always been defined by functional cooperation and underlying tension. The Templars serve the Archbishop — but they are not commanded by him. The Lord Commander is chosen by the [[High Houses of Carinthia|High Houses]], not appointed by the Archbishop, a distinction that has caused friction in several historical periods.

## Narrative Relevance

The Knights Templar represent Carinthia's secular and martial pragmatism — the part of the kingdom that bleeds and dies on the front lines regardless of doctrine. Lord Commander [[Adrien Frost]]'s relationship with the Archbishop, the Dawnbound, and the broader question of what Carinthia is actually fighting *for* makes the Templars a meaningful counterweight to the Church's theological certainty.
