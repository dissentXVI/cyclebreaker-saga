## Overview

**Founders:** Four surviving Dawnbound knights of Archbishop Louis-roi Arch de Soleil  
**Primary Location:** [[Heldenheim]], Carinthia  
**Current Leaders:** One head per house (see individual entries)

The High Houses are the four noble bloodlines of [[Heldenheim|Carinthia]], each descended from a knight who survived the battle against [[Skadraxes]] during the early [[Wyrmsfall War]]. Elevated by [[Louis'roi Arch de Soleil]] as a reward for their sacrifice, each house was granted a domain of authority that has defined its identity across generations.

The four houses are:

- **[[House Vaillant]]** — The martial house; guardians of Carinthia's borders and military tradition
- **[[House Achaard]]** — The ecclesiastical house; the living arm of the [[The Light of Alophia|Church of Alophia]]
- **[[House Courtemanche]]** — The arcane house; keepers of esoteric knowledge and sigilcraft
- **[[House Bellefeuille]]** — The craftsman house; stewards of commerce, architecture, and artisan tradition

## History

The High Houses were founded in the aftermath of the battle that felled [[Skadraxes]]. Of the Dawnbound who rode with Archbishop Louis into that confrontation, only four survived. Louis-roi ennobled their bloodlines and granted each a seat of power in the newly consolidated Carinthian theocracy, binding faith and feudal authority into a single structure that endures to the present day.

Though all four houses nominally serve the [[The Light of Alophia|Church of Alophia]] and the Archbishop, their loyalties, methods, and relationships with one another are frequently complicated by history and competing interests.

## Narrative Relevance

The political dynamics of Carinthia run largely through the tensions between and within the High Houses — their alliances, secrets, and rivalries form a significant undercurrent of the Carinthian arc.
