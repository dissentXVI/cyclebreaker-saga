## Overview

**Founders:** Established under the early [[Karo'kem Dynasty]]; reformed under [[Tarik Tazi]]
**Primary Location:** [[Tarwa'kem]]; barracks near the [[Kemtabi Shelf]]
**Current Leader(s):** General [[Tarik Tazi]]

## Structure and Ranks

- **General** – Supreme military commander; appointed directly by the Sultana. Currently [[Tarik Tazi]].
- **Legion Commanders** – Senior officers overseeing major divisions and strategic operations.
- **Wardens** – Mid-rank officers commanding units, responsible for training discipline and field decisions.
- **Shield-Bearers** – Core infantry; the Legion's backbone, trained in formation combat and city defense.
- **Gilded Scouts** – Reconnaissance and fast-response operatives, used for intelligence and rapid deployment.
- **Conscripts** – New recruits undergoing the Legion's standardized training regime.

## History

The Gilded Legion has served as [[Tarwa'kem]]'s standing military force since the early centuries of the [[Karo'kem Dynasty]], evolving from a dynastic guard into a professional army over generations of expansion and conflict. For most of its history it was led by career officers drawn from established noble and merchant families, its command structure reflecting the city's social hierarchy more than its military needs.

That changed when [[Neema Karo'kem|Sultana Neema]] named [[Tarik Tazi]] General — a former D'yadyan refugee and pit fighter from [[Meninos' Theater]] with no noble lineage and every measurable qualification. The appointment shocked the aristocracy and energized the city's lower classes. [[Tarik Tazi|Tazi]] responded by immediately reforming the Legion's training standards, purging dead-weight appointments, and deliberately recruiting from the [[Dustway]], [[Misrata]], and the [[Ash Ward]] — populations the Legion had historically overlooked.

The result is a military force that is, by most assessments, more capable than it has been in a generation. It is also one whose loyalty runs to [[Tarik Tazi|Tazi]] and to [[Neema Karo'kem|Neema]] personally, rather than to the council structures that previously exercised indirect influence over Legion appointments.

## Narrative Relevance

The Gilded Legion is the physical expression of [[Neema Karo'kem|Neema]]'s power — the reason her reform agenda is taken seriously by opponents who might otherwise simply wait her out. Without [[Tarik Tazi|Tazi]] and the Legion behind her, her political position would be far more fragile.

[[Tarik Tazi|Tazi]] uses his position to monitor the exploitation of D'yadyan labor by figures like [[Ngor Azizi]] and to quietly protect the [[Misrata]] settlement from the worst of the resentment directed at it. The Legion's presence near [[Misrata]] is officially described as civic order maintenance. Those familiar with [[Tarik Tazi|Tazi]]'s history understand it as something more personal.

The Monetarist Council's discomfort with a General of [[Tarik Tazi|Tazi]]'s background and loyalties is an open secret. Several council members have explored the limits of their influence over Legion appointments and found those limits firmly held.
