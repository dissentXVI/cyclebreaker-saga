## Origin
Krisnarax was once a mighty dragon slain during the final century of the [[Navarresean See]].

The Vociran [[Vociran Military Ranks & Terms|Legatus]] [[Cassius gen Amata]], guided by the Archoran [[Kyrichos]], led a clandestine expedition through the Amrakebir Mountains into the uncovered caves surrounding Sticaea Lake, sacrificing every last one of his own troops in a heretical rite that reawakened the long-dead beast. But before the full awakening could be completed, [[Velethoui Arsenault|Archmage Velethoui]] of [[Shurevaire]] intervened.

The Aqualondian forces that had marched to the region arrived too late to prevent Vociterra's treachery — but just in time to witness the sacrifice of the archmage, whose powerful binding spell halted Krisnarax's return and dispersed its corruptive influence before it could consume the continent.

All that remained of Velethoui was the shattered head of his staff, now studied in secrecy by the scholars of [[Shurevaire]].

## Legacy
The emergence of the summoned dragon created a giant rift in the middle of [[Halinor]], draining Sticaean Lake and revealing [[The Cosmic Pillar|the Watcher of the Lake]] and Navarresean ruins thought lost to time.

The sacrifice of Velethoui is now seen as the defining act that prevented another global cataclysm.
