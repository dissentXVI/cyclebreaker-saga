Breathstone is crystallized pneuma, formed naturally in veins beneath the earth or as residual deposits near places of strong magical flow. While rare in large quantities, smaller shards are common across [[Halinor]], making Breathstone both a mystical treasure and a practical commodity.

Unspent crystals are highly prized for their ability to fuel enchantments, reinforce wards, or empower relics. Entire caches have been seized to summon [[Paragons]], with catastrophic results. More commonly, however, Breathstone sees mundane use: shards are set into lamps that glow with steady light, woven into charms or weapons to hold enchantments, or even fashioned into jewelry once their pneuma has been drained. Its versatility ensures that nearly every city-state has some dependency on the stone, from Aqualondian artisans to Carinthian priests.

At its rarest, Resplendent Breathstone contains pneuma so pure it can alter the balance of great conflicts. Most, however, encounter Breathstone as a simple light in the dark—a reminder that the world itself breathes.
