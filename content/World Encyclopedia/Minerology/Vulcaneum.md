A volatile, azure-hued liquid mined from deep volcanic veins beneath [[Vociterra]], vulcaneum is the lifeblood of the empire. When refined, it produces immense heat and energy, fueling the engines, weapons, and constructs known collectively as [[Vultek]]. Though inert when properly contained, even a small leak can cause violent combustion, making its extraction and transport among the most dangerous trades in the empire.

To the [[Vocirans]], vulcaneum is more than a resource—it is the foundation of their independence from the gods and the gift that raised them above other nations. To outsiders, it is a source of dread: an unnatural power that scars the land and empowers machines without [[Souls|soul]] or [[pneuma]]. Imperial law forbids its export, and smugglers who traffic in it often meet swift and brutal ends.
