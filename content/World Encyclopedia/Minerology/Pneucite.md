Pneucite is a crystalline rarity found only in the most improbable of places. Unlike [[Breathstone]], it cannot be mined or cultivated — no vein has ever been located, no deposit mapped, no method of production discovered. To the eye it is pale and nearly translucent, veined with faint motes of inner light, and unnervingly heavy for its size. How it forms is unknown. Scholars, priests, and artificers have failed to explain its origin, though most agree that its presence marks the site of profound spiritual upheaval, as though the world itself produced it in response to something that should not have been.

Where [[Breathstone]] absorbs and channels [[Pneuma]] — storing it, transmitting it, amplifying it — Pneucite does the opposite, disrupting and negating concentrated pneuma rather than harnessing it. Because of its rarity, even small fragments are housed in reliquaries or entrusted to chosen guardians. Legends claim that the gods themselves cannot touch it without their forms faltering, and a single shard has been known to shift the course of wars. Whether it is a relic of some forgotten craft or a natural defense of [[Aspiria]] herself, no one has yet determined.
