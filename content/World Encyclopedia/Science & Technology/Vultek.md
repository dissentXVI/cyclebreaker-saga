The term _Vultek_ refers to the advanced machinery and devices of [[the Vociran Empire]], all powered by refined [[vulcaneum]]. Ranging from simple tools to massive war engines, Vultek represents the core of [[Vociterra]]’s technological supremacy. Airships, cannons, automata, and even prosthetics are built upon this fusion of engineering and volatile energy, allowing a people without [[pneuma]] to rival the magic of other nations.

To outsiders, Vultek often appears both wondrous and terrifying—its machines roar with unnatural power, and its weapons cut through spells and steel alike. Within [[Vociterra]], however, Vultek is celebrated as proof of human ingenuity: a philosophy of progress without gods, fueled only by discipline and invention. Closely guarded by imperial foundries, Vultek devices rarely leave [[Vociterra]]’s borders, and smuggling or reverse-engineering them is punishable by death.
