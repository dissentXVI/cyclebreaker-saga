Creation Magicks were the supreme art of the [[Predecessors]] — the power to manifest living things, concepts, and entire forms of life directly from [[pneuma]], shaped by will and imagination alone. Where modern spellcraft bends and channels the world's existing energies, Creation Magicks brought entirely new things into being. Under the guidance of [[the Ekklesia]], it was practiced with great discipline, most notably at [[Arkadia]], a floating research facility dedicated to the study and responsible creation of new life.

Its potential, however, was inseparable from its danger. Creation Magicks responds not only to intent but to emotion — and when wielded without perfect clarity of purpose, the grief, despair, or fear of the caster could take shape as something real and terrible. When the [[the Terminus|Terminus]] struck the unsundered world, it caused Creation Magicks to run rampant across [[Neumeris]], spawning creatures born of pure anguish that could not be reasoned with or contained. The catastrophe that followed ended the age of the Predecessors entirely. No mortal born in the sundered world has ever possessed the pneuma — or the wholeness of spirit — required to wield it.
