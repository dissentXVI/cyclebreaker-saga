Pneuma is the vital essence of [[Aspiria]], the invisible current of life and magic that flows through every living being, stone, and stream. It is the source from which spellcraft draws power and the substance that allows mortals to channel divine miracles. Where Atma is the unseen force of will and emotion, Pneuma is the tangible lifeblood of the world—measurable, manipulable, and inexhaustibly present.

In its raw form, Pneuma suffuses the land, circulating like breath through sky, soil, and sea. Skilled mages weave it into wards and spells; druids call it forth from root and rain; clerics invoke it through prayer. When Pneuma crystallizes naturally, it forms [[breathstone]], a resource central to enchantment and daily life across [[Halinor]]. Entire city-states have risen or fallen on their ability to harness Pneuma’s flow.

Though every culture explains it differently—divine gift, natural law, or cosmic inheritance—all agree: without Pneuma, there would be no magic, no life, and no [[Aspiria]] as it is known.
