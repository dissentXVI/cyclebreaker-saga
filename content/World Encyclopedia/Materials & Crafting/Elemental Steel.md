Elemental Steel is a martial discipline developed by [[The Moonblade Forge|the Moonblades]] of [[Aqualonde]], blending smithcraft with pneuma-forged combat. Practitioners wield weapons tempered in [[The Gloam Smithy]] and inscribed with lunar runes, allowing them to channel elemental forces directly through steel. A glaive might unleash a crescent arc of lunar fire, a hammer a shockwave of earth, or a blade a magnetic pulse to draw foes off balance.

Unlike conventional spellcasting, Elemental Steel is not learned through incantation but through forging: each technique is etched into the weapon itself, then awakened by the warrior’s pneuma in battle. The art demands both martial skill and smithing knowledge, for only those who understand how steel is made can unlock its hidden breath. To [[Aqualonde]], Elemental Steel is the union of craft and combat—a reminder that defense of the forest requires not just guardians, but creators.
