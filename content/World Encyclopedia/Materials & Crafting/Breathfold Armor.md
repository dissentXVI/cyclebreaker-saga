Breathfold Armor is the signature defensive pattern of [[Aqualonde]]’s [[The Moonblade Forge|Moonblades]], first devised by Grand Forgewright [[Jannalor Rokas]]. Forged with layers of tempered steel interwoven with Breathstone filaments, each suit harmonizes with its wearer’s [[pneuma]], granting both physical resilience and arcane resistance. Unlike heavier Carinthian plate, Breathfold Armor is designed to flow with movement, its runic seams flexing rather than locking, allowing warriors to fight with speed and precision.

The process of forging a set is long and ritualized—each plate is named, inscribed, and blessed beneath moonlight before being joined to the whole. Breathfold Armor is thus more than equipment: it is a personal covenant, a pact between warrior, forge, and forest. Outside [[Aqualonde]], it is vanishingly rare, worn only by [[The Moonblade Forge|Moonblades]] and their chosen apprentices.
