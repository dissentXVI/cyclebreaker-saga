Native to the far northern reaches of [[Halinor]], Rimewood trees are among the oldest and slowest-growing flora in [[Aspiria]]. Their growth rings are so compact that the timber is denser and sturdier than most stone, resistant to splitting even under extreme cold. Harvesting a single mature trunk can take centuries of waiting, making Rimewood a rare and highly prized material.

Carinthia in particular covets Rimewood for its use in fortifications, reliquaries, and the hafts of sacred weapons—objects meant to endure the long war against dragonkind. Outside the north, Rimewood is traded sparingly, often as heirloom planks or fragments repurposed across generations. To many, a carved piece of Rimewood is more than a tool or ornament; it is a fragment of an ancient life, carrying the weight of centuries within its grain.
