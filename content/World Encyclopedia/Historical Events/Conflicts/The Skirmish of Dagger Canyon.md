The Skirmish of Dagger Canyon was an unauthorized Vociran military incursion into [[Tarwa'kem]] territory in 4008 ApN, launched by [[Vociran Military Ranks & Terms|Legatus]] [[Cassius gen Amata]] in direct defiance of orders from both [[Vociran Military Ranks & Terms|Legatus]] [[Gallus gen Quietus]] and [[Lucius sep Magnus|the Emperor]]. It ended in decisive Vociran defeat and stands as one of the most consequential acts of military insubordination in the Empire's history — not for the battle itself, but for what followed it.

Cassius, driven by personal ambition and contempt for what he viewed as cautious, unworthy leadership, led his forces through [[Dagger Canyon]] into the mountainous approaches of [[Tarwa'kem]]. He expected to seize territory and present the Empire with a conquest it could not refuse. Instead, [[The Gilded Legion]] — Tarwa'kem's elite heavy infantry — was waiting. The canyon's narrow terrain negated Vociran tactical advantages, and Cassius's forces were routed with heavy losses.

Among the dead was [[Megaera cen Magellus]], Cassius's sister and [[Vociran Military Ranks & Terms|Pilus]] of his cohort. Her death undid him. In the weeks surrounding the battle, Sultan [[Shakir Karo'kem]] was also assassinated under circumstances that were never officially resolved, throwing [[Tarwa'kem]] into political crisis and leaving the young [[Neema Karo'kem]] too inexperienced to assume the throne.

The battle left no winners. Cassius returned with a shattered force, a disgraced name, and a grief he could not contain. [[Vociterra]] quietly disavowed the incursion. [[Tarwa'kem]] was left without its ruler at a moment of vulnerability. And Cassius, in the months that followed, began to unravel entirely — a descent that would eventually set in motion the events of [[The Trembling Foundations]].
