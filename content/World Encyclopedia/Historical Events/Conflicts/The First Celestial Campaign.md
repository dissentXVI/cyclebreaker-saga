The First Celestial Campaign was the [[Navarresean See]]'s first and most decisive military campaign against dragonkind, launched under Emperor [[Caelum Aes'Thiir]] and spanning approximately 907 to 1237 ApN. It remains the most consequential act of organized violence in Halinor's recorded history — the event that broke the First Brood, established the Navarresean See as the dominant force on the continent, and set in motion a chain of consequences that have not yet fully resolved.

The campaign was made possible by a discovery during Aes'Thiir's reign that gave the empire capabilities it had never before possessed. Armed with this advantage, Navarresean forces drove north into the territories of [[Wodirethrax]], [[Skadraxes]], and [[Durnirax]] — three of [[Ymirethor]]'s children who had long occupied the northern reaches of [[Halinor]]. When [[Krisnarax]] flew from [[Kokyotos]] to defend his kin, he too was met and slain by then-General [[Isandor Vael'Dorath]], whose blade carried enchantments specifically designed for the purpose.

By the campaign's end, only Krisnarax had been killed outright. [[Skadraxes]] and [[Durnirax]] were driven into hiding, wounded and scattered but alive. Only [[Wodirethrax]] remained openly defiant, retreating to the frozen north and refusing any terms — a decision that would define the next several centuries of northern conflict.

The campaign was celebrated within the empire as the fulfillment of the Holy Mission. Dissenting voices circulated quietly, questioning whether the dragons had ever struck first and what it meant that the empire's greatest triumph required such a cost. The Inquest ensured these voices did not circulate far.
