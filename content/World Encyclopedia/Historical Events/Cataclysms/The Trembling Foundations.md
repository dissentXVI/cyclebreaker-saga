## Origin
The Trembling Foundations began in 4009 ApN when [[Vociran Military Ranks & Terms|Legatus]] [[Cassius gen Amata]], consumed by grief and guided by [[Kyrichos]], led a clandestine expedition through the [[Amrakebir Mountains]] into the caves beneath [[Sticaea Lake]]. There, he sacrificed his entire contingent in a heretical rite designed to reawaken the long-dead dragon [[Krisnarax, the Radiant Fury|Krisnarax]]. The resulting surge of earth pneuma triggered catastrophic seismic activity across [[Halinor]] before [[Archmage Velethoui]] of [[Shurevaire]] could intervene. Though the cataclysm was cut short, the damage was severe enough that historians granted it the title regardless.
## Overview
Earthquakes struck without warning across [[Halinor]], reaching regions with no history of fault activity. Landslides, sinkholes, and newly opened cave systems destabilized settlements and severed roads across the continent. The worst of the destruction was concentrated in central [[Halinor]], where the seismic violence carved a massive rift through the lakebed of [[Sticaea Lake]], draining it entirely within days and leaving behind the vast fractured scar now known as the [[Sticaean Abyss]].

As the lake drained, the [[breathstone]] spire of [[the Cosmic Pillar]] — submerged since the Tempest of Madness two thousand years prior — rose from the exposed lakebed for the first time in living memory. Aqualondian and Tarwa'kin forces converged on the site as [[Archmage Velethoui]] arrived and gave his life to bind [[Krisnarax, the Radiant Fury|Krisnarax]]'s shade into the depths of the newly formed basin, stabilizing the earth pneuma surge and preventing a catastrophe of far greater scale. All that remained of the Archmage was the shattered head of his staff.
## Legacy
The Trembling Foundations reshaped the geography of central [[Halinor]] permanently. [[Sticaea Lake]] no longer exists. The [[Sticaean Abyss]] has effectively split the continent's interior in two, severing trade routes and collapsing the economies of every settlement that once depended on the lake. The sacrifice of [[Velethoui Arsenault|Archmage Velethoui]] is now regarded as the defining act that prevented a second global cataclysm caused by [[Krisnarax, the Radiant Fury|Krisnarax]] — the first time in history a Paragon was contained before worldwide devastation could unfold. The [[the Cosmic Pillar|Cosmic Pillar]]'s reemergence drew scholars, adventurers, and relic hunters to the region, transforming [[Laconia]] from a quiet lakeside town into the most scrutinized site in [[Halinor]].
