## Origin
The Tempest of Madness was unleashed in 2012 ApN by Emperor [[Thallan Lael'Nathor II]], the final ruler of the Navarresean Empire. Consumed by grief and a nihilistic conviction that death was inescapable and life therefore meaningless, he turned the power of [[The Cosmic Pillar]] toward destruction, triggering a catastrophic surge of water pneuma that drowned the world in flood and madness.
## Overview
The rains came without end. Rivers burst their banks, coastlines were swallowed, and crops across [[Halinor]] were lost beneath rising waters. But the floods were only half the horror — the waters carried with them an insidious madness, infecting survivors who might otherwise have simply drowned. Rare records from the period describe such survivors ranting of voices beneath the water, singing of every despair. Scholars note that this cataclysm was made especially dangerous by the presence of a second elemental influence alongside the dominant water pneuma, amplifying both the physical destruction and the psychological toll. Portals were opened to drain off the floodwaters, buying time for the surge of water pneuma to naturally disperse. The Navarresean Empire, already hollowed out by internal decay, did not survive the aftermath. Technology was lost and civilization across [[Halinor]] reverted to a pre-Navarresean state. Dwarven tribes fled to the shelter of the [[Amrakebir Mountains]], while lowborn elves sought refuge in the northern highlands.
## Legacy
The Tempest of Madness marked the end of the Navarresean Empire and erased centuries of arcane and technological progress in a matter of years. The civilizations that emerged in its wake — [[Tarwa'kem]], [[Heldenheim\|Carinthia]], and others — were built from scratch by survivors with little more than memory and determination. The madness-touched accounts from this period remain among the most disturbing documents in Aspirian archival history, and the cataclysm is frequently cited as the clearest example of what unchecked imperial power, left to fester, can ultimately unleash upon the world.
