A Deep or Insidious Wound is a wound beyond the reach of conventional healing — magical or otherwise. When a character suffers one, no spell, salve, or miracle will reverse it. It is a narrative declaration that the injury is fatal, and that the only remaining question is how much time remains.

The term exists to set clear expectations at the table: resources spent attempting to cure a Deep Wound are resources spent in vain.
