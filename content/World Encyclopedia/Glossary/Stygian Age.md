A Stygian Age is a period of world-spanning crisis and suffering opened by a [[World Encyclopedia/Cosmology & Phenomena/Cataclysm]]. Elemental forces run unchecked, civilizations fracture or retreat, and survival displaces progress as the dominant concern of mortal life. The devastation left by each Stygian Age reshapes the political and cultural landscape of [[Aspiria]] in ways that echo long after stability returns.

Seven Stygian Ages have been recorded in history, each distinct in its cause and character — from apocalyptic floods and world-freezing winters to insidious madness and the iron grip of a [[Paragons|Paragon]]'s will. What they share is the weight they leave behind: a world diminished, and a people left to reckon with what survived.
