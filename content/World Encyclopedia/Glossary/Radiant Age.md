A Radiant Age is a period of relative stability, growth, and renewal that follows the end of a [[World Encyclopedia/Cosmology & Phenomena/Cataclysm]]. Trade recovers, cities are rebuilt or founded anew, magic is studied and refined, and the peoples of [[Aspiria]] reclaim what was lost — sometimes surpassing what came before. Radiant Ages vary in length and character, shaped by which civilizations survived and what they chose to build.

Seven Radiant Ages have been recorded in history. Scholars note that no two have looked alike: some were brief flashes of brilliance before the next disaster, while others stretched for centuries of genuine flourishing. All of them ended.
