The [[Vociterra|Vociran Empire]] maintains a rigid hierarchy of military and civic titles, each carrying specific authority, responsibility, and a corresponding name suffix used to identify rank at a glance. These titles govern everything from battlefield command to imperial administration, and are treated with the same reverence other cultures reserve for noble birth.

---

### Imperius — *suffix: "sep"*
The Emperor of [[Vociterra]] and supreme commander of all military forces. The Imperius holds final authority over both the Legatii (the military leadership council) and the Praeses (the civilian leadership council). The title is hereditary within the Magnus line. 

*Current holder: [[Lucius sep Magnus]].*

---

### Principes — *suffix: "seq"*
Heir to the Vociran throne. The Principes sits as the Imperius's second on both the Legatii and the Praeses, and is groomed to assume total command.

*Current holder: [[Caedus seq Magnus]].*

---

### Legatus — *suffix: "gen"*
General and commanding officer of one of the twelve legions of the Vociran military. The Legatus holds full tactical authority over their legion and answers directly to the Imperius. 

*Active example: [[Gallus gen Quietus|Legatus Gallus gen Quietus]], Legatus of the First Legion.*

---

### Tribunus — *suffix: "tes"*
A knight-class officer. Each legion maintains a trio of Tribunii to assist the Legatus or assume full tactical command in their absence. The suffix "tes" denotes a Tribunis of standing rank, unless overridden by a higher rank e.g. "met"

*Active examples: [[Tribunis Ignia tes Metellus]], [[Tribunis Serena tes Paulus]], [[Tribunis Recius met Helvus]].*

---

### Pilus — *suffix: "cen"*
Direct commander of a cohort. The Pilus serves as the operational link between the Tribunii and the soldiers under their charge. 

*Known example: [[Pilus]] [[Megaera cen Magellus]].*

---

### Immunes — *suffix: "met"*
Prime Engineer of the Vociran Empire. The Immunes oversees the highest level of [[Vultek]] development and maintenance within the imperial military apparatus.

---

### Factorum — *suffix: "lut"*
Engineer of the Vociran Empire. The Factorum carries out the practical design, construction, and repair of [[Vultek]] machinery and weaponry in the field.

---

### Decanus / Decana — *suffix: "dec"*
Leader of a small squad. Not a full officer, but a trusted soldier given authority to carry out operations independently. The Decana form is used for female rank-holders.

---

### Custodia — *suffix: "opt"*
A catchall title for auxiliary enforcers and field security agents. Custodia are often given authority over checkpoints, prisoners, or occupied zones during imperial campaigns.

---

### Castellum
A Vociran fortress - a designation for fortified imperial installations used as military staging grounds and garrisons across occupied territory.

---

### Exemplar
The Vociran term for a [[Paragons|Paragon]]. Rather than treating these beings as gods or miracles, [[Vociterra]] classifies them as dangerous constructs born of mortal weakness — weapons, not divinity. The term is deliberately clinical.
