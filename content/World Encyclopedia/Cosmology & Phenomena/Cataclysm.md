A Cataclysm is a world-altering disaster of elemental force that signals the end of a [[Radiant Age]] and the beginning of a [[Stygian Age]]. Each Cataclysm manifests differently — some arrive as unnatural fires, freezing winters, or catastrophic floods, others as storms, madness, or the unchecked rise of a [[Paragons|Paragon]] — but all share a common result: the disruption of [[pneuma]] across [[Aspiria]] and the collapse of civilizations that could not endure the change.

Scholars have catalogued seven known Cataclysms throughout recorded history, each bearing a name drawn from the disaster it wrought or the forces believed responsible. While their origins remain debated — divine punishment, natural imbalance, mortal folly, or some convergence of all three — their consequences are not. Cities fall. Trade routes vanish. Knowledge is lost. And in the silence that follows, the world slowly begins again.
